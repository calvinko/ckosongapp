# Lift up Your Head and Rejoice

All the trees raise their hands.
All the flow'rs lift up their heads.
O' my soul, Behold the Lord. 
He's now in glory!

2. All the trees make melody
And the birds sing along.
O' my soul, Rejoice! Rejoice! 
Rejoice in the Lord!

3. "Vict'ry leaves" say, "It's finished!"
Heartshaped leaves speak of His love.
O' my soul, Enjoy God's love, 
the fruit of the cross!

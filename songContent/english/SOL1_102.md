# The Glorious Vision of New Jerusalem

New Jerusalem! This most glorious vision
Captures my heart, is my dream, drive, and joy!
O sweetest union! The Lord is my Beloved!
This love fully satisfies my heart.
I'm God's dearest, born of Him; He is my dearest Abba;
This highest love satisfies my heart.

2. New Jerusalem is God's tabernacle
And our fairest eternal sweet home!
Its wall is jasper, with precious stones as its base,
The gates are pearls, the street is pure gold;
There the river of life flows, pure and clear as crystal,
The banks blooming with the trees of life.

3. New Jerusalem is God's tabernacle!
'Tis His eternal, sweet resting place,
And our fairest, warmest heavenly home!
God, man in love, fully satisfied!
Enthralled in oneness with God and with one another,
Love and joy forever overflow!

4. New Jerusalem is the bride of the Lamb,
His love's masterpiece shines forth forever.
The union of love between Christ and the church
Is most moving! All will call us blessed!
Sit with the Lord on the throne, glory fore'er increasing,
O' most colorful eternity!

5. My Abba wants me to be His most glorious child,
To inherit all with His beloved Son!
I'm His dearest child, His dream, joy and glory!
His infinite love is my sweet rest.
Father and son's mutual love satisfies His heart and mine,
(The love of Father and son)
Fore'er we'll wonder at His love plan!

# The Father's Love

1. I am born of God.
I am His dear child.
We are closely united.
The highest Father's love
Is the same forever and ever.

2. He holds me in His arms,
He smiles with great content,
Kissing me with kisses.
His delight towards me
Is the same forever and ever.

3. He gazes upon me.
His desire is for me.
From eternity,
His thoughts of love for me
Are the same forever and ever.

4. He gave His Son for me.
He gives me all freely.
He cares for my whole life,
gives me endless glory.
Father, O' Father, I love You.

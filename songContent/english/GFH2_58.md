# The Glorious Hope 

1. My Lord Jesus was resurrected,
He has overcome Satan.
Seated at the right of the Father,
He is the victorious King!
He is the Lord of lords, He is the King of kings,
The ever-living God! *(Hallelujah!)*
He’s enthroned eternally, And reigns forevermore,
He is the glorious King! *(Hallelujah!)*

2. Soon He’ll come back, The church to receive,
To His home in heaven so fair.
In the City, New Jerusalem,
Taste the wedding feast of the Lamb,
What a joy that overflows! No tears and no sorrows,
To enter sweet eternity! *(Hallelujah!)*
Reign in the Millennium, Over the universe,
Fore’er the Lord and we! *(Hallelujah!)*

3. Brothers, sisters, how happy we are,
In heaven with God e’er to live!
Partake His joy, His glory and bliss,
Live with Him the eternal spring!
It’s so bright beyond compare,
God’s kingdom is righteous,
Unshakeable, full of peace. *(Hallelujah!)*
Let us join to sing and praise
The ever-living God
To Him be all glory! *(Hallelujah!)*

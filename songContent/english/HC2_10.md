# The Beautiful Journey of the Son of Man

O' God, preserve me, preserve me,
For I take refuge in You.
O' my soul, you have said to the Lord,
"You are my Lord. I have no good besides You."
The saints in the earth are the fairest one
In whom is all my delight.
Those run after another god,
The sorrows of them shall be multiplied.
O' Lord, You are my chosen portion.
You are my cup. You hold my lot.
You support my lot.
The lines have fallen unto me
In the pleasant places.
My heritage is beautiful.
I will bless the Lord who have given me counsel.
Indeed my heart instructs me in the night.
I have set the Lord always before me.
I have set Him before me.
Because He is at my right hand,
I will not be moved. I will not be shaken.
My heart is glad, my glory rejoices.
My flesh will dwell steadfastly in hope.
My flesh will rest in hope.
For You will not leave my soul
In sheol, in sheol.
Nor will You allow Your Holy One
To see corruption or to undergo decay.
You will show me the path of life.
In Your presence is fullness of joy.
At Your right hand are pleasures forever,
Pleasures forever.

# The Father's Cross

I could hear, I could hear the voice of My Son.
I knew His voice, His plea, His cry.
And I saw He was covered with wounds,
His hands, His feet were pierced.
I could feel, I could feel the stress in His heart -
The injustice, tortures and pains.
I kept silent. I walked away from Him.
I covered My face.
I did not save, did not bind up His wounds,
Nor defend Him. My face was against Him.
I struck Him as if He were my enemy.
I crushed Him, caused Him to suffer
without mercy.
For many sons, and to finish, to finish
The eternal salvation,
I put Him to be cursed, to be cursed.
He was hung on the tree,
And I endured the pains of the cross.
My heart was pierced. My heart was broken.
Would I had died instead of You!
O' My son, My son, do you know My heart?
Do you know My love for you?

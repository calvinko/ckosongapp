# Consider This Grace

1. "He loved me and died for me!"
Consider this grace
That changes all my life,
Gives me hope and joy!
Lord, Your great and tender love
Heals the wounds of men;
Yet, You were oft despised,
Acquainted with grief.

*Chorus:
On the cross You bled and died,
To ransom my soul.
Your heart was wounded for me!
There's a living way
To the Father's fold!
Think'ng of Your love, I'm moved to tears.*

2. You were forsaken for me
Both by God and men;
Lonely, You prayed that night
In great agony!
Silently, You bore the cross,
Walked to Calvary,
Bled all the way, and were lifted on the tree!

*Chorus:
On the cross You bled and died,
To ransom my soul.
Your heart was wounded for me!
There's a living way
To the Father's fold!
Think'ng of Your love, I'm moved to tears.*

# Walk Hand in Hand

Side by side, marching forward,
With love, I respond to His grace.
Heart to heart we are, hand in hand follow Him.
For Him, I'll pour out my whole life.
By faith, we never stop.
Together we strive for the breakthrough.
Toward our goal, we jump over hurdles.
His will let us accomplish.
March on, we won't. turn back.
We will fly over all storms,
Conquer all trials together.
With same mind, we are one.
Hand in hand, we walk without fears.
For the love of God is in my heart.
For my life, I won't turn back.

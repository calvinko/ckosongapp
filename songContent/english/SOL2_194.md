# Flaming Love of the Cross

Behold! The sun gave not its light,
The earth shook, covered with darkness.
God's beloved Son was forsaken.
He tasted death for me.

2. Hung on the cross, my lonely Lord,
Received no sympathy.
Man's mockery pierced through His heart.
For me, He was despised.

3. Covered with blood and wounds, my Lord.
He paid the ransom for me.
He loved me, gave Himself for me.
He bled and died for me.
*He loved me, gave Himself for me.
He bled and died for me.*

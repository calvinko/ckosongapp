# What a Glorious Beloved

I have such a glorious Beloved.
He is the Son of the Most High God.
He's so gentle, humble, friendly,
Perfect, adorable.
He once calmed the raging water.
He cast away Satan's power.
He upholds all things; He raised the dead.
He healed the sick and He filled the hunger.

O' He's God and the Son of Man.
He died but He rose again.
He died for me to bear my sins,
And today He lives in my heart!

# Until Today You're Pursuing Me

Even until today, Lord, You're pursuing me.
The love of my youth You never forget.
Now You still appear to me, kiss me.
Your love to me always the same.
Your love, Your grace is for a lifetime.
Every morning they're new and fresh.
Your love ever fervent;
Your flaming love will never die.
You and I embrace eternity
Filled with endless discoveries,
Beyond all sweet dreams and love stories.
Our journey of love will have no end.
We'll live in the ever
Flowing river of love!

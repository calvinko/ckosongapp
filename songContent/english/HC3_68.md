# The Most High Rules the Kingdom of Men

Behold high in the heavens,
There is God's glorious righteous throne.
The Most High God rules the kingdom of men,
As head over all things for the church.
In the time of the flood,
The Lord sits enthroned as King,
The Lord sits enthroned as King fore'er.
He makes all work together,
To accomplish the church gloriously.
He will come back to give justice and peace.

# The Father's Passionate Love

Abba, since eternity You have thought of me.
Your great love was fully revealed on the cross.
You indeed suffered the deepest pain for me.
On the cross, You forsook Your begotten Son.
You opened a way: I can go to Your bosom.
You love to be on my neck.
You are overjoyed for me.
Abba, I treasure so much that You need me most,
And Your passion to me, Your fatherly love.

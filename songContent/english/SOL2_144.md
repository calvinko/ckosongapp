# When I Consider Your Love

Whenever I consider Your love,
My heart is deeply moved.
Silently You bore all agony,
Malice of sinners.
One slap after another,
One slash after another,
One nail after another,
All these pierced through Your heart.

2. My dear Lord, I think of Your wounded back.
It's like a land being plowed.
Your head was pierced by the crown of thorn,
But You were still steadfast
You carried the bitter cross to the place called Golgotha.
Heavens and earth reject'd You; Abba forsook You.

3. When I behold my Lord and His cross,
How can my heart not be moved?
His head and back were wounded, hands, feet were nailed;
His side was pierced, heartbroken.
Up high was my Saviour, down dripped His precious blood.
It was shed for me, to wash away my sins.

4. Whenever I think of my Lord and His cross,
My heart is melted and moved.
Such love, pain, righteousness, all weave into
Boundless forgiveness.
Love so deep, grace abundant, flowing to my heart,
Turning into a fountain, overflows forever.

5. Thinking of the tender love of the cross,
I fall in His ocean of love.
My stubborn and unyielding heart is melted.
My wounded spirit is healed.
The boundless love of the cross compels me always.
In His ocean of love I'll be enthralled fore'er.

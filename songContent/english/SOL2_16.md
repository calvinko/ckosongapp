# I am Really Rich

I am really really rich!
Almighty God is my Father.
Jesus Christ has thousand names
To satisfy my every need.
Holy Spirit abides in me.
He is my Helper forever.
I have a warm, eternal home
And the Bible dearer than gold.

2. See the sky and clouds so bright,
Flowers and trees, mountains and seas.
Birds and bugs give tunes so nice.
The moon and stars light up the night.
My Lord walks with me all the way,
Giving me joy, faith, hope, and love.
I have glad tidings of glory
For thousands souls in misery.

3. O Lord, make me give, give, give.
Make me a giver all my life.
More I have as more I give
Till overflowing in my heart.
Giving water to little ones,
Eternal reward will bring me.
"Better to give than to receive."
Five loaves, two fish can feed many.

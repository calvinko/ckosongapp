# The Trinity Made the Eternal New Covenant With Me

1. God, You made a cov'nant with me,
O my heart is moved!
Lord, You used Your precious blood to seal
This new cov'nant of love.
All my lawless deeds
Your remember no more.
All my shadows you removed.
I can rest in You.
I can freely draw close to You
And enjoy You.

2. O the glorious, better cov’nant
Stands forever firm!
Gloriously I’m born again of God,
Made compatible to Him,
To be Abba’s dearest child
And the darling of Christ,
To enjoy eternal life,
To know God and Christ.

3. Lord, You are a Man forever,
Your love touches my heart!
You with fervour went up to the cross.
You were smitten by God.
With Your blood You have made
A love cov’nant with me,
That we’ll have a union
Of love and life.

4. O the Son of God with His blood
Made a cov’nant of love,
Now and ever, I belong to Him
As His ultimate love.
With this better covenant
That surpasses all else,
God’s achieved the purpose and dream
Hidden in God’s heart.

5. You’re the guarantor of this new cov’nant for all time!
God’s love cov’nant and His dream of love
Are gloriously fulfilled!
You retain Your scar of love to declare fore’er
That You will cherish our love forevermore.

O the Holy Trinity
Made a cov'nant with me!
I will have the fullest faith
To enjoy Your love!
God has made a cov'nant with me.
O my heart is moved!

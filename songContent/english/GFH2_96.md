# The Lord and I In Love Forever

My darling, My bride, My dove,
You are altogether beautiful.
Your form is lovely and your voice is sweet.
My darling, there’s no blemish in you.
You have ravished My heart with a glance of your eyes,
And your eyes have overcome Me.
You are like a lily growing mid the thorns.
O the fairest one among all,
You are the dearest of My heart.

2. My Beloved and my precious Lord,
Your love moves my heart so deeply.
It’s unique, greater than the sky and the sea.
All things cannot be compared to it.
May You set me as a seal on Your heart
Set me as a seal on Your arm.
My Beloved, may You return and take me up
To the home above You’ve prepared.
Ever to dwell in love with You.
My Beloved, may You come soon!

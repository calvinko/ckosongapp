# O' Youthful One

O' youthful one, do you have dreams?
Am I part of your dreams?
You let your heart cheer you.
Is your pleasure like bubbles?
It bursts out before reaching into your hands.
O' youthful one, will you wake up
You will come to realize
This world defiles your heart, consumes your time and strength,
Hurts you deeply. It's not worthy of you.
O' youthful one, your days will soon fly away.
You'll grow old and weary, and you will slow down.
You'll run no more. By then you'll think of Me.
Would it be a bit late?
I'm calling you today!
Be awakened, and see what I have planned for your life,
How much I treasure you,
How much I miss you!
I once gave you My youthful days.
I sanctified Myself.
I gave up all for you.
O' youthful one, can you be Mine today?

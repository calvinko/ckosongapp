# Living in the Embrace of Abba, the Lord, and the Holy Spirit

Abba, the Lord, the Spirit
Day and night embrace me.
I'm surround'd by Abba
With His deep affection;
Embraced by the Lord
With His romantic love;
And the Spirit bore me and carries me
With tender love.
I'm so blessed, I can take part in Their
Eternal dance, enjoy deepest union.
We sing and dance fore'er.

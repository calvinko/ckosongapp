# Psalm 103

Bless the Lord, O my soul, bless the Lord!
And all that's within me, bless His holy name.
Bless the Lord, O my soul, bless the Lord.
And forget none of His benefits.
He pardons all your transgressions
And heals all your diseases.
He redeems your life from death,
And crowns you with love and mercies.
He satisfies you with good things,
So that your youth is renewed like the eagle's

2. The Lord is merciful and gracious,
Slow to anger, abounding in mercy.
He won't always strive, nor be angry fore'er
He hasn't dealt with us due to our sins.
As the heavens are high above,
As the heavens are high above,
So great is His lovingkindness
To those who, those who fear Him.
As far as the east's from the west,
So far has He removed our transgressions from us.

As a father pities his children,
So the Lord pities those who fear Him.
His loving-kindness on those who fear Him.
Is from everlasting to everlasting.

# How the Father Felt When He Swore the Oath

Appointing Your Son to be the High Priest,
You foresaw the scene Your oath would bring:
Your beloved Son would be whipped, nailed and hung;
He would taste death crueler than hell!
Then You heard His cry that finally came,
His words that pierced through Your broken heart:
"My God, My God, why have You forsaken Me?"
These words torn apart Your heart!
You foresaw this when You took the oath,
Yet You never wavered nor did You hesitate.
Scenes flashed past, one by one, striking Your heart;
Yet Your fervour blazed ever strong!
The zeal of Jehovah will this perform!
Even when passing through depthless agony!
You will never relent nor will You delay;
And the oath You took showed Your determined heart!

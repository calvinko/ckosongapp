# The Loving Course Of Jesus -The Crucifixion

1. “Away with Him!”‚ cried men on earth.
They drove the Lord out of the gate.
Wounded He bore the cross for me,
To Golgotha bled all the way.

2. Though He was rich; He became poor
Even His clothes cast lots they took.
All thro’ His life, weary and sore,
In manger born, died on the cross.

3. His hands were nailed; His feet were pierced,
Hung on the tree, dreadfully cursed.
His blood flowed down, bones could be counted,
Tongue cleaved to jaws, heart melted like wax.

4. “Fountain of Life‚” said, “I’m thirsty.”
From lake of fire, He did save me.
God’s beloved Son, by God forsaken.
Lonely, He tasted death for me.

5. His face was marred, and He was crushed.
Bloody and scarred, within and without.
The precious veil is rent for me.
A living way to God He leads me.

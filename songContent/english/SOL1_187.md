# Living in the Lord's Delight

You leaped with joy, came as a child.
You saw the joy ahead,
Despised all shame, endured the cross.
Now I know You delight in me.

2. Your personal love towards me
Made You give Yourself for me.
Your desire, Your flame of love,
Now I know You delight in me.

3. Your eternal dream of love is
To live fore'er in my heart.
My heart is Your favourite dwelling.
Now I know You delight in me.

4. How can I not respond to You?
I long to love You only,
Day and night draw near to You.
O' Lord Jesus, may You come soon.

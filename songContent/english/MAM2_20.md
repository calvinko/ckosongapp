# The Lord is Faithful and Kind (Ps 145:14-21)

1. The Lord upholds all who are falling
And raises up all who are bowed down.
The eyes of all look to You,
You give them their food in due season.

2. You open Your hand; You satisfy
The desire of every living thing.
The Lord is righteous in all His ways
And kind in all His works.

3. The Lord is near to all who call on Him,
To all who call on Him in truth.
He fulfills the desire of those who fear Him;
He also hears their cry and saves them.

4. The Lord preserves all those who love Him,
But all the wicked He will destroy
My mouth will speak the praise of the Lord,
And let all flesh bless His holy name.
Forever bless His holy name.
Forever and ever.

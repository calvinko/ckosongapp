 # Where Love Abides

 1. Where love abides, where love abides,
 Mountains are turned to plains;
 Where love abides, where love abides,
 Darkness is turned to day.

 2. Where love abides, where love abides,
 Winter feels just like spring;
 Where love abides, where love abides,
 Sweetness whelms over pain.

 3. Love your household, love your household,
 And you will see them change;
 Don't cease to pray, don't cease to pray,
 And you'll see God's glory.

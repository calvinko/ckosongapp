# Song 8:6-7

Set me as a seal upon your heart
And as a seal upon your arm,
For jealousy is fierce as the grave,
And love is strong as death.
Its flashes are the flashes of fire,
The very flame of Jehovah.
Many waters cannot, cannot quench love,
Neither can floods drown it.
If a man were to give all his wealth for love,
It would be utterly despised.

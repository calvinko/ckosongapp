# Wonderful Miracle

O' 'tis God's greatest miracle!
Almighty miracle!
I have become the true child of God;
Born of Him, He's my Abba!
Who can describe my bliss!

2. I can enjoy the Father's love
Toward His beloved Son,
And the Father's deepest affection!
Let me sing and dance with joy,
Have the heart of a child.

3. My heart is free, delight'd and sweet -
I can cry out, "Abba!"
In His bosom I'm delight'd and warm.
Joyfully I work with Him,
To build Abba's household.
(One day I'll see His face.)

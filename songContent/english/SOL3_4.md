# A Never-Relenting Oath

1. Abba, You sent your Son -
Your only begotten Son.
He came to earth, became flesh,
Fore'er a man for me!
Abba, You have made an oath.
You'll never, never relent.
And You have ordained Him
As my High Priest fore'er.
You sent You only Son
To come down from heaven to earth,
To live for me and die for me.
Fore'er He lives for me

2. You have brought me to You,
Forever perfect, complete,
To enter the depths of
Your warmest, fairest heart!
For Your beloved only Son,
A wedding feast You've prepared.
You gave me to You Son
To be His sweet darling love.
I'm His and He is mine.
Forever we're joined as one.
With Him I'll inherit all!
Fore'er He lives for me

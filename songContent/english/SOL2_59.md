# Resting Place of My Heart

May I oft' consider
With me forever You abide.
My heart is forever Your desire.
Day and night You are here by my side.

2. May I oft' gaze at You,
Set You always before my eyes.
My heart's glad and peaceful in Your presence.
My faith has captured Your heart.

3. May I have eyes like dove's
To see You living in heav'n for me,
Consider that You live in my heart.
I set my love and my thoughts on You.

4. May You gain every day
My mind, my love and my will.
You are the glorious vision of my heart.
In Your love, I rest in great delight.

5. When I awake from sleep,
Let me first behold You in heaven.
Consider that You are with me always,
Walking with me toward each new day.

# In The River Of Love

1. O God, Your name how sweet and how fragrant!
In Your bosom, how peaceful and pleasant!
In love with You, at night I still can sing.
In love with You, I fear not long dark night.

2. With Your presence, alone yet not lonely.
With Your presence, I own heavenly bliss.
Walking with You, the dark vale seems so bright.
Nearness to You, my heart’s and Yours delight.

3. You in my life, what warmth and goodness give!
You in my life, make me fervently live.
Eternally, with You there’s endless spring.
Eternally, with You there’s love so sweet.

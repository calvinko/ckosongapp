# Love Getting Deeper and Deeper

I enjoyed Your love from my youth
And now what shall I fear?
Our friendship is long and deep.
You know how to treat Your friend.
When You kiss me
I forget the world.
Enjoy in Your bosom of love,
Embrace in river of love.

2. Day by day our friendship grows deep.
Now is even better.
Your love paved my past journey.
All suff'ring is sweet to me.
Your love is the same.
Now I know it more.
Your love and desire ever fresh.
Union with You is so sweet.

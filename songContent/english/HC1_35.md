# What do You See?

1. What do you see? Why do I have so little
But others have so so much?
Why is that? It is not fair!
How can I have a better life?
When will I have a better life?
Turn your eyes and see God's will.
He richly provides us
With every good thing.
He is fully involved in our life.
He surely give me the best. He will
Give me the best.

2. What do you see? Wicked people have prospered;
Those who do good are not reward'd.
Why is that? It is not fair!
How can I have a better life?
When will I have a better life?
Turn your eyes and see God's plan.
He looks down from heaven
And He knows all things.
At His time He'll repay each person
For what he's done. He'll reward you for the
goodness of your heart.

3. What do you see? Gold and silver, jewelry,
Lavish feasts, fine food and fancy clothes.
Why is that? It is not fair!
How can I have a better life?
When will I have a better life?
Turn your eyes and see God's hand.
He has opened
The gate of heaven.
He has poured down spiritual blessings
Into your heart. The glory of the Lord
Surrounds, surrounds you.
God is with you now.
He's with you forever.

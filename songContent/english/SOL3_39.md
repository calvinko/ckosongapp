# In the Scarred Hands of My Loving Lord

In the scarred hands of my loving Lord,
I'm embraced, protected by Him.
I shall lie in His once wounded side.
In my Lord's bosom I shall rest.
You bear and carry me all my life,
Leading me till I reach Your throne.
You'll love me through eternity.
I have been Your love dream from long ago!
I'm forever Your darling love.
The eternal love co'vnant in Your blood
Stands firm ever to the end!

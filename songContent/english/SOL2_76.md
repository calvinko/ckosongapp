# Enjoy the Lord

Like an apple tree of the woods,
So is my Beloved among the sons.
I sit down in His shade with joy;
His fruit is sweet to my taste.
He brings me to the banquet hall;
His banner over me is love.
Provide me with cakes of raisins
To sustain me, sustain me.
Give me apples to refresh me,
Because I am love sick.
His left hand is under my head;
His right hand embraces me.
O' daughters of Jerusalem,
I charge you by the gazelles,
Or by the does of the field,
Do not stir up nor awaken love.
Wait until it pleases.

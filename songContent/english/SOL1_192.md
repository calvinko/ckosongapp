# Daily You are before Me

Search no more, for daily You're before me,
Closely, closely You look at me, hear me,
With a big smile, full of delight and joy.
You are everywhere,
Yet I have Your special presence.

2. There's not a moment You're not gazing at me.
You surround me, fully commit Yourself.
Unceasingly, we have love interflow.
I'll be anchored in Your infinite, personal love.

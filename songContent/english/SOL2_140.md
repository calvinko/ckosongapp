# He Came and Died for Me, Now Lives in Me

A Child was born for me, for me,
He's the Father's only Son.
He has no beginning, no end;
He is the infinite One.
For love, from heaven, He came
To be a Man forever.
He leaped with joy upon mountains.
For me, He's born in the manger.

2. He even walked to the cruel cross.
He saw the great joy ahead,
Endured the cross, despised the shame.
"It is finished!" He cried out.
He and I are in one spirit.
Fore'er He lives in my heart.
Day and night He lives with me.
O' Lord, Your love melts my heart.
O' Lord, Your love captures my heart.
Your banner o'er me is love.
May I follow You closely,
Forever love You only.

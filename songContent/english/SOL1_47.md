# Enjoy the Fruits of the Cross and Deeply Enter into His Beautiful Heart of Love

In the Lord and the cross, I rejoice and glory!
For He gave Himself for me,
Bought me to Him by His blood.
This strongest love changes my life,
Constrains me, gives me faith.
O' this greatest, most precious deed
Is stronger than the rock,
As stable as the throne!
The fruits of the cross, sweeter than honey,
Give me faith, peace and victory.
O' how pre-cious! Thick clouds 
and mist van-ished,
No more hindrance or shadow!
In His presence I rejoice!
Now I can bathe in His warm glorious light,
It's so sweet and lifegiving! O' what a joy
To enter deep into His fairest heart,
Drink fully of my Beloved's love,
Enjoy His all-knowing friendship.
O' what a joy to live with Him!
Makes me feel His affection
Filling the whole universe,
Filling heaven and the earth.
Who knows in this dark and suffering world,
There is heaven on the earth!
Who knows in this dark and suffering world
Is hidden the deepest love between He and I!
It makes me feel His affection
Filling the whole universe,
Filling heaven and the earth.

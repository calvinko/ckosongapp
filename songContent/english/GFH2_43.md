# One Spirit With The Lord

1. Love, boundless love! To the Lord ever I’m joined!
One in spirit, ever closest to Him.
Entered into the untold riches of Christ.
Exalt Him high; He is my all, my life.

2. The glorious Lord in my heart ever abides.
‘Living union’, I am His. He is mine.
Never alone, live in sweetness He and I.
As close as one, split not we for a while!

3. Deeper union than the branches with the vine.
Deepest union, beyond head-body tie.
One in spirit, no hindrance as we draw nigh.
He ‘nd I as one, what precious bliss of mine!

4. He’s now in me to be my glorious life.
Day ‘nd night supplies my every need inside.
He is my drive, nourishes, transforms and guides.
Through me His light, glory and love to shine.

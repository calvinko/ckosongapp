# In the Vineyard I Give My Love to You

I'm my Beloved's, He desires me.
Let us rise early to the vineyards we go;
Let us see if the vine has grown,
And if the pomegranates have bloomed.
There I will give my love to you,
There I will give my love to you!
Mandrakes give off a fragrant scent,
All the choice fruits are beside our door!
All manner new and old I've laid up for you,
O my beloved, I've saved them up for you.

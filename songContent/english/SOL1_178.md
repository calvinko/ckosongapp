# Infinitely Personal Love

Lord, You're the infinite God, attend fully to me.
Lord, Your beautiful love is the most personal.
O Lord, I love You. You're my treasure.
I cherish Your true love. It's beyond measure.

2. Infinitely personal is Your love to me.
Your wholehearted, committ'd love
Draws me and captures my heart.
O' Lord, I love You. You're my treasure.
In heav'n, on earth there's no one who can compare to You.

(Lord, You desire me! You long for me.
Your ever o'erflowing love how can I deny?)

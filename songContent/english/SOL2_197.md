# Many Mountains ⛰️

Behold, He came leaping over mountains.
He longed to see my face, and to hear my voice.
O' Lord, how many mountains You've climbed?
How much pain You've gone through?
O' how deep is Your desire for me?
From heaven to the earth You came for me.
You became a Man in flesh and blood.
Lord, You took the form of a slave.
For me You bore the bitter cross.

2. O' Lord, to see my face and hear my voice
Whispering, "I love You", to be unit'd with me,
You came leaping over mountains,
Breaking all the distance!
Forever we are one, heart to heart.
With rapt'rous joy You came into my heart,
And lead me into the depth of Your heart.
You bring me to Your glorious throne.
I share Your all, live in sweet love with You
Through all eternity.

# Jesus Christ In My Heart 💖

1. Now that I have been crucified with Christ,
It is no longer I who live,
But it’s Jesus Christ who lives in me,
Jesus Christ who lives in me.
The life which I now live in the flesh,
I live by faith in the Son of God,
Who loved me and gave Himself for me,
Loved me and gave Himself for me.

2. How great is the riches of the glory
of the Mystery of “Christ in me”.
My Lord Jesus Christ is mighty in me.
He’s greater than all in the world.
Christ dwells in my heart through faith,
I am rooted and grounded in love.
I abide in Him and thus bear much fruit.
Living water flows from my heart.

# The Dawn has Come

The dark night is gone and the dawn has come!
The Lord left His tomb, leaving the linen wrapping.
He thought of Peter and the disciples.
He saw their sorrow, their disappointment.
He saw all their tears and their wounds.
He'll take away shadows.
He'll wipe away all tears.
He'll destroy all darkness, despair.
He'll cover them with gladness.
He will give them the glorious hope.
And now they know His love stronger than death,
Always the same, never sep'rate from His love.
The Lord will be with them always
Until the end of the age,
Un- til the end of the age.

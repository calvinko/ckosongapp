# Wing to the Air

Though the Morning Star has not appeared,
The world's signs, Bible prophecies
Make it clear the dawn is near;
Let me fight battles daily!
In my heart is the hope of glory.
Be steadfast till the task's achieved.
Today and tomorrow march on still,
For the toil in God is not in vain.
Be faithful till death the crown to gain.
When the last trumpet we'll hear that day,
Lift our eyes, and see His glorious face.
Endless jubilation!
Our body will be changed!
We'll exult and glory.
We'll be filled with overwhelming joy,
Winging to the air with all the saints.
O' what a glorious moment!
I long to meet the Lord in the clouds.

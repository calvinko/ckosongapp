# Behold, The Veil Is Rent!

1. Behold, the veil is rent!
The Father runs to me;
He embraces and kisses me;
In His bosom so sweet.

*Chorus :
Deeper than the sea,
His love e’er will be!
In His sea of grace I live,
Drink from salvation fount.*

2. The lost son has returned,
The Father lays a feast.
Dancing, rejoicing, He’s merry.
In His delight I live.

*Chorus :
Deeper than the sea,
His love e’er will be!
In His sea of grace I live,
Drink from salvation fount.*

3. The lost son has returned.
There’s no more wandering;
The Father’s love captures my heart.
I mind only His things.

*Chorus :
Deeper than the sea,
His love e’er will be!
In His sea of grace I live,
Drink from salvation fount.*

4. The Cross removed hindrance,
Opened the gate of grace;
The Father’s love from heaven pours,
Fills up my thirsty heart.

*Chorus :
Deeper than the sea,
His love e’er will be!
In His sea of grace I live,
Drink from salvation fount.*

5. The Cross removed hindrance;
His wondrous grace I taste;
Living water from heaven flows,
Becomes a spring in me.

*Chorus :
Deeper than the sea,
His love e’er will be!
In His sea of grace I live,
Drink from salvation fount.*

6. Behold, the veil is rent!
I see the Father’s face;
Talking with Him so glad and free,
How warm and sweet to me.

*Chorus :
Deeper than the sea,
His love e’er will be!
In His sea of grace I live,
Drink from salvation fount.*

7. Behold, the veil is rent!
I see His boundless love;
The Holiest‘s my dwelling place.
I stay before His face.

*Chorus :
Deeper than the sea,
His love e’er will be!
In His sea of grace I live,
Drink from salvation fount.*

8. Behold, the veil is rent!
Christ’s at the Father’s right;
I see His glory, face unveiled,
Am changed to like Him be.

*Chorus :
Deeper than the sea,
His love e’er will be!
In His sea of grace I live,
Drink from salvation fount.*

9. Behold, the veil is rent!
In heaven sit with Him,
I share the Father’s face of love,
Enjoy cloudless mornings.

*Chorus :
Deeper than the sea,
His love e’er will be!
In His sea of grace I live,
Drink from salvation fount.*

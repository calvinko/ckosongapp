# The Costly Grace

1. The grace of my Lord Jesus Christ -
O what a costly grace!
The Son of God once became poor for me.
In manger born, He was humble and toiled,
Died on the cross, for me suffered all loss,
Of flesh and blood, bore the cruel punishment,
God’s beloved Son was by God forsaken.
O what a costly grace, that makes me rich in faith,
My Lord, I love You, adore You,
Such love deserves my heart to gain.

2. The grace of my Lord, Jesus Christ
Immense, glorious and rich!
Surpasses human thoughts God’s vast ocean of love!
Captives of sin now with the Lord shall reign;
Men of distress now are children of God;
Once enemies now become His darling;
One with the Lord, never sep’rate again
O what a costly grace! What a beautiful heart!
My Lord, I love You, embrace You,
Enjoy and trust. My heart‘s amazed!

# Enjoy Walking Each Step with the Lord

Sufficient is a day for its own trouble.
Your yoke indeed is easy and light.
I cast all my loads on You.
You will comfort me.
This is the day that You have made.
By You I shall rejoice.
I enjoy walking with You every step of the way.
There's a beautiful purpose in it.
Let Your heart and mind be harmonized.
You mould me. You have the highest purpose on me.
In every moment Your love thoughts towards me
Are so precious and countless.
Your extraordinary thoughts are
In my every ordinary day.
Every moment is a success.
It's not in vain.
By You, I can rejoice, give thanks for all.
I always live before You.

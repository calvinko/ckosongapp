# The Lord’s Personal Love To Me

1. The Lord’s personal love to me,
Is pure, undying to the end.
Waters, great waters cannot quench it;
Time, even years, can’t make it fade.
Withstanding various trials and tests,
It never fails my heart to gain.
The Lord’s personal love to me,
Encourages me all my life.
When I’m despaired, His love comforts me,
Great and steadfast above all things.
Rich and abundant, tender and soft,
I will boast of forevermore.

2. The Lord’s personal love to me,
Is sweet and warm and full of hope,
Shares in my life all sorrows and joy,
Gives me courage and gives me faith,
Fills my heart like a living spring,
His bosom may I ever stay!
The Lord’s personal love to me,
Makes the angels admire, amaze.
Deeper than the sea, high’r than the sky,
His love paves all my paths of life.
Grace upon grace, ‘tis uncountable,
Beyond compare, no else to find.

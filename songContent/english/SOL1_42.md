# I Love Your Appearing

1. O' Lord, I love Your appearing.
You'll meet me face to face!
In love for years, no words can tell
Such deep love and friendship.
Seeing Your face, I will love You even more,
The clouds'll be brighter than before.
E'er to abide with You and know Your wonder,
'Tis bliss beyond compare!

2. O' Lord, I love Your appearing.
The world I lay aside!
This world, its lusts will pass away,
The toil in God e'er abides.
Not far from me I see the coming glory,
You'll praise me at the stage of Christ!
How You cherish all that I have done for You!
All these You bear in mind.

3. O' Lord, I love Your appearing.
I don't mind suffering!
The glory's of exceeding weight.
You have laid up crowns for me!
You hold my hand through floods
and flames and trials.
Soon You'll bring me into glory!
Then I will sit with You fore'er on Your throne.
With You hold sovereignty.

# “Home” — The Love Dream of Old in God’s Heart

1. Since eternity past,
A sweet dream outflowed from God's heart.
A household He desires
To make known His great glory!
He broke through the "Impossible",
Passed through shame, all sufferings, the cross.
He poured out His boundless all
To accomplish His dream of old!

Chorus:
Home sweet home! Home sweet home!
'Tis the ultimate mystery of God!
God's sweet home! God's sweet home!
Endless, fairest sweet love dream!

2. O the Holy Trinity
Embraces all His precious ones!
O this is God's household -
The myst'ry deep in God's heart.
Each one pours out his all;
O we live out perfect love!
'Tis the hidden fair love dream
In the heart of the great I AM!

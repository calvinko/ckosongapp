# Father of the Fatherless

1. A father of the fatherless,
a defender of widows,
Is God in His holy dwelling,
looking after the helpless.
God makes the lonely ones
to live in a home of love.
God makes the lonely ones
to live in His warm family.
From eternity He desires me to be
His bosom darling child!

2. Now He's my dearest Abba,
my eternal Father!
O I'm His joy, His darling child,
fellow heir with Christ forever.
As He loves His beloved Son,
He loves me with the highest love!
I can face the world with Abba,
rejoice daily in His love.
I can dance and shout for victory,
sweetly rest in His warm bosom!

3. Now You're my dearest Abba,
my eternal Father!
O I'm Your joy, Your darling child,
fellow heir with Christ forever.
As You love Your beloved Son,
You love me with the highest love!
I can face the world with You Abba,
rejoice daily in Your love.
I can dance and shout for victory,
sweetly rest in Your warm bosom!

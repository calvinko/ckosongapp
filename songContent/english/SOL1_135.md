# What the Lord Treasures

The Lord longs to see my face.
One thing I will seek.
I need to choose the good part -
Face to face with my dear Lord.
There're indeed trials on earth
But in Him I have peace.
With the Lord, my heart can rest,
For He has overcome all.

*Chorus
O' Lord, I love You. Draw me close to You.
Make me the closest to You, respond to Your love.*

2. There're indeed trials on earth;
My Lord'll carry me through.
He is moved with compassion.
His love can heal me.
All things happen for my good -
Cause me to know Him more.
His love, wisdom, power,
His heart and arms are mine.

*Chorus
O' Lord, I love You. Draw me close to You.
Make me the closest to You, respond to Your love.*

3. He loved me and died for me.
His true love I cherish.
For the Lord I'll break my flask -
Offer my perfume for Him.
(Respond to Him in this life.)
He treasures what Mary did.
He missed Bethany.
He desires my heart for Him,
For He yearns for my love most.

*Chorus
O' Lord, I love You. Draw me close to You.
Make me the closest to You, respond to Your love.*

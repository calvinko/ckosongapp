# Walk with the Same Heart and Same Footsteps

You and I have one precious message.
You and I have one everlasting way.
You and I have one glorious goal.
Let's share with all brethren.
(Let's share with all churches.)
O' come Let us walk the everlasting way.
Let us follow the trail of the flock -
With the same heart and same footsteps.
Press on toward the goal!
(Finish the Lord's commission!)
(Wait for our dear Lord with joy!)

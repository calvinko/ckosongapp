# Abba Gives All to Me

God, You gladly pours down all to me,
Every blessing, every treasure.
Nothing held back, You are so passionate to me.
From heaven You freely pour down all to me.
What a beautiful loving heart,
Unlimited blessings outflow.
I am happy to receive,
Yet You are even happier for me.
My joy is Your greatest joy.
So I open my arms
And gladly receive.

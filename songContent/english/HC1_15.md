# I Will Follow the Lord Every Day

1. Lord, You are the real and living God.
I rely on You. I follow You
On the righteous path.
I obey all You commanded.
Now I am in Christ, united with the Lord,
Live with Him in the light,
Out of the power of darkness.

2. Lord, You loved me and died for me.
I rely on You. I'm dead to sin.
Old things have passed away.
Today I live to You.
Now I am in Christ, united with the Lord,
Live with Him in the light,
Out of the power of darkness.
Pursue Him every day,
Close to Him every day,
Follow Him every day,
Till I go to the heavenly home.

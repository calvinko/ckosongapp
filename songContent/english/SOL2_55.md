# Two Places Far Apart

You and I far apart, 
but our hearts are closest.
We can not meet face to face.
We may not say too many words.
Yet I know Your heart 
and You know me well.
We're heart to heart.
Though You're in heaven I'm on earth.
You poured out Your all for my blessing.
I offer myself to strive for You.
You watch me from above, 
I'll fix my eyes on You.
For a while, we're apart, 
but we are one forever.
One day we'll meet face to face.
I'll be overwhelmed with joy 
beyond compare!

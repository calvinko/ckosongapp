# How to Behold Him-Your Most Real True Beloved

My Lord is now in glory
and He is everywhere.
He is surrounding me
and living in my heart.
He is more real than all things,
deeply unit'd with me.
I have His special presence,
the most blessed union.

2. My Lord is now in glory
and He is everywhere.
He is surrounding me
and living in my heart.
His personal love to me
is ever infinite.
His wholehearted, commit'd love
is warm and draws my heart.

3. We are ever united
deepest, closest union.
This love union's beautiful,
romantic, colourful.
My heart's warm, overflowing
as we're on this love path.
The love of my true Beloved
fills my heart, heav'n and earth.
(It's my rest. I'm content.)

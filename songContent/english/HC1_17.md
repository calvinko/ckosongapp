# Lord, I was Created for You

1. Before I knew You, I felt
Empty deep inside.
I had many friends around.
I was seeking a better one.
It was endless search for true love.
Can it be ever found?

2. Before I knew You, I didn't know
The secret of the universe,
The meaning of relationships.
Why is there romantic love?
It was endless search for meaning.
Can it be ever found?

3. O' Now I know I was created for You.
Only when I return to Your heart,
Only when I fall in love with You,
Then I can be satisfied.
Every day I come to You.
You draw near to me.
On earth You and I live our every day.
In heaven You and I
Will live better days.

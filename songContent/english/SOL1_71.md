# The Lord's Secret: His Desire is toward Me

From eternity past
You thought of me;
Your precious thoughts of love
Outnumber the sands.
You have designed my life
Up to eternity.
O' Your desire of love is toward me!

2. You knew Your life's journey
paved with much suffering,
From manger to the cross,
full of trials and pain;
But You longed to come down
and love to be a Man,
To ever be like me and close to me.

3. When You faced the cruel cross,
You thought of me;
After You have risen,
You thought of me;
Now in glorious heaven,
all day You think of me;
You really long for me!
Your heart is mine!

4. One day You come for me!
My joy overflows!
The wedding feast of love
dissolves my heart.
In the presence of all
You show Your love for me,
O' Your desire of love is toward me!

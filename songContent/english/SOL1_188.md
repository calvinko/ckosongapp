# Your Desire and Gazing Day and Night

Day and night, think of me,
desire me, dear Lord,
You always attend to me.
You long to see my face,
hear my voice, dear Lord,
Sweetly and closely live with me.
Since eternity You have loved me, dear Lord,
Unchanging, o'erflowing, ever fresh.
Living in the depth of
Your heart and embrace,
I feel so sweet. My heart o'erflows.

2. Fully You involve Yourself in my life, Lord,
Now and through all eternity.
Forever live with You, in love with You, Lord,
Nothing can compare my joy.
Now I live in Your love and desire, dear Lord,
My heart's satisfied, overflowing.
With all my heart I'll respond to You, dear Lord.
Forever we embrace in love.

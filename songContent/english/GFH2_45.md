# The Son Of Man United With Me

1. You came as Son of Man, triumphed for me,
Conquering ev’rything, all to inherit.
You share the promise of the Spirit with me.
One spirit I’m with You, my life You have been.

2. You became Son of Man, to give me warmth.
As flashes came Your love, from high You brought forth.
How gloriously You lived, God’s heart to men unsealed,
Taught me to live by You, reveal Your glory.

3. Son of God, Son of Man, fore’er You’ll be!
You’re my elder Brother; You are my Beloved.
One in spirit with You, a living union,
I am drawn after You by Your love so deep.

4. Now I’m never alone, ev’r knit to You.
When I think of myself, rightly think of You.
To fight battles with You, earth’s journey walk with You,
Face everything with You, my heart rests in You.

5. At times it’s so lonely, cheerless it seems,
Yet You dwell in my heart, give a melody.
As in heaven I feel, enjoy Your love and warmth.
My heart’s can leap and shine; flames of love grow strong.

6. Though now I live on earth, my heart‘s on high.
Always I think of You living in glory.
Each other we possess, closer than all on earth.
Falling in love with You - my greatest treasure.

# My All-knowing Friend

I treasure You are my best friend.
You have searched me and known me.
You know my thoughts from afar off.
All my desires are before You.

2. O' my dear Lord, You know all things.
You are mindful of my thoughts.
Let me open my heart to You.
You know my thoughts before they're told.

3. You have known me inside out, Lord,
You perfectly understand me.
Teach me how to live with You 
To be set free and without fears.

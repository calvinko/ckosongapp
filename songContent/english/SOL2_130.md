# The Wonderful Eternal Dance of Love

The eternal dance of Abba and the Lord
And the Holy Spirit wonderful and sweet!
You are boundless; You self-exist,
Overflowing with life and love.
I partake in this circle of love.
What a blessing to live in this love!
Let me worship Abba and the Lord.
My joy is full; my heart rests, overflows.
My heart rests, overflows.

# I Completely Live to You

1. O' My Lord, I live to You.
In this busy and troubled world,
I don't want anything to distract me.
I fully live to You -
My heart's desire, my affection,
All my longing, my thoughts, my mind,
My eyes, my lips and my ears.
I turn my face to Your countenance.
All of me - my inside and out,
My whole person, my two hands,
My whole body -
I give to You all my love,
All my attention. All that I offer,
I dedicate them to You.
All for You.

2. O' My Lord, let your presence
Surround me, fill me, affect me.
Lord, You surround me. We are the closest.
We're deeply connected
We're united; You're in me. We are one!
All pass away, all fade out,
All diminish, become dim,
And fade away.
At this moment, it's only You and me.
I draw near to You; You draw near to me.
It's the sweet fellowship
Between You and me.
This is the life I love to live,
The blessings and joy of heaven,
The resting place of my heart.

# Risen! Ascended! Coming!

1. He’s risen! He’s risen!
Jesus has conquered death!
My Lord is risen,
He has conquered Satan.
My Lord Jesus has saved me;
My Saviour has set me free.

*Chorus:
So let us sing! Hallelujah!
Hallelujah! Hallelujah!*

2. He’s ascended on high!
He’s ascended to heav’n!
He’s given glory,
That is above all things.
He’s living up there for me;
He’s holding authority.

*Chorus:
So let us sing! Hallelujah!
Hallelujah! Hallelujah!*

3. He’s ascended on high!
He’s ascended to heav’n!
He’s thinking of me;
He is praying for me.
Preparing a home for me,
How He longs to receive me!

*Chorus:
So let us sing! Hallelujah!
Hallelujah! Hallelujah!*

4. He’s coming! He’s coming!
He promised to come soon.
To meet me in the clouds,
Ever to be with Him.
He has kept rewards for me,
And prepared a wedding feast!

*Chorus:
So let us sing! Hallelujah!
Hallelujah! Hallelujah!*

5. Dear Lord of my heart!
May You come soon, come soon!
Then I’ll stay by Your side,
O how happy and sweet!
In the City stroll with You,
By the River walk with You.

*Chorus:
So let us sing! Hallelujah!
Hallelujah! Hallelujah!*

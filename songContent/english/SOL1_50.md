# Enthralled in His Matchless Love

Enthralled in His matchless love,
Like living in a love dream!
His love fairer than flowers,
Tastes sweeter than honey,
No dream can compare,
Beyond the thoughts of man,
wonder of wonders!
But His love is true! His love is true!
O' His love fills the whole universe,
Heav'n and the earth,
And my heart which He desires.

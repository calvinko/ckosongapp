# The New Heaven, the New Earth and the Holy City

1. And I saw a new heaven, a new earth:
For the first heaven 
and the first earth passed away;
And the sea is no more. 
And I saw the Holy City,
New Jerusalem, New Jerusalem,
Coming down out of heaven from God, 
made ready as a bride
Adorned, adorned for her husband.

2. And I heard a great voice 
out of the throne saying,
Behold, the tabernacle of God is with men.
And He shall dwell with them, 
and they shall be His peoples,
And God Himself shall be with them,
And God Himself shall be with them, 
God Himself shall be with them,
And be their God, and be their God:

3. And He shall wipe away 
every tear from their eyes,
And death shall be no more;
And death shall be no more; 
neither, neither shall there be
Mourning, nor crying, 
nor pain anymore:
The first things passed away. 
And He that sits on the throne said,
Behold, I make all things new.

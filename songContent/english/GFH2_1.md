# The Love of the Father, The Son And The Holy Spirit

1. You are the living God
Forevermore,
Of wonder and glory,
“I am that I am.”
Mid the Father, the Son,
And the Holy Spirit,
There’s interflow of love
before time began.

2. The Father and the Son and the Spirit,
Sweet and perfect in one, love boundless and deep.
Now I can partake such closest tie and love,
Drink from the highest love, my heart rests in peace.

3. O God, the First, the Last, great Your glory!
Yet You have thought of me since eternity.
How precious are Your thoughts, they outnumber the sand
Or the stars of heaven, all with love for me.

4. Through all eternity, You’re loving me,
Long that I’m joined to You, e’er in love abide.
Lord, You are my Beloved; Your heart , Your arm are mine,
Sharing Your everything, with me You desire.

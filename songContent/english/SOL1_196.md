# That is the Day

That is the day,
the wedding feast of the Lamb.
That is the feast set by Abba,
the core of His plan.
That is the day of the gladness of His heart -
The Lord waits every day.
We are not invited to the feast, but indeed
We are the glorious bride of the Lamb,
Compatible to the Lord.
He died for us He used His blood
And His true love to betroth us.
How joyous He is
that He has gained my heart.

And on that day
my dear Lord'll come to hold my hand.
Before all things,
He'll declare His heart is forever mine.
We are always the jewel of His heart.
His love is in our hearts.
No one can ever set us apart.
Fore'er we are heart to heart.
He has poured forth His highest love.
He won't turn back; to Him I belong.
We are in love fore'er.

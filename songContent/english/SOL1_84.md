# Manifold Evidence

The heavens and earth tell me Your love.
Your letter of love shows me Your love.
Your journey on earth proves me Your love.
Your precious blood is the proof
that shakes the world.
Now and ever You live in me,
Commit Yourself to affirm Your love to me.
My past journey full of Your grace.
All show Your love so tender, steadfast to me.
O' my Beloved, forever I'm Yours.
Truly, Your heart, Your arms,
Your love, Your strength, Your life
Forever belong to me.

# Strive Till He Comes

*Chorus:
My dearest Lord, may You come soon!
May You return quickly from the blue.
Bright Morning Star, may You come soon!
Take me to the clouds to meet with You.*

Lord, I have been waiting for so long,
Even dark hair has become grey.
The night of this world is far spent,
How much longer should all groan and wait?

# His Unforgettable Appearing

Rejoice! Rejoice! The Lord's risen!
Overcame Satan and death, He is risen!
Rejoice! Rejoice! The Lord's risen!
How can I forget His love appearing!

2. The Lord's appearing 
Wiped off my tears.
His Father is my Father,
I'm dear to Him.
The Lord's appearing 
Removed my grief.
It's too good to be true.
I clung to Him.

3. The Lord's appearing 
Cast off my fear.
The Lord's love will never change.
My heart's comfort'd.
The Lord's appearing 
Opened my eyes,
Took away all my sorrow.
My heart's fervent!

4. He appeared to me 
And gave me peace.
He sent me to the world
To work with Him.
He appeared to me, 
Showed me His wounds.
"My Lord, and my God,
I love You so!"

5. He appeared to me 
By the seashore.
He prepared breakfast for me
With tender love.
He said to me,
"Do you love Me?
If you do, shepherd My sheep
And follow Me."

 6. He commissioned me 
On the mountain
To finish the glorious church,
Wait till He comes.
I'll never forget
His face and voice.
With same mind I'll work with Him,
Strive till He comes.

7. He appeared to me, 
Opened my eyes:
My Brother - the Son of God,
Who came for me.
He's the Son of Man,
The same with me.
Yet He is without sin.
He's glorious.

8. On Mount of Olives 
He said to me,
"You'll receive the Spirit.
Be My witness."
When He's blessing me,
He's taken up,
Ascended to the Father,
Living for me.

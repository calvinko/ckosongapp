# My Lord in Heaven

Christ my Lord ascend'd to heaven,
He led captive a host of captives.
Christ my Lord ascend'd to heaven,
He won the vict'ry and gave gifts to men.
God has exalted my Lord,
Crowned Him with glory and honour.
He's waiting His foes to be His footstool.
Hallelujah!

2. Christ my Lord ascend'd to heaven,
Sitting at the right of the Highest.
Christ my Lord ascended to heaven,
As head over all things to the church.
My Lord has been glorified,
And the Spirit lives in me forever.
In one spirit I am with the Lord.
Hallelujah!

3. Christ my Lord ascend'd to heaven,
He's my Advocate with the Father.
Christ my Lord ascend'd to heaven,
He is my High Priestalways blesses me.
He will save me to the end;
He lives to make intercession for me.
He presents me perfect before God.
Hallelujah!

4. Christ my Lord ascend'd to heaven,
He's in heaven but He's thinking of me.
Christ my Lord ascend'd to heaven,
He is my High Priest full of sympathy.
I am set free before God,
Now I come boldly to the throne of grace.
All my prayers Christ offers to God.
Hallelujah!

5. Christ my Lord ascend'd to heaven,
It tells me all my sins are washed away.
Christ my Lord ascend'd to heaven,
It tells me Abba fully accepts me.
I am perfect before God.
I am His delight as the Lord is His.
Abba loves me as He loves the Lord.
Hallelujah!

6. Christ my Lord ascend'd to heaven,
For me He prepares a heavenly home.
Christ my Lord ascend'd to heaven,
But He longs to take me into glory
To be His darling fore'er.
I will inherit all with the Lord.
I'll know His glory in eternity.
Hallelujah!

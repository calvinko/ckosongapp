# Let Not Your Heart Be Troubled

Let not your heart be troubled,
Neither let it be afraid.
Do not fear, just believe;
Those who trust won't be in haste.
O do not fret, the Lord is with you.
Do not be troubled by stress or anything.
By Him we step back to see all things through His eyes.
Look to Him, and smile, rest assured,
And know His guidance through and through.

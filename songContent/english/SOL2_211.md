# May You See the Good of the Church All Your Life

For all your life may you see 
the good of the church.
Be root'd in God's household,
Flourish in the church.
May they prosper who love You,
O' those who love the church.
O' may the Lord bless you 
from His household.

2. For all your life may you see 
the good of the church.
O' God's abode of rest is also my rest.
If I forget you, O' church, 
let me utter no word.
God's most glorious purpose is my vision.

3. The church is God's purpose, 
the hope of the world.
God's most glorious purpose 
is my meaning of life.
The Lord has sent His church 
to accomplish His will.
May you desire the church
for all your life,
Build His church, 
live out God's family.

# His Zealous Heart to Me

Lord, You are God, dwelling in the light which no man
Can come near. Yet You long for me.
For Your love dream, You came from heaven to the earth,
Became a Man, to draw near to me.
The Great I AM, with no beginning and no end;
And yet for me, You are a Man forever.
Because of me, You treasure to be God and Man.
I am amazed, Lord, You're no longer God only.

2. Fore'er a Man, You deeply comfort my heart.
Fore'er a Man, You explain Your great love.
I am amazed, O' Lord I affect You so much.
For Your desire, You're a Man forever.
Fore'er a Man, You're glad to give Yourself for me.
Fore'er a Man, You've proved Your love to me.
Fore'er a Man, You've declared Your full commitment.
You gladly kept the wounds of love on Your body
To confirm Your love and desire for me.
O' Lord, Your love moves me and captures my heart.

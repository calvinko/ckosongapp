# The Song Of Solomon

1. My darling, my dove, I long for you;
Let Me hear your voice, see your face,
For your voice is sweet, and your face is lovely.
Let’s meet in the clefts of the rock.
My darling, my dove, I’ll share with you your joy and tears.
Walk with you heavenward, E’er be your company,
Until we meet in eternity.

2. My Saviour, my Beloved, I long to draw near!
For Your love to me is high and deep!
Down from heaven You came, paid the ransom for me.
Such great love forever constrains me!
My Saviour, my Beloved, I am wholly drawn after You.
None in heaven I love, none on earth I desire,
None else I will seek besides You.

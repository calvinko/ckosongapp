# The Dawn

At dawn He’s risen! Bright morn light shines,
Drives off the world’s dark night.
The glorious Lord parts from the grave,
Wipes all sorrows away.
The world has hope hence forth! *(Hurray!)*
And I can boast to the end! *(Hurray!)*
The risen Lord has conquered death,
1. A glorious hope He gives. *(Wonderful!)* 
2. Takes all my fears a - way. *(Wonderful!)*
What a boastful hope He gives! *(Hallelujah!)*

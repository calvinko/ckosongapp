# The Bright Morning Star 🌟

I behold the bright Morning Star.
I'll leave this world, heaven to go.
Dark is the world, the night's far spent.
Bright Morning Star, I long for You.

2. I can see the finish line.
I'll run the course, steadfast I'll go.
Short is the time. God's will be done.
Forget the past, march on forward.

3. I see the church will be finished.
With diligence, I'll serve my Lord.
Things of this world will pass away.
My labour is never in vain.

4. I can see the Lord's coming soon.
In Him, I will trust and obey.
Say no to sin, endure all trials.
You will dry my tears when You come.

5. I can see the crowns and rewards.
I'll gladly bear shame for the Lord.
Take up my cross, follow the Lord.
I wait for His praises with joy.

6. I can see the coming glory.
I'll despise all trials and pain.
Light affliction's working for us
The eternal weight of glory.

7. I can see my heavenly home.
As a pilgrim, I live on earth.
This world has no lasting city.
A fairer home's prepared for me.

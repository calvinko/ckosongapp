# Let Us Carry and Bring Them Home

My heart grieves, O' Abba Father,
Your heart must be broken.
How can I still live in
The world of my own?
Though it may cost me dearly,
I still jump in the pit.
I'll bring them out of the pit of despair.
Maybe I'll be in danger;
Maybe I'll only save one;
Maybe it'll cost my all.
Others may sneer at me,
But You know how I feel.
How can I stand aside?
How can I give up?
Let us arise, we don't have many days.
Together we will carry and bring them home.

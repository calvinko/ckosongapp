# United To The Lord

1. All that I encounter throughout my life
Are united to You and Your will;
You and I belong to each other,
Facing every thing together.
For Your will though I suffer hardship,
Persecutions, slanders and sneers,
Yet my heart can rejoice in all these,
For Your grace abounds through my journey.
Your presence, the warmth in God’s family
Moves me deeply and gives me joy and rest;
Your fervent love compels my heart,
Floods and flames to pass I’m steadfast!

2. We’ve fought many fierce battles, yet I find
Your wondrous salvation every time;
By You, I lift my head and boast,
By faith press on toward the goal!
Lord, You do appreciate my love for You,
On this suff’ring yet glorious journey;
Then one day, You’ll come to receive me
Into Your endless joy and glory!
Your presence, the warmth in God’s family
Moves me deeply and gives me joy and rest;
Your fervent love compels my heart,
Put on the armour, I march on!

May all that I decide throughout my life,
Be united to You and Your will;
You and I deeply love each other,
Walking this life journey together.

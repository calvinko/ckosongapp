# His Delight and Desire

The song of songs reveals Your heart,
So I can enjoy Your heart.
You lead me to inner chambers.
Now I know You delight in me.

2. I have captured Your heart and sight.
I'm Your lily among thorns.
Your banner o'er me is love.
Now I know You delight in me.

3. Forever, Lord, You are a Man.
You came leaping on mountains
To see my face and hear my voice.
Now I know You delight in me.

4. Abba prepares a wedding feast,
Gives me to You as "beloved".
I'm Your greatest treasure and joy.
Now I know You delight in me.

5. You praise me, "You are all fair,
No blemish, capture My heart."
I satisfy You the most.
Now I know You delight in me.

6. My eyes have overcome You
I've captured Your heart and sight.
I'm Your only perfect one.
Now I know You delight in me.

7. From feet to head You praise me
I am Your delight and joy,
Capture Your heart. You desire me.
Now I know You delight in me.

8. May we embrace forever.
Your heart is reserved for me.
I was creat'd to satisfy
Now I know You delight in me.

9. I'm set as a seal on Your heart,
As a seal upon Your arm.
The flames of love is strong as death.
Now I know You delight in me.

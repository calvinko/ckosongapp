# The Thrill in the Love of the Cross

O' Lord, keep me near the cross,
May I perceive Your heart.
Let Your love pierce through my soul,
I shall never be free.
Lord, Your love of the cross
Thrills my heart immensely!
Could I still live for myself?
Should I ignore Your love?

2. God's own Son walked to the cross,
Such love is amazing!
I am captured by His cross,
I surrender myself.
Lord, Your love of the cross
Thrills my heart immensely!
This love conquers, compels me
To be same heart with You.

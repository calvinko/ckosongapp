# I am the Lord's Disciple

1. Lord, You command'd us to go to
The corners of the world
To preach the good news to all nations.
You desire all to be saved.

Chorus:
Lord, I love You.
I follow You.
Lord, I love You.
I'll bring many to follow You.

2. Lord, You command'd us to baptize many
In the name of the Father
And of the Son and of the Spirit.
You desire all to be Yours.

Chorus:
Lord, I love You.
I follow You.
Lord, I love You.
I'll bring many to follow You.

3. Lord, You command'd us to make disciples
To teach them to keep all the commandments
You desire all to be close to You,
To walk on the righteous path.

Chorus:
Lord, I love You.
I follow You.
Lord, I love You.
I'll bring many to follow You.

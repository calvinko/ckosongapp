# The Eternal Love, the Deepest Affection and the Happy Family

You loved me most from eternity past.
You predestined me to be Your child.
You longed to be my dearest Abba.
You love me as Your only begotten.
I am Your dearest treasure.
You gave me to Your Son.
I'm Your greatest delight.
For me, You dance and sing.
All celebrate, and sing aloud,
"Your love dream has come true.
Your lost son has come home
To enjoy Your deep affection."
My Abba, I treasure this affection.
We're closely united in one spirit.
You hold my hand, walk to glory.
May I live in freedom and joy
As Your dearest child.
Daily enjoy family life with You.
My heart's ever close to You.
You and I, heart to heart, walk towards
The glorious eternity.

# The Flaming Love

1. Christ’s fervent love does melt my heart,
Make me amazed, adore!
The thought of it thrills all my soul,
Compels me to the end.

2. Such flaming love is my drive -
I live my live for you!
Rightly I am beside myself
And strive to save the world.

*A self-denying life-
For the flaming love of Christ!*

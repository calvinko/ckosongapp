# For This Love Relationship

For this love, this affection,
and for this relationship,
I want to do more, to be more committed.
You have done all that You could.
You're always committed,
Selfless and give Your all
You want to gain Your love.
In faithfulness, in steadfast love,
You have betrothed Your darling.
You are full of longing, expectation to be
In love with Your loved one,
And make many moments between You and me.
Now it's my turn.

To see the blossoms, the mountains, the sea,
The night sky, the moon and stars.
Hand in hand, side by side,
We are walking in love.
I enjoy with You whisp'ring words of love
And the time of quietness.

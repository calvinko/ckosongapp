# The Most Disparate Yet the Closest

1. Infinite distance - I dare not reach You.
Lord, it's You who came to pursue me.
Before time, I was Your sweet love dream!
Your eternal love is fair!
You desire to be a Man forever,
To make You and I the closest.
You lived Your life, facing the cross ahead.
You were born for me and You died for me.
You drank the bitter cup, kept Your scare of love.
This forever declares Your steadfast love!
I am the bone of Your bones,
Most compatible with You!
Only I can fill the space
Kept for me in your heart!
You cried out, "It is finished!"
Winter's passed, the fair endless spring has come!
You've overcome 'The impossible',
Triumphed gloriously with love!
You've accomplished Your fair, sweet love dream!

2. All creation can't help but celebrate
Your glorious resurrection,
Your love journey on earth, Your victorious love,
And Your love dream fulfilled!
Endless sweet love dream! Beautiful future!
Lovely flowers that sweetly blossom
Pave our endless romantic journey of love.
Dancing butterflies and singing birds rejoice!
We're forever deeply united.
O the LORD's love dream will flourish
Forevermore! Oh!
Our sweetest future! The Lamb's wedding feast!
Everlasting, glorious New Jerusalem
Will abound with hope and splendour!
My beloved Lord, the great I AM,
Said, "Behold, I make all things new!
O the heavens and earth I make new!"

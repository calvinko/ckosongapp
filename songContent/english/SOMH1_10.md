# The Lord's Love Dream  

I am Your longing of love.
Since eternity
You have desired me.You laid down Your life,
Shed Your precious blood to make this dream come true.
You live in me and I live in You.
Deepest union, we are heart to heart.
Living union overflows with life and love.
We're one spirit, closest, dearest!
I'm Your deepest longing, content, and joy.
You're my greatest treasure, true joy, perfect rest!

2. You are mine, boundless Lord.
You are mine forever.
I am Your love dream, eternal lover,
I am Your darling, compatible with You.
Your love for me is everlasting.
Path of love is paved with pink petals.
River of love ever springing with bliss and love.
I'm blessed! In this union I'm blessed!
Enthralled by Your abundance forever,
We'll live in this sweet eternal love dream!

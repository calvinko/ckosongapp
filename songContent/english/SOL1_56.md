# Who Affect the Father the Most
I am amazed! I affect You so much!
You thought of me from eternity past.
For me, You had sent Your beloved Son from heav'n,
And He gave Himself for me on the cross;
Father, Your beautiful heart
Was pierced through with arrows!
O' glorious! The Lord cried out 'It is finished!'
The veil was rent; You dashed to me;
Embraced me to Your heart;
Kissed me with kisses;
Rested on my neck in ecstasy!
You prepared a rich feast; 
You sang and danced;
Shouted for joy over me, 
Your heart was filled;
Embracing me, ravished in love!
I am amazed! Infinite You are;
You need me out of love.
Yearning for me to be close to You
Fore'er hand in hand, live in love!

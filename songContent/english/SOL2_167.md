# Same with Me in All Things

Lord, for Your love for me,
Your eternal, exquisite love,
You longed to be a Man,
Same with me in all things.
You want to take away
All the distance and fear.
You chose to come for me
To taste the life of a man.
Lord, You comfort'd my heart,
Healed me and filled me with gladness.
Lord, You want to be with me,
To live closest with me.

 2. Lord, for Your love for me,
Your eternal, exquisite love,
You longed to be a Man,
Forever live in me.
You leaped o'er the mountains.
You saw the great joy ahead.
You despised all the shame,
Endured the pain on the cross.
Now we are joined as one.
You are my Beloved forever.
I am Your dearest darling.
Forever we live in love.
Forever and ever
We relish this deepest love.
You love me; I love You,
Heart to heart forever,
Enjoy eternal love.

# The Risen Lord

1. O what a joy! I have the risen Lord.
O what a joy! I can live in love with Him.
He has passed through the darkest night.
He's overcome the world, Satan and death!
He holds my hand and walks with me through life.
He leads me through storms and fires.
And I shout, "Vict'ry!"

2. The risen Lord, my life and spring of joy!
This joy no one can snatch away from me.
He fills my heart with faith and hope.
I joy in Him while onward go.
He holds my hand and walks with me through life.
He leads me toward the peak.
And I shout, "Vict'ry!"

3. I've tasted death, but now am living.
I've conquered death and forever I will live.
I hold the keys of Sheol and Death.
My hand holds all authority.
Fear not, My love, I'll always be with you,
Yes, to the end of the age!
O let's shout, "Vict'ry!"
O let us rejoice!

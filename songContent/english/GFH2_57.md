# I Know For Sure

I know for sure where I will go one day -
A place where carols ring, where no sorrows remain,
A city of glitt’ring gold, walls of lovable jasper.
I know for sure where I will go one day -
A home of scenic land, where love fills every place,
With my Lord ever to dwell, His countless riches to taste.
This is the home that You prepare for me -
A hea’enly home above I’ll see.
Now and fore’er, my heart is glad and sure.
Joined to Your heart, we shall never part!

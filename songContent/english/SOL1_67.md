# My Lover in the Glory

In the glory my Lover,
My best Friend holds all authority!
He's the beloved Son of God,
My glorious Savior.
For me, He came down to the earth
As a man forever.
He loved me so much that He gave Himself

On the cruel cross for me,
But He has risen from the dead
And ascended to heaven!
Wow! One day, from the glory, He will come back
To take me to His most wonderful home.
Now in heav'n, He lives for me;
From morning to night He's thinking of me;
I belong to Him; His desire is toward me!
His fairest heart and strong arms all are mine!
O' I love Him! He's my treasure and port-ion
Incomparable!

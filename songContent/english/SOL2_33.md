# Smile for the Lord

1. Smile all the day, for You are by my side.
Smile all the day, for Your love surrounds me.
I'm of good cheer even in various trials,
For You are holding all authority.

2. While I'm tempted, let me smile to You still,
For You've overcome the world and the foes!
Your grace's enough. The Spirit gives me life.
You uphold me walking on life's billows!

3. You carry me through the days of trouble.
Even in battles, I can smile by You.
Pressed by the foes, I still see Your face.
Before the foes, You lay a feast for me.

4. With You I pass through floods and flames in life;
Our friendship deepens as the days go by.
Suff'ring with You makes sweetest memories.
It works for me the eternal glory.

5. My dear brethren, we can only live once.
Let's bear the cross for Him in this brief life.
Break my vial, offer Him perfume today.
Anoint Him with love, give our heart to Him.

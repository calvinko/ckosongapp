# Run to Thee for Fire, Revival Fire

Won, won, You've won the vict'ry
Too, too, it's too good to be free!
Free from bondage I go forth.
For Your heart and will I strive.
Fire, fire, deep inside,
Give me five then I will fly.
Fly, fly, flying on high,
Run to Thee for fire.
(Revival fire! Give me five!)

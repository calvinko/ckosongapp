# The Flame of Love of Jehovah

Jehovah is my true Beloved 
Boundless, self-existing.
His wholehearted and fervent love
Overflows to me fore'er.
Indeed I'm His desire.
I'm His dearest darling.

2. Jehovah is my true Beloved 
He is above all things.
His matchless love so beautiful.
We're most deeply united.
His love is my sweet rest.
His love captures my heart.

 3. Jehovah is my true Beloved 
His love is wonderful.
It's the love from the beginning 
Explained by all kinds of love.
Indeed I'm His desire.
I'm embraced by His love.

# Your Scar of Love, Forever Riven Deep for Me

1. Lord, Your scar of love fore'er riven deep,
Tells me tenderly You adore me deeply.
Stuns my heart, reveals Your heart:
You want me as Your bone of bones.
Lord, Your scar of love fore'er riven deep,
Tells me tenderly how deep You desire me.
Deep in Your heart, ever engraved in You:
I'm Your darling, compatible with You.
Dearest to Your heart, 
I quench Your thirst of love.
I am Your one and only bone of bones.
Besides me, no one can 
fill Your heart's empty space.
Your ever riven scar of love
Forever declares to me:
Your flaming love, Your love of the cross
Will always surround me. You tell me Your pain.
Truly I know You love me most.
Lord, Your scar of love fore'er riven deep,
Lets me, anchor, rest in Your bosom.
Enthralled by Your love, I nest in Your heart,
Your darling, closest to You.
All Your proofs of love, stun my heart,
I'm Your ultimate sweet dream of love.
O my Lord, I'm Your beloved darling.

2. Lord, Your scar of love ever riven deep,
Ever declares You love me deeply.
I'm Your sweetheart, longing of love,
I am Your ultimate desire.
You committ'd Yourself for Your love dream.
You have bridged the gap,
made Your love dream come true.
You will share with me Your eternity!
Lord, I am Yours; You are forever mine!
I'm the endless love gift the Father gave to You.
In one spirit forever You and I!
Our story of love, which has no end,
Comprises countless enchanting chapters.
Everflowing river of love,
Everlasting path of love
Better than all dreams,
we live in endless spring.
We'll have ceaseless discoveries,
Live in eternal contentment.
Together we'll sit on the throne.
We'll be enthralled by the river of love.
This is the fairest love dream of all.
I'm Your compatible lover.
Flies over me Your banner of love.
Forever I'm Your only love dream!
O Lord, I am Your eternal love!
Forever, You are my true Beloved.

# I Long for You

I long for You! I long for You!
My Love, my King, my life, my song.
I long for You! My Morning Star,
To walk with me, to stay with me.

2. I long for You! I long for You!
My Love, my King, my life, my song.
I long for You! My Morning Star,
To take me home, to take me home.

3. I long for You! I long for You!
My Love, my King, my life, my song.
I long for You! My Morning Star,
Come, to take me into glory!

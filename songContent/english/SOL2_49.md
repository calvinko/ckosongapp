# Lean on the Father's Bosom

Father, I'll lean on Your bosom,
In Your love, I'm fully healed.
Rest in Your bosom of love,
Enjoy Your sweet kisses.

2. Father, I'll lean on Your bosom.
I'll feel Your beautiful heart,
Where Your endless love flows
As a spring quenching my thirst.

3. Father, I'll lean on Your bosom.
Let me hear Your words of love.
Your love and Your bosom
Forever capture my heart.

# Love like a Rainbow

It's so precious! You are my Beloved!
You have fully committed Yourself;
You confirm it with Your precious blood,
It really deeply touches my heart.
Your love is stronger than death,
And forever the same.
Now You and I are most deeply united.
The love's covenant is forever sure.
Your love is like a rainbow!
Colorful, beautiful,
So tender, so bountiful,
So unique and personal; from morn to dusk,
You're gazing on me, thinking of me,
Longing for me, embracing me!
Help me to have the bravest faith,
And a dancing spirit,
To echo Your matchless touching love,
And set my love on You!

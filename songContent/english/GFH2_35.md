# Christ Arose From The Dead

1. Christ arose from the dead!
The stone was rolled away;
O what a joyous, glorious news,
The angels did proclaim,
He is not here, He is not here,
For He is risen, For He is risen, as He said

2. Christ arose from the dead!
And death was swallowed up!
Satan who had the pow’r of death
Forever was destroyed.
Christ holds the key of death,
Set free those who fear death;
O Death, where is your sting?
O Hades, where ‘s your victory?

3. Christ arose from the dead!
And death was swallowed up!
He brought life and immortality
To light so gloriously.
He fills my life with hope,
With meaning and bounty.
It is so sweet - the risen Lord
Is now living with me!

# Grace Before Time Began

Before time began was salvation grace
Given to me in Christ Jesus.
From e’erlasting, He’s thought of me.
Such love draws me, com-forts my soul.

2. What length, breadth, height and depth, 
Lord, is Your love!
Gives me warmth, melts my heart each passing hour.
My soul adores, flows endless praise.
Your bands of love draws me to You.

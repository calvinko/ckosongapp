# Press on Towards the Goal

Whatever things were gain to me,
For Christ I have counted as loss,
For the surpassing value of knowing Christ.
To gain Christ I count all as dross.
Not that I have laid hold of it.
There's only one thing in my heart 
Forget what's behind, 
reach forward to what's ahead,
I press on toward the goal.

*Chorus:
Yet, I am continually with You;
You hold me by my right hand.
With Your counsel You'll guide me.
Then receive me to glory.
Besides You there's nothing I desire.*

Now, I press on toward the goal,
For the prize of the upward call,
For the prize of the upward call of God in Christ,
I mind not things of this world.

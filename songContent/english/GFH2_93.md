# The Lord Who Cares

1. My past journey You’ve cared continually.
My course ahead, Lord, You will surely lead.
In storms or waves, You are the First, the Last.
Oh my Beloved, You’ll uphold all my paths.

2. My God Your name is “I am who I am”,
The First, the Last, with all love and fullness.
You can supply my needs and my desires.
Now and ever, in You I’m satisfied

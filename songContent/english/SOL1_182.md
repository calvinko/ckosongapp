# To Live in Me

To live in me is Your deepest desire.
Facing the cross, You sweetly thought of this.
The Spirit came, You gladly live in me.
Sweetest union You're with me forever.

2. To live in me is Your eternal dream.
You creat'd me to be closest to You.
Living union! My life's bound up in Yours.
Union of love we're ever heart to heart.
(Deepest union - our hearts are satisfied.)

3. Now is better - You're forever in me.
Glory upon glory, You are my life.
Deepest union surpasses all things.
One day You'll come. I'll see you face to face.

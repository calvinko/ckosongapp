# Dearest Abba, How Great You are!

Dearest Abba, How great You are!
You once sent Your beloved Son,
And on the cross You chastised Him.
O You love me dearly!
Your gentleness and Your great love
Make me re'lize how great You are!
You love me as You love the Lord.
How beautiful is Your heart!

 2. Abba, who can measure Your love?
You once sent Your beloved Son,
And on the cross You chastised Him,
For You need me the most.
Your love's boundless, Your heart's all fair,
Make me re'lize how great You are!
You embrace me into Your heart.
And in Your love, I feel so warm.
(In Your bosom my heart feels sweet.)

3. Dearest Abba, how great You are!
You love me as You love the Lord!
I'm born of You though created;
I am joint heir with Christ.
You are my true Abba!
Your affection and Your great love
Make me re'lize how great You are.
Let me deeply enter Your love.
Love You fore'er, love You the most.

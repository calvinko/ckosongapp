# The Long Expected Embrace

When I lifted my head for Your face I wished to see,
I met Your gaze on me which was from eternity.
I could not realize when You began
To put me on Your heart!
When I considered that was just our first encounter,
Instead it was Your long expected embrace with me.
I was surprised with joy when I met You the first time,
Yet, all along, You've been deeply loving me.
As time goes by, I love You more and more.
Since long ago You've desired me, thought of me.
O' This sweet love that surpasses time and space
Is a true experience of You and me.

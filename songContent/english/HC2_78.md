# May Everyone be Useful

If I were the mouth,
I would preach and sing out loud.
I'd let everyone
Know that the Lord is good.

2. If I were the hand,
I would serve and I would work hard.
O' let me be
Helpful to everyone.

3. If I were the foot,
I would run to places in need.
O' let me follow
The footsteps of the Lord.

4. Each one of us is precious
Each one of us is important.
Let us come together, be united as one.
We stand as one man - a giant.
Let us arise and shine forth
To light up the world
And let many see the way of the Lord.

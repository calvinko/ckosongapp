# Be Strong and Courageous

Christ, the Victor, holds on high
All authority;
He gives you the pow'r to
Trample enemies.
Be awesome as an army
With its banners bold;
Sing your praise, and use the pow'r to
Conquer all the foes.
He prepares a feast for you
Before your enemies;
Raise your head with glory,
With your heart set free.

2. Facing various trials you should
Be more rejoicing;
'Tis the way to grow fast,
To receive glory!
Be strong and courageous,
Face the fight with joy;
Cast not away your confidence
To gain great reward
He is with you through the valley
Of the shadow of death,
Protects you with His ord,
Guides you with His staff.

# I am Content

All things that happen to me in this life
Are from Your beautiful will.
Either things present, or things that are to come
Cannot change Your love to me.

2. To be abased or to abound,
All things I face in my life 
All work together to refine me
So I can finish Your will.

3. I have learned the secret of being filled.
In the Lord I have strength.
Give thanks in all things, with Him I am content.
He is my fountain of joy.

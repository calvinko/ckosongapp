# The Deepest Affection! The Highest Father's Love

In this whole universe,
There's no deeper affection,
No higher father's love,
Your bosom is my sweet rest.
Father, daily You long to see my face,
Love to hear my sweet voice call-ing to You;
Daily You long to kiss me with kisses,
Embrace me.

Abba, Your matchless plan,
Full of wisdom and love!
As Your child, born of You,
I'm carefree and drawn by You.
You desire to bring me to great glory,
Sit with You on the throne, to inherit all;
You ev'n prepare a wedding feast for Your Son,
O' how sweet!
Abba, my heart loves You;
I long to know You more,
Fully drink of Your love,
Daily re-joice before You.

# Only Jesus

Do you know where I can find true smiles?
Where does true love hide?
Come and go, we live and die,
Wand'ring for a thousand miles.
Who is willing to share with a true heart
My laughter and tears?
Who can lead me to the right path,
That I may not spoil my years?

*Chorus:
Only Jesus! Only Jesus!
He is the light of man.
He lights up my life journey,
Gives me warmth and glorious hope.
He walks with me, He dries my tears,
Gives me abundant life;
Brilliant forever!
Brilliant forever!*

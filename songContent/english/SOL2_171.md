# The Yearning of Love

You are a Man forever.
You live with me from morn to night.
I'm not Your obligation.
But I'm Your love dream indeed,
Your yearning from eternity,
Your joy ahead, Your joy fore'er.
Lord, I'm thrilled by Your love for me.
Let me respond to You always.
Lord, Your contentment let me be.
Fore ver we are heart to heart.

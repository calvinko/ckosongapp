# You have Prepared

Before I realized it, You had prepared all,
Such as the heavens and the earth
And the fullness of them all,
Such as Your love journey and salvation.
Never knew I could ask for Your help,
Lord, You have come.
My daily bread; clothes for all seasons.
In the days I faced endless toils 
and ceaseless waves,
By the Sea of Tiberias,
Labouring all night, being hungry and weary,
When I was pondering under the fig tree
You have come.
When I fail and lose hope, surely You and Your help
Will come faster to me.
O' Lord, You're my Brother. You will take care of me,
You will defeat my enemies,
You will ease my troubles, lead me in this life journey.
O' Lord, I count on You.

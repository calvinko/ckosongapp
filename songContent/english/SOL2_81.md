# Take up the Task and Sojourn for the Lord

I'm a stranger and pilgrim on earth.
I long for a better home above.
Gladly would I sojourn all my life.
Fight the good fight as He sends me.
I would rather live just like the Lord
Who had no place to lay His head.
By faith reach to the end of the earth,
Be one whom the world's not worthy.

2. Let's take the commission of the Lord,
Build the church according to His will.
Spread the truth to all nations and lands.
The church we'll revive and rebuild.
By faith, climb mountains and cross the Red Sea.
By faith, I boast and shout for joy.
By faith, I satisfy His heart.
By faith, I praise His name!

3. For the Lord, I count all things as loss.
O' Lord, keep my heart to the end.
Ever be faithful and pure to Him,
Love Him more than I first began.
By faith I feed on His faithfulness,
Fortress and giants are my food.
By faith march on with joy always.
By faith I live for Him.

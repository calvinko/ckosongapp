# In Everything I Give Thanks

In everything I give thanks.
I think of God's goodness.
I remember His help
And the salvation grace.
From morning till the night,
Since the day I believe,
From young to old,
I give thanks to Him fore'er.

It's good when the day is easy and light
It's good when the day is busy and tight
I trust God will make use of all things.
I give thanks to Him forever.
Let me give thanks again and again.
Let me trust again - strength upon strength
Victory upon victory.
This life journey shines brighter
And brighter forever.

# Walk in Love with the Lord

From eternity past to eternity
You desire me.
This beautiful love plan came from 
Your dream of love.
You want to live with me in love and joy.

2. Lord, You became a Man.
You died and shed Your blood
To betroth me.
Deeply we're united, one spirit, 
heart to heart.
Forever we walk on this path of love.

3. To have You I'm so blessed.
Share with You joy and tears 
All are precious.
Work in same heart with You, 
I'll accomplish Your will.
I wait for You to take me to the clouds.

# The Union of Love

In this whole universe,
There is no higher love,
There's no deeper union,
Now I'm joined to You as one!
I am amazed at Your desire and love,
From eternity, forever outflows.
It is Your heart's deepest hidden secret,
Thrilled am I!
Lord, Your sweet dream of love
Has gloriously fulfilled,
I'm joined with You in love;
Forev'r You dwell in my heart!
Daily let me live close to You in love,
Fore'er live in the bright beautiful spring!
It's most precious I am Your dearest lover,
O' how sweet!
Your desire is for me,
And I love You dearly;
I long to know You more;
Daily live in Your rich love!

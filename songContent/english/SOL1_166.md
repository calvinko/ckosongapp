# His Attention of Love

Day and night my Abba surrounds me.
He lays His hand on me.
From young to old He carries me,
Holds me on His lap.
His thoughts of love to me
Are so precious, and so tender,
More than the stars and the sand.
O' He is my dear Abba!
(I'm so blessed to have You/Him!)

2. My Abba is acquainted with
My path and my lying down.
When I smile, clap hands and shout for joy,
Abba admires me.
Day 'n night He thinks of me,
Attends to me, knows me deeply.
O' He shouts for joy for me.
I am His delight and joy.
(It's so precious I have You/Him!)

3. When I lift my head and raise my hand,
Abba looks at me sweetly.
When I think of Him and sing to Him,
Abba's heart is ravished.
Precious fatherly love!
It is Your joy to embrace me.
You are my dearest Abba.
We walk hand in hand fore'er.

# My Warm Dwelling Place

In the universe, my heart finds no resting place-
Only You are my eternal rest.
In the universe, to whom can I give my love?
Only You're my secure resting place.
You are my warm dwelling place.
How blessed! We are united!
Fore'er we're in the river of love.

2. Apart from You whom do I have in heaven?
Only You worth my heart and worship.
Apart from You I have no desire on earth.
Only Your love captures my heart.
You are my warm dwelling place,
My eternal resting place,
You are my glorious Beloved.

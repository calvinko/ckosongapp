# Song 1:1-4 Draw Me Away

The song of songs which is Solomon's.
Kiss me with kisses of your mouth
Better than wine is your love;
Your ointments are pleasant.
Your name is oil poured forth, oil poured forth.
Therefore the young women love you.
Draw me after you! Let us run together.
The kings brought me into his chambers.
We will exult and rejoice in you.
We'll remember your love,
Praise your love more than wine,
And rightly do they love you, they love you.

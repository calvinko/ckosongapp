# Manifold Love

Manifold love abides in You,
How beautiful! Your heart!
Your affection so deep, so sure,
Sealed with Your precious blood!
Love's union, Love's commitment
Will endure forever.
One day with You, sit on the throne!
Fore'er I'll sing and praise.

2. Now I live in river of love;
The bliss I can't express!
I long to go to all kingdoms,
To praise Your matchless love.
My spirit, soul and body
Respond with love to You.
To wait for You, steadfast I'll be,
With faith march on with You.

# Love Never Fails

Love! Love has no end, it never fails
'Tis my spirit's experience.
Sometimes, I even find myself ordinary.
Yet, beyond the sky, in this universe,
There is a gaze of tender love on me.
Each time when I lift up my head,
The moment we meet,
I know that I am not ordinary,
Because I have ravished His heart.
(For He is a man forever.)

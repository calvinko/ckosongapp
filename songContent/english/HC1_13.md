# In the Name of the Lord

1. Let's pray in the name of the Lord,
Ask according to His will.
Let us pray for the glory of God.
Let us bless many.

2. Let's speak in the name of the Lord,
Speak words of blessing and give thanks.
Let us speak for the glory of God.
Let us bless many.

3. Let's work in the name of the Lord,
Speak words of blessing and give thanks.
Let us speak for the glory of God.
Let us bless many.

# Though You Have Not Seen Him

Though you have not seen Him,
You love Him, you love Him.
Though you do not see Him now,
You believe, believe in Him,
You greatly rejoice with joy,
You greatly rejoice with joy,
With joy in expressible,
And full of glory.

2. Though I have not seen You,
I love You, I love You.
Though I do not see You now,
I believe, believe in You.
I rejoice with joy untold,
I rejoice, full of glory.
I can't tell how sweet it'll be
When I see You one day.

3. Though I have not seen You,
I love You, I love You.
Though I do not see You now,
I believe, believe in You.
Lord, You are above all things,
Far beyond what I can see.
O' my heart longs for You,
Think of You day and night.
My Beloved, whom my soul loves,
You deserve all my heart.

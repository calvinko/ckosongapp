# Such Love As Yours

1. Such love as Yours, makes me know the true worth in me;
Such love as Yours, makes me see the beauty of all things.
Life is full of meaning, worth well to live.
Then I understand why there’s eternity,
And I know why, Your desire is to abide in me.

2. Such love as Yours, gives me rest in days dim or bright;
Such love as Yours, makes me live in tender morning light,
Gives me hope to run my course with delight.
Thorns and swords I conquer overwhelmingly.
Lord, in Your love, there is never fear but sweetness only.

3. Such love as Yours, teaches me what beauty really means;
Such love as Yours, teaches me to praise and to give thanks.
Turns me into a poet, a morning bird;
Melts me into a river, vast and flood’d with love,
Ever flowing, toward the earth’s end, till eternity.

4. Such love as Yours, abounds in deeds, warmth and thoughts so deep;
Such love as Yours, keeps my spirit alive, ever-fresh,
Cheering me to climb up the highest peak,
Blowing me to sail towards the boundless sea,
Ever love You, follow You to the corners of the world.

5. Such love as Yours, makes me long for the heavenly home;
Such love as Yours, makes me long to see Your glorious face;
O Lord Jesus, may You come, may You come!
In the Holy City of light and love we live,
Sharing with You, endless songs and mornings eternally.

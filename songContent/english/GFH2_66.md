# The Will Of The Father And The Son

1. Lord, Your body is given for me.
The veil is rent, was torn in two.
Now there is no more hindrance of sin.
With my face unveiled I see Your glory.
Hallelujah! How You long that I am Yours!
You desire to draw near to me,
And I treasure to draw near to You.
“Close to Him!” How precious ‘tis to me!
My good part and my eternal bliss.
When I rise up early ev’ry day.
I long to draw near to Him.

2. Father, how broad and loving is Your heart!
You gave Your Son to ransom me,
That I may fore’er know and follow You.
And you bring me into Your household, too.
Hallelujah! The Father’s house has appeared!
‘Tis my family, and your family.
Let’s together love and care for it.
Father, help us to love even more,
To accomplish Your beautiful will,
Ever to be rooted in this house.
Heart to heart we journey home.

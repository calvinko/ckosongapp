# The Most Stunning Love Union

O' how different - I'm a man; You're God.
Out of Your beautiful, steadfast love
You came from heaven to pursue me.
I have long been Your love dream!
You desired to be a Man fore'er
To be closest, to unite with me.
You were steadfast in Your whole life journey.
You came for me; You died for me!
You took the cup, reserved Your scar of love.
This forever declares Your steadfast love!
I'm ever Your bone of bones, Your compatible one,
Besides me, who could fill Your longing heart?
You cried aloud "It is finished!"
Dark nights have past; eternal spring has arrived.
Achieved the impossible, Your love overcame all.
Your sweet dream of love has come true!

Your creation all comes to celebrate
You are risen forever for me,
On earth, You once lived and gave Your life.
Your sweet love dream came true!
Sweet eternity, endless discov'ries,
Flowers blooming with sweet fragrance,
You hold my hand on this endless path of love.
Birds are singing; butterflies are dancing!
We're forever deeply united.
Your love dream will go beyond all forever!
O' Our brightest future
Hope of glory!
The Lamb's glorious wedding feast in heaven,
Eternal New Jerusalem!
The infinite, most high Lord declares:
Behold! I make all things new!
Heaven and earth are made anew!

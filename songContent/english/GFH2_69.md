# The Love Of Creation

1. This is my Father’s world.
See the wonder of His works!
All show His love and tender care,
Give grace and joy to man.
He made birds of fine tunes,
“Alarm-cocks” that crow at dawn;
He made insects play the symphony,
He clothes the night with peace.

2. He made the sun that shines,
The moon that glows at night;
He made clouds to shower and cleanse,
And as sun-shade so nice.
He made mountains and seas,
For my delight in the scenes,
Lovely and thoughtful are His designs,
Showing His love tender and real.

3. He made the galaxies,
To widen my eyesight,
My Father’s greatness I may perceive,
That draws my heart to worship.
Your love is high and deep,
Thoughts of love as sand at sea.
I praise Your love; I admire Your grace;
Echoes of love I give.

# God's Kingdom Comes

1. When God's kingdom comes, 
our Lord Jesus will reign.
He brings healing and peace, 
He restores all things.
The blind see, the deaf hear, 
the lame dance, the mute sing!
Deserts blossom as the rose, 
thirsty lands gush with springs.
We will reign with the Lord, 
Earth and nature sing;
We'll toast and drink to our King of kings!
On Earth He reigns! With Him we'll reign!

2. When God's kingdom comes, 
the Prince of Peace reigns!
Swords hammered into ploughshares 
when peace comes on Earth.
Wolves are lambs' guests and friends, 
calves and lions feed on grass,
While a little child will feed 
and lead them with fun.
Groaning, sadness flee, 
Earth and nature sing!
Peace on Earth, joy fills the world
When Jesus reigns! O comes our King!

3. When God's kingdom comes, 
God's Spirit's poured out.
All the Earth sees 
His glory and learns His ways.
The knowledge of our Lord 
will fill the Earth,
As waters fill the ocean and sea.
All the Earth remembers Him, 
serves and worships Him.
He'll spread a rich feast 
for all people on His mount.
We'll drink in joy to our King of kings!
On Earth He reigns! With Him we'll reign!

# Moment After Moment

Lord, now I am before You. My heart turns to You.
The next moment: I still want You;
I want to talk to You.
Another moment: I don’t
Want to leave Your presence.
The moment after: I still stay in
The light of Your countenance.
And then sometime after: I stay before You,
But I need to do my work.
I cannot focus on You,
But I never forget
You are here; I am not alone.
When I have a break at any moment,
I will quickly fly to You
Even though it’s for a little while.
The moment after,
And after that moment:
I’m in Your presence to face all
And all circumstances.
Whether everything is just as usual,
Or it is busy and tight,
My heart won’t leave You.
I just don’t have much time
To say many words to You.
A moment has passed;
Lord, I am before You.
A moment has passed;
I am still here before You.
You are in my heart.
It’s so sweet we are heart to heart.
Every moment I’m close to You.
I’ll be with You forever.

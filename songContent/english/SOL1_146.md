# O' Lord, Thank You You've Brought Me to Vancouver

O' Lord, thank You 
You've brought me to Vancouver.
You know deeply what are my needs.
Under the blue sky, You appear to me,
That heals, encourages me.
Your love lights up my heart,
Makes me smile in darkness and storms.
We have love interflow in the white snow,
Leaving Your foot-prints and mine.

2. O' Lord, thank You 
You've brought me to Vancouver.
You know deeply what are my needs.
You long for me to enter Your love, desire,
And the secret of Your heart.
You want me to lay down all,
To have sweet rest in Your love.
You renew my strength, 
I'll mount up with wings,
Go from glory to glory.

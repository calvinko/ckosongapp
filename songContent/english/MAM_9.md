# It's So Good to be with the Lord

It's so good to believe in Jesus
We have the Lord by our side.
He will never leave me. He is so close.
La la la la, together we live with Him.

2. It's so good to believe in Jesus.
We are not afraid of death.
The Lord will receive our soul to heaven.
La la la la, it's so good to be with Him.

3. It's so good to believe in Jesus.
The Lord will come to take the church.
Our body will be transformed! How glorious!
La la la la, O' Lord, come to take me up!

# Your Choice. Your Love

Your choice of love moves me deeply.
You set Your love on me forever.
You chose to be a Man forever.
Your choice made You go onto the cross.
Now I live in union with You.
With greatest joy You live in me.
You chose me to be Your first love.
I share Your all forevermore.

2. Your choice of love moves me deeply.
You set Your love on me forever.
I'm Your love dream since eternity.
You chose to be a Man forever.
You are fully committed to me,
Involve Yourself eternally.
You and I dwell in deepest union.
We become one - how warm and sweet.

3. I shall love You with all my heart,
Lay aside all and overcome all.
I shall love You, follow You fore'er.
Apart from You, all are vanities.
I'm Your first love now and forever.
You'll be with me eternally.
O' Lord, may I love You only,
Follow closely, till I see Your face.

4. It is Your choice to love me fore'er.
Your flame of love has overcome all.
You're overjoyed to live in me,
Set Your banner of love on me.
O' Lord, Your love, Your glorious life,
Your heart, Your all forever are mine.
There'll be endless discoveries
In Your boundless ocean of love.

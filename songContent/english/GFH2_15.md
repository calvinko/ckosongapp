# The Love Of Salvation

1. How I thank You, dear Lord!
You came to die for me.
You shed Your blood; Hands, feet were pierced,
To save a wretch like me.

2. Moved deeply by this love,
Eyes brimmed with tears I bow.
O Son of God, how can it be
That You should die for my sins!

3. Saved from eternal death,
Set free from pow’r of sin,
Led into the marvelous light,
E’er in God’s kingdom I live.

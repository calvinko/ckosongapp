# My Response to "It's Finished!"

"Sing, my spirit!"
My response to "It's finished!" - Great is the Lord!
By Lord Jesus, I've got perfect salvation,
Become child of God!
Taste the Father's eternal love, full acceptance,
Fore'er I can drink from His vast ocean of grace!

2. "Be bold and free!" —
My response to "It's finished!" - Worship with joy!
How beautiful and precious is God's heart!
How sweet is His love!
O' by His cross all the hindrance is removed;
Now I long to draw closer to my dearest Lord!

3. "Shout for joy!" —
My response to "It's finished!" - Hallelujah!
The Lord's risen, ascended to God's right hand,
To be my High Priest!
Now the Father accepts me as He does the Lord;
Christ carries me. He appears for me before God.

4. "Be full of faith" —
My response to "It's finished!" - Fearless and bold!
The Lord has solved my greatest problem of life!
I have perfect peace.
He has gained all authority in heaven, on earth!
His grace must be more than sufficient for me!

5. "Fully love Him!"
My response to "It's finished" - Rightly I should!
The Lord bought me with His blood to be His own,
His love moves my heart!
With the highest and whole-hearted love He loves me!
O', how precious, I'll respond to Him with my love!

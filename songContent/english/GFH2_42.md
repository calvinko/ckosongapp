# Fix My Eyes Above

1. My Beloved longs that I
behold Him now in glory.
He who lives, once was dead,
is alive forevermore.
He’s ascend’d to the Father’s right hand,
Given glory and authority.
May my eyes fix on Him,
let His glory transform me.

2. He longs that I see Him
seated at the Father’s right.
How glorious! He appears
before the Father for me.
The Father loves me as He loves Him.
He is pleased with me; I am His dear.
May my eyes fix above -
Changes not the Father’s love!

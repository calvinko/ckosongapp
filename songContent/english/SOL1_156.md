# The Heart of My Dear Abba

Out of His love, Abba needs me most.
It is His joy to kiss me, embrace me.
My smiling face is His great delight.
For me He sings and dances,
shouts for joy.
We enjoy this affection
And our hearts feel so warm.
The past journey,
sweet memories, He treasures.
Seeing the future, we leap for joy.
We're closely united,
Hand in hand walk forward
To glory. It is so sweet.
The glory and grace
Abba saves for me
Are precious and great as
the boundless sea.
Their height and depth
can't be measured.
How blessed it is He is my dear Abba!
This deepest affection
Satisfies His heart and mine.
Long to embrace forever!

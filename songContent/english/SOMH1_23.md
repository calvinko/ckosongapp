# The Infinite Lord Enters Into Our Life

He walked with Adam and Eve.*(Gen. 3:1-11)*
He ate the meal prepared by Abraham.*(Gen. 18:1-8)*
The Lord wrestled with Jacob!*(Gen. 3:22-32)*
The Lord buried Moses!*(Deut. 34)*
He made a pledge with David! *(2 Sam.7; Ps. 89:3, 34, 39; Jer. 33:19-21)*
He became a nursing child! *(Luke 2:25-35)*
Joseph and Mary, He obeyed, *(Luke 2:25-35; Mark 6:16)*
The carpenter's son He was. *(Matt. 13:53-58)*
The same with me in all things.*(Heb. 2:16-18)*
He sat by the well, asked for a drink.*(John 4:1-42)*
He gave sight to the blind, *(John 9:1-41)*
He opened His heart to Mary;
He wept before the tomb.*(Luke 10:18-42; John 11:33-40)*
The disciples' feet He washed!*(John 13:1-17)*
He bled for me *(Matt. 26:26-30; 1 Pet. 1:18-21)*
To ever live in me,*(John 14:16-24, 17:26)*
To unite with me.*(Gal. 2:20; 1 Cor. 6:17)*
He said, "Reach out your hand,
And place it in My side!"*(John 20:24-29)*
He asked, "Do you love Me?
Feed My lambs; tend My sheep.
Come along, follow Me!*(John 21:15-22)*
One day I'll come to take you.
You'll always be with Me!"*(1 Thess. 4:16-18)*

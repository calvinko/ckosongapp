# The Power of Darkness Will Vanish

The enemies seem to be winning.
They are walking around like roaring lions.
They lead many into sins by temptations.
Behold, the Lord's salvation's powerful.
He saves us from sins,
And we walk on the path of light.

2. The enemies make a final struggle.
They make up accusations and shadows.
They suppress us. They work willfully.
Behold, the truth from the Lord gives forth light.
He has set us free.
We shout for vict'ry every day.

3. Don't be afraid. In a little while,
The war of ages will be done!
The Lord's glorious victory
Will be proclaimed to all corners of the world.
Satan's power will be shattered
The darkness wil flee away.
The truth will shine.
The Lord will reign forever.

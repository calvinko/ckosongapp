# The Lord Is Fair And Good

1. He’s so fair! He’s so good!
My abundant love feast!
He’s so fair! He’s so good!
More than tongue or pen can tell.
Close to Him, close to Him;
I foretaste heavenly joy.
Close to Him, close to Him;
Is my portion on earth.

2. He’s so fair! He’s so good!
My Friend faithful and true;
He’s so fair! He’s so good!
My Beloved, sweet and dear.
He’s with me, He’s with me,
Nothing on earth shall I fear!
He’s with me, He’s with me,
My heart’s gladdened and cheered.

3. He’s so fair! He’s so good!
Spurs me on all my life.
He’s so fair! He’s so good!
Gives me joy and comfort.
For the Lord, for the Lord,
I will bravely go forth.
For the Lord, for the Lord,
I will strive through my course.

# Father Welcomes Me Home

Once I was lost, away from home,
Wand'ring, grieving in sins.
Abba sent His beloved Son
To become a Man.
He's forsaken by the Father.
He drank the bitter cup.
The Lord cried,
"It's finished!" It is finished!
Forever the veil is torn!
I have a new, living way.
I enter the Holy Place,
See Abba face to face, behold His glory.
He presses me deeply into His heart.
The lost son's come home. Let's celebrate!

# 2.  Hours’ Presence

1. Twenty-four hours’ presence with me!
What a sweet and precious blessing!
Who is so close to me but You?
You’re my present help in time of need.

*Chorus:
Presence day and night, how warm and sweet!
With You by my side I’m not alone.
Presence day and night, joined heart to heart!
Living with You, I taste heav’nly bliss.*

2. Twenty-four hours’ presence with me!
You are my support and comfort.
With You, I’ll not fear nor worry.
You are always glad to stay with me.

3. Twenty-four hours’ presence with me!
I count on You, pour out my heart.
You are my Beloved and best Friend,
Carry me ahead, and hold my hand.

4. Twenty-four hours’ presence with me!
Lord, Your love can endure all tests!
Your friendship is warm and precious.
All the world can’t be compared to it

5. Twenty-four hours’ presence with me!
You have filled my life with Your grace;
Having lived with You for these years,
Can I live in heaven without You?

# The Most Beautiful Love Dream

Your beautiful love 
from the beginning
Was set on me from eternity.
It's Your deepest secret 
hidden in Your heart,
Your sweet love dream, longing.
For You and me, 
You have a stunning love plan.
You have seen the joy ahead.
For Your love dream, 
You came gladly as a Man,
And You despised all the shame.
Lord, You loved me; 
You gave Yourself for me.
Your death-daring love shakes 
God's heart and mine.
You betrothed me 
with Your precious blood.
We're one spirit forever.

You live in me, and I live in You.
We're heart to heart, 
I'm Yours and You are mine.
I've gained the most 
beautiful love in this world.
I have captured Your heart.
Now and forever, 
full of warmth and romance,
We will walk on this path of love.
In this matchless beauty, 
warmth, and love
I'll wait for Your return.
You long to carry me home.
Then we'll enter the eternity of love!
O' Lord Jesus, may You return!

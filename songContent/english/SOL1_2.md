# The Adorable and Captivating Risen Lord

1. Let's rejoice! He is risen!
Overcame Satan and death;
He swallowed up death, gives us glow-ing hope.
The glorious Lord rose from the dead,
For-ever we should rejoice!

2. My Jesus' tomb is empty,
He has overcome death,
Manifesting life incorruptible.
In life's journey, He walks with me,
He is my fountain of joy.

3. The risen Lord lives with me,
He clothes me with joy and smile.
He's my strength and drive, He's my sweet portion.
He lives in my heart; He's my Beloved;
With Him, life is warm and sweet.

4. The risen Lord is like the dawn,
Like the dew in the bright morn.
He is my Beloved; He's my closest Friend,
Joined in spirit, we live in love;
Life is meaningful and sweet.

5. The risen Lord is full of power,
But He is kind and gentle.
He shattered my doubts, wiped away my tears;
His love's appearance, I so relish.
My dear Lord, may You come soon.

6. Let's rejoice! He is risen! *(Ha! Ha!)*
*(O that glorious dawn!)*
He conquered Satan and death, *(Ha! Ha!)*
Broke the bar of death, brought us light and hope.
*(O death, where is your victory?)*
*(O death, where is your sting?)*
"The glorious Lord rose from the dead!"
What a glorious news of joy! *(Ha! Ha!)*
*(Hallelujah!)*

# Such Great Love

You became flesh, dwelt among men,
Full of grace and full of truth.
You came from heaven to seek me,
And to give me hope.
You had no place to lay Your head.
You suffered much pain and grief.
To fully sympathize with me,
You offered Yourself.

*Chorus:
Such love as Yours so wide and deep;
It will always stay the same -
Everlasting!
I can't measure this great love.
My heart is amazed.
You deserve my love.*

2. Nailed on the cross,
God-forsaken,
Who could understand Your pain?
Hands, feet were nailed,
and blood was shed;
You gave up Your all.
In that while I was a sinner,
Christ gave His life for me;
God shows His own love toward me -
O' such love as Yours!

*Chorus:
Such love as Yours so wide and deep;
It will always stay the same -
Everlasting!
I can't measure this great love.
My heart is amazed.
You deserve my love.*

# I Trust! I Hope! I Love!

Lord, with Your love, with Your presence,
I trust! I hope! I love!
I will rise above storms and waves, distress, and sickness,
Slanders, tribulations, come what may!
I trust! I hope! I love!
I fight the good fight by Your grace.
My heart's radiant; sing all the way.
I trust! I hope! I love!
May I offer my forever burning heart!

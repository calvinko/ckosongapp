# Who Affect the Lord the Most

I am amazed! I affect You so much!
For me, You came from heaven to the earth;
You love to be a Man forever,
Gladly gave Yourself for me on the cross!
Your love is overwhelming!
Melts and captures my heart.
You want to have the deepest union with me,
And live with me forevermore.
You are self sufficient, infinite, ov'rflowing,
Because of love You need me most!
You long to see my face and hear my voice;
You lead me to Your fair inner chambers,
To enjoy love's interflow.
May I live in freedom and joy
As Your darling and friend!
Daily lean on Your bosom of love,
My heart ever close to You!

# The Lord in the Flesh and Blood

1. In the silent night sky, stars twinkling bright,
In the town of Bethlehem my Saviour's born.
Lying in a manger, wrapped in cloths,
My Lord came in the flesh and blood.

*Chorus:
The Lord loved me and became flesh,
Fully understands my troubles.
He became a Man. He feels what I feel.
He's full of compassion and love.*

2. He once lived on the earth. He once was tired.
He once was thirsty, hungry, sad and angry.
His heart was once pierced through. He once did weep.
He once suffered all pains for me.

*Chorus:
The Lord loved me and became flesh,
Fully understands my troubles.
He became a Man. He feels what I feel.
He's full of compassion and love.*

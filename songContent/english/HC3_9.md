# After the Cross

1. After the cross
All that's in heaven roars. The praises rise.
From God's creations,
And His glorious will, to the song of the Lamb,
All of us can see God's whole-hearted love
And His commitment:
He is faithful and truthful and His sacrifice
Is righteous and selfless.

2. He did not complain.
He did not argue. He is humble.
He did not seek His own.
Nor did He look for others' recognition,
Nor any acceptance, any praises, rewards,
Any glory.
The Lamb walked up to the cross. The lamb was slain.
The Lamb bled and died.

3. Abba forsook Him.
Abba covered His face. He chastised Him.
Abba's heart was pierced.
The Holy Spirit gave strength to the Lord
To offer Himself, to give His all to God
Without blemish.
From every step of His life to the last moment,
He led Him to the cross.

After the cross, the enemy's silenced:
No more slanders, or accusations.
It is final. The truth's revealed.
God's love has gained the vict'ry
In heaven and earth.
There are no other voices, but only voice of praise -
Giving thanks without ceasing.
All shout for joy! All sing and cheer
Forever and ever!

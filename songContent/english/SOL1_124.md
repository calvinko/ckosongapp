# You in My Life

You in my life, I'm no longer lonely or afraid.
You in my life, I rejoice, overflow with hope.
Your love compels me, it is my sweet rest.
Your heart, Your arms are mine. My heart is Yours.
March on with You. We fight and dance,
Accomplish Your will, await Your coming.

2. You in my life it's so blessed, warm and beautiful.
With You I can use problems and challenge all things.
You are my life, giving me the drive.
We'll finish Your glorious commission with joy.
We're hand in hand, with love interflows,
To fight the battle, accomplish Your will.

# The Lord's Personal Love and Affection to Me

His personal love to me
Is from eternity past.
Which made Him long to be a Man,
To be like me, closest to me,
Despise all shame, endure the cross
To unite with me forever.

2. His personal love to me
Is wholehearted, committed,
Full of passion, warmth and romance.
It's the flame of Jehovah,
It's the love from the beginning.
I'm enthralled in this great love.

3. His personal love to me
Is the secret in His heart,
His eternal sweet dream of love,
His joy ahead on the cross,
His desire and flames of love.
He embraces me to His heart.

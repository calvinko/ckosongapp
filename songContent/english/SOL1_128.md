# Eternal Dance of Love

My Abba stretches out His hand,
Invites me gladly to join
Him and the Lord and the Spirit
In Their eternal dance of love.
Such love who can fathom,
Such joy who can describe,
Enter into God's circle of love,
My bliss and joy overflow.

2. Daily from morning to night,
Abba, the Lord and the Spirit
Abide with me and surround me;
They walk, work and dance with me.
They share Their all with me;
Enter deep into my life.
Live in such warm and deep fellowship,
They are my life and sweet portion.

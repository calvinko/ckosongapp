# I'm His Ultimate Love Secret

The Father loves the Son; 
He brought forth a stunning plan:
From God the Eternal to God the Eternal
I'm His never-ending love gift!
He who gave and He who received,
Poured out all and suffered the most.
Father's love dream for His Son 
has been gloriously fulfilled!
O' expression of boundless love!

2. I am Your sweet love dream, 
Your first love, eternal love,
From everlasting to everlasting,
I satisfy Your heart the most.
Lord, I was made for You.
I belong to You alone;
I'm the bone of Your bones, 
the love secret of Your heart;
Your ultimate, eternal choice!
My Lord, You will never
Have another secret of love!

O, nothing beyond!
O, nothing beyond! *(Final!! Final!!)*
I'm His ultimate love secret.
O, nothing beyond! *(Final!! Final!!)*
I'm Your eternal, ultimate love dream!
This endless path of love sweet and fairest,
Like a poem, like a dream!

3. From the Light, Lord, You came,
To declare God's love secret.
The Trinity made the impossible possible.
God's flaming love has been revealed.
O' Lord, You became a Man,
Bore the cross, and kept the scars.
The love dream of the Father 
and the Son and the Spirit
Has come true! I'm in His heart!
I am God's ultimate dream of love,
His only love, dearest treasure.

O, nothing beyond!
O, nothing beyond! *(Final!! Final!!)*
I'm His ultimate love secret.
O, nothing beyond! *(Final!! Final!!)*
He will embrace all His beloved ones
To His fairest heav'nly home
In New Jerusalem,
To eternal glory!

# The Comforter has Come

The Comforter has come. 
It's the Father's promise.
O' how precious it is, 
fore'er He lives in me,
Like a well of water, 
ever overflowing,
And I will never thirst.

2. The Comforter has come. 
Fore'er He lives in me.
He never leaves me, 
helps me in time of need.
O' how gentle He is. 
His love is long and deep.
He'll bring me to glory.

3. He's always by my side. 
We go through floods and fires.
He speaks comfort to me. 
He makes me full of hope.
He gives me my vineyard, 
and I'll sing to the Lord.
I'm in love with the Lord.

4. The Comforter's with me. 
He is always my Guide.
All things He teaches me, 
so I can please the Lord.
When I'm sad and weary, 
He comforts me deeply
And gives me joy and peace.

5. When I draw near to God, 
He teaches me the truth,
Makes my heart live again. 
I'm captured by the Lord.
His love draws me away. 
The world I lay aside,
Yearn for His love only.

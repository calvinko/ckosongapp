# O' Lord, What do You Want Today?

Every day I'll ask, "Lord, what do You want?"
Do You want me to provide for Your Holy Place?
At this moment, how do You feel?
What are You longing for?
Not only do I pour out
My heart before You,
O' Lord, but I want to know
What You are thinking now.
I don't want to just live for myself.
I don't want to just think of my needs.
I don't want to just think of the work
That I've been doing for You.
O' Lord, what do You want today?
Please tell me! Please tell me!
O' Lord, what do You want today?
I desire to know.

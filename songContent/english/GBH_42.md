# A Day of Glory and Sweetness

One day there will be
no more foes and fears.
One day there will be
no more cries and tears.
One day there will be
no more sickness, hunger, and war,
No more bleak cold winter.
There will be only bright cloudless dawn,
beautiful eternal spring.
One day there will be
no more heartbreak and death.
There will be only sweetness eternally.

Everywhere's full of life, full of warmth,
flooding with light, love, joy, and songs!
One day I shall see You face to face!
Forever I will live in the depth of Your heart,
Ever drinking, enjoying, Your endless love,
Enjoying with You love's sweet eternity.
O sweetness, sweetness, sweetness!
O, love's eternity!
O glory, glory, glory, for all eternity!

# You Want Me the Most

Year by year, You're always with me.
Today I still enjoy You are with me.
Your love appearing in the past journey,
Your closeness to us,
Tell me clearly You love us dearly.
You chose us for Your commission.
You commit Yourself into my life.
You pour out Your grace endlessly.
You bring me into the depth of Your heart,
I know for sure I'm Your eternal love!
May You compel me to strive on
Until the church is accomplished.
Today, You still want me the most.
Every tomorrow, You want me most.

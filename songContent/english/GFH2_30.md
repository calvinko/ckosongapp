# His Wounds My Hiding Place

1. Rock of Ages cleft for me!
Precious blood was shed for me!
Cleansed me of my guilt and sin,
Cleared away thick clouds and mist;
Your love and blood, the best balm,
Heal the wounds deep in my heart.

2. Rock of Ages cleft for me!
Precious blood was shed for me!
Washed me whiter than the snow,
Even brighter than crystal.
The Father opens His arms,
Welcomes me back to His fold.

3. Rock of Ages cleft for me!
Precious blood was shed for me!
Cast away my fears and doubts,
Bestow me sufficient faith.
Boldly to the Holiest,
Father’s bosom is my place.

4. Rock of Ages cleft for me!
Precious veil is rent for me!
Let me see the Father’s face,
Enjoy His full acceptance;
Ever live in cloudless morn,
Abide in the Holy Place.

5. Rock of Ages cleft for me!
Your wounds are my hiding place,
Rest and shelter of my heart,
Shield me from tempest and charge.
No more shadows and dark clouds,
I rejoice in Your embrace.

6. Rock of Ages cleft for me!
Let me hide e’er in Your heart;
Fully taste fruit of the cross,
Daily drink of Your great love;
No more shadows, no more doubt,
My heart is drawn after You.

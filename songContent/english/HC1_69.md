# Steadfast and Colourful

I have the same mind with You,
And You are with me.
As we walk, we get closer.
Think of what You think. Love what You love.
We feel the same. We think the same.
It is not moving mountains,
Crossing the Red Sea every day;
But I walk steadfastly on the way,
Do the work of my hand.
All these days in my life enrich
Our experience. Our friendship grows.
All these I won't forget. Not being aware,
I love You more and more.
You and I live better days.
You'll treasure even more.
Our yesterday, today, tomorrow -
All are steadfast and colourful.

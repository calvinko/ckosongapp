# The Deepest Affection of the Son of God. His River of Joy

O' Lord, Your life, 
Your affection and joy
Is like an ever rushing falls.
Ever overflows, overwhelms my heart.
Now I can drink from Your river of joy.
Day'n night You attend to me,
Gazing with Your longing.
Your love's full of passion, 
romance, and beauty,
The very flame of Jehovah.
Your reaction is like volcanic eruption.
Your thoughts of love are more than stars.
For me You shout for joy; You sing and dance.
You delight in me, You desire me.
You meet me with loving kindness.
You in my life, infinite bliss and joy.
In the depth of Your heart,
I enjoy living in love with You.
I feel satisfied and warm.
(My heart ever overflows.)

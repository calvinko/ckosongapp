# My Father Who Loves Me Ever

1. My Father who loves me ever,
Holds me with His loving hand;
With His highest love that knows no end,
Paves my journey of glory.
In afflictions, through dark valley,
My heart can rejoice in You;
“Through suff’ring to glory” - t’is my way,
Too beautiful is my gain.

2. My Father who loves me ever,
You've listened to all my pray’rs;
All Your promises will never fail,
And Your time must be the best.
Mid the trials You open a way,
Perfect me through sufferings;
What You’ve planned for Your beloved ones
Must surpass what I can think

# You are the Conclusion of My Dreams

You are the conclusion of my dreams!
I love You! I pursue You!
In You, I have found all my dreams come true.
There's no better story.
No other endings can be as perfect.
From the beautiful heart of Yours
Comes this exquisite, perfect plan,
Your love, affection
Make up all of my days.
It makes all so fair; it makes all so sweet.
There may be tears, but joy comes at last.
What a wonderful life!
Because of You, because of Your will
I treasure my every day.

# I Affect You Most. You Need Me Most

You are self-existing, self-sufficient,
Yet, I'm the bone of Your bones.
This is Your desire to closely join with me
In one spirit, heart to heart.

2. Lord, You created me for Yourself.
Gladly You became a Man.
You suffered much and were nailed on the cross,
For You want to gain my heart.

3. For me You came from heaven to the earth.
Your love journey moves my heart.
You passed through all pain and poured out Your life
To seal this love covenant.

4. For love You went to the mount of myrrh.
Lord, You saw the joy ahead.
You're willing to endure the bitter cross
To be in love with me.

5. For love You passed through all pain and suff'ring.
Lord, I have captured Your heart.
You rejoice for me, quiet me with Your love.
O' I affect You so much!

6. In Your love I have found my resting place.
Lord, I know You need me most;
I'm fore'er amazed I affect You so much!
I have captured Your heart.

7. For love You're deeply in need of me.
Without me You are in want.
Throughout the ages, across the universe,
Who could satisfy Your want?

8. You are thinking of me day and night.
Your love thoughts are more than stars.
Lord, Your fervent love draws my heart away.
Sweetly I live in Your love.

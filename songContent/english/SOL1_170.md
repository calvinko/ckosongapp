# My Eternal Father

Parents and friends may forsake me,
You're my eternal Father!
You're Father of the fatherless.
Your eternal bosom is mine!
You carry me forever!

2. Your big hand holds my little hand.
I entrust my life to You.
You will perfect all things for me.
Abba, Your time must be the best.

3. Let me always enjoy with You
This Father-son affection,
Live in the depths of Your heart.
Abba, Your heart is satisfied.

# Your Jealousy of Love is Unyielding as the Grave

1. Your jealousy is fierce as the grave!
Your love to me is like flames of fire!
You long for me to set my love on You.
Your love has overcome pains and trials!
Every barrier torn down,
Every idol destroyed,
Clouds and shadows removed,
You have vanquished all foes!
Your love conquered all,
Triumphed over all things.
Your love banner flies over me.

2. Your desire and love began before time!
Your love thoughts outnumber sands and all stars!
Your love of the cross is stronger than death!
You leapt over mountains to see my face.
You request to hear my voice.
Your love I can't forget.
Lord I relish Your love.
Your affections fill my soul!
Draw me to a secret place.
We'll be face to face, heart to heart.
May Your banner fly over me,
Fly over my heart evermore!

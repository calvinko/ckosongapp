# This Deepest Affection is True

It's most precious I am born of You.
Your seed abides in me.
Your life and my life forever united!
I am Your bosom dear child.

2. Father, You have numbered my hair.
Your love thoughts are more than stars.
Over me, You shout for joy and dance!
O' what a father I have.

3. Father, You have committed Yourself,
Forever I affect You!
Your calling is so glorious.
Your love plan is beautiful.

4. You are the only normal Abba.
Nothing can compare Your love.
Father-son affection satisfies our hearts!
(Abba and me)
For this affection is true!!

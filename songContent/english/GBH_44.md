# Carry Me Home

1. One day from Heaven He'll come; 
He'll come to carry me home.
To the New Jerusalem, 
He'll come to carry me home.
In the clouds, His radiant chariot, 
I'll see His glorious face.
He'll take me to His warm fairest home, 
He'll come to carry me home.

2. One day from Heaven He'll come; 
He'll come to carry us home.
To the New Jerusalem, 
He'll come to carry us home.
In the clouds, His radiant chariot, 
we'll see His glorious face.

He'll take us to His warm fairest home, 
He'll come to carry us home.

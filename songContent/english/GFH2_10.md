# All For Me

1. Lord Jesus, You once gave Yourself for me.
Once You poured out Your life freely for me;
Walked up to Golgotha, You were hung on the tree,
Blood and water came out, all for me.

2. Lord Jesus, You suffered so much for me.
For me You bore those stripes in your body;
You are God, yet for me, were despised by the world.
That I might gain You. Thank You, Lord.

3. O Lord Jesus, whom my soul loves!
Now and ever, You abide in my heart;
Precious indwelling, union forever!
Nothing can keep You and me apart.

4. Storms and waves may come to me all the way,
But all these just urge me to draw near You;
Lord, I’m glad to abide in Your bosom of love,
And respond to You with all my life.

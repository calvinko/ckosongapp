# Let's Dance and Shout for Joy

Let us dance for the joyful new -
Christ arose from the dead!
He's risen! He's risen!
We shall rejoice. Let us dance!
For te Lord is risen!
For the Lord lives forevermore!

2. Let us dance! He rules over all.
He loves me forever.
He's ascend'd. He's ascend'd.
We shall rejoice, shout for joy!
For the Lord is glorified.
For the Lord is glorified forever!

3. Shout for joy! He's coming quickly
To take us to His home.
He's coming! He's coming!
This is our hope, 'tis our hope!
May the Lord Jesus come soon!
We'll live together in heav'n forever!

4. Shout for joy! He will come to reign,
To restore all things.
The Lord reigns! The Lord reigns!
All lands rejoice, shout for joy,
The Lord will reign forever!
The Lord Jesus will reign forever!

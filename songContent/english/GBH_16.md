# His Personal Love to Me

His personal love to me!
Undying, pure and ever new,
therefore with loving kindness
His love great floods cannot quench,
Through all ages, it will not fade,
Overcomes all trials and pain.
He ever strives my heart to gain!

2. His personal love to me
Is my great drive through all my life. have I drawn you.
When I'm in despair, He comforts me,
His love overcomes all things,
Colourful, warm, tender and strong.
I'll praise His love forever!

3. His personal love to me!
So sweet and warm, it gives me hope.
He shares all my sorrow and joy.
His love is my courage and faith,
Fills my heart like a living spring.
O in His bosom may I ever stay!

4. His personal love to me
Captures my heart and makes me sing!
O what breadth and length, height and depth!
His love paves my lifelong path,
Grace upon grace through all my days,
Beyond compare, His love to me!

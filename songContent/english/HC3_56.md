# My Heart Will Never Die

If one day, I can't go as my heart wants,
And I can't walk anymore,
My heart will never due.
I'll still remember those who are from afar,
To give thanks and pray for them.
My heart is toward You.
If I can't sing, the voice for prayers is no more, I still have fellowship with You with my all.
My heart stays in Your presence.
If one day, I can't remember anything,
And don't know what happened in the past,
I cannot recognize anyone,
Not even knowing who I am,
I just wait for the day I fade away.
Let the "present me" speak for the "future me"
The words from my heart: my heart will not die;
I will still love You the most.
It is just at that moment I'm constrained,
I forget how to tell You.
Bound in this weakness, I may forget and lose all.
O' Lord, You will never forget.
Your heart to me will never die.
At that moment I can't respond to You.
Lord, You'll still surround me,
And hold my hand tight
Though I can't feel it anymore.
You'll hold me closer to You.
You will carry me
To enter eternity.

# Your Grace Makes Me Strong and Brave

Lord, Your grace makes me strong and brave,
Helps me walk on and be steadfast.
Thinking of Your love, bountiful grace,
How can my heart turn back?

*Chorus:
Arise now, O' my spirit!
Fear no giants nor mountains!
Arise now, O' my spirit!
Heed the voice of the Lord.*

 2. Lord, Your grace makes me strong and brave.
You can turn weakness into strength,
You change dry bones into a great army.
You give life to the dead.

*Chorus:
Arise now, O' my spirit!
Fear no giants nor mountains!
Arise now, O' my spirit!
Heed the voice of the Lord.*

3. Lord, Your grace makes me strong and brave,
I despise all trials and pain.
I can sing in dark night, full of great hope.
By You I can do all things.

*Chorus:
Arise now, O' my spirit!
Fear no giants nor mountains!
Arise now, O' my spirit!
Heed the voice of the Lord.*

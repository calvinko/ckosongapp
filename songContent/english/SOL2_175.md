# Heart to Heart

Though You're in heaven high,
I am on earth below,
You're in my heart.
I'm joined to You as one,
Fore'er in one spirit.
I am Yours; You are mine. We're heart to heart.

2. Though You're in heaven high,
You are closest to me in one spirit.
You've made a home in me.
Gladly I live in You.
Love interflow with You
O' such sweet bliss!
Living with You feels like spring every day.

3. Though You're in heaven high,
I am on earth below,
we're joined as one.
No distance, no hindrance,
we share the closest tie.
I'll never be lonely in this union.
We're lovers, hand in hand in unison.

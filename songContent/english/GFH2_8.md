# The Love Of Endurance

From heaven, Lord, You came.
It makes me so amazed.
You were mocked and despised,
yet Your love was still the same.
E’en Your brothers’ sneers could not quench Your love,
Men’s indifference showed more Your love of endurance.
The world forsook You, and cried, “Away with Him!”
You were steadfast, walked to Calvary!
Wondrous Cross! Wondrous Cross!
Vast forgiveness and bountiful grace flowing from the cross!
Love as flashes of fire, as the flame of God!
Your fervent love to men makes me ever amazed, adore!

# Long for Revival

Long for revivals - may the Spirit
Of God be upon us
And we will bring the good news to all nations.
Long for revivals - we won't be silent.
We've set the watchmen to pray
Day and night,
Until her righteousness
Goes forth as brightness,
Her salvation as a burning light.
The church is a praise in the earth;
Until we can see the beauty -
Liberty, comfort,
The year of the Lord's favour,
The oil of gladness,
The cloak of praise, trees of righteousness,
The robe of righteousness, the royal crown
Adorned with jewels, what is sown
Bringing forth its sprouts, goodness upon goodness,
Double portion, everlasting joy.
Long for revivals! Long for revivals!

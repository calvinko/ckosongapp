# Be Strong and Arise

Be strong, O' My son, be strong!
Be strong and courageous, be strong!
There are no failures,
For the battle's not over.
Be strong O' My Son, be strong!
Arise, O' My love, arise!
Arise, My warrior bride!
Come now, My awesome army
With banners up high.
Arise, O' My darling, arise!

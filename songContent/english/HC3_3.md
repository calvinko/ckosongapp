# Tell His Name

1. To all nations I'll tell His name;
I'll proclaim His amazing love.
Though I'm so weak, and I have nothing,
His pow'r is perfected in me.

2. He loves me, what else should I love?
I will remain faithful to Him!
I'm always young because of His love.
I'll tell His name till I die.

Chorus:
Let us go to all corners of the world
To build the church after His heart everywhere.
Let us bear witness for Him in all lands.
"O' Lord, please come. I long for You to come soon."

1. Yes, we must go and tell His name
To raise the cup of salvation,
Facing even thousands of enemies,
Yet we see His greater glory.

2. Sending me, He must be with me.
Thousands to love Him, 'tis my plea.
I will be brave, fight the good fight for Him.
Onward, let's strive to tell His name.

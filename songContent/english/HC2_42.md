# Heavenly Gate is Open  

The Heav'nly gate is now open.
His love pours down, my cup runs over with joy.
From my heart flows rivers of living water.
He gives me life; endless joy fills my heart.
The Heav'nly gate is forever open.
Abba's bosom my warm dwelling fore'er.
Abba, the Lord long to embrace me
In love eternally.
God's dream of love has now come true.
Face to face we will meet in the clouds.
Enjoy eternally in sweet love.

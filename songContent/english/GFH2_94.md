# The Deep Affection

My Elder Brother, My dear Friend is the Lord,
Our friendship endures, our affection grows deep;
He paved my past journey with His grace and His love,
For my days ahead, I walked by my Beloved.
Though my future is yet to be known,
The Lord has promised to be with me always;
He has promised to be my Shepherd,
To guide me each step, until I see His face.
The path of the just is as the shining light,
That shines more and more till the perfect day.

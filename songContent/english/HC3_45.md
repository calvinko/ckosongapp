# Let Me See that You are with Me

O' God may You open my eyes.
Let me see that You are with me,
And Your armies are tens of thousands;
All things work for my good!
I am always the love in Your heart.
What more shall I fear?
You are my guarantee and my strength.
By You, I sing and rejoice.

2. O' God may You open my eyes.
Let me see that You are with me.
I may walk through the valley of death.
I will fear no evil.
Your rod and Your staff they comfort me,
What more shall I fear?
Surely goodness, mercy shall follow me
All the days of my life.

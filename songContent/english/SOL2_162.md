# He Desires Me and Delights in Me the Most

Your love to me is wholehearted,
Infinitely personal.
Your have reserved Your heart for me.
Now I know You delight in me.
Now I know Your desire for me.

2. You'll embrace me forever
With Your love from beginning.
Deathdaring love shakes heav'n and earth.
Now I know You delight in me.
Now I know Your desire for me.

3. You've longed to live in my heart,
To unite with me forev'r.
We are in love; live together.
Now I know You delight in me.
Now I know Your desire for me.

4. For You and me, You have designed
An eternal plan of love.
We have the same beautiful dream.
Now I know You delight in me.
Now I know Your desire for me.

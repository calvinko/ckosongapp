# The Blood of the Lamb

The blood of the Lamb opened a new way
To the Holy Place, Abba's bosom.
How He loves the Lord, He loves me the same.
I am His dear child; He's my rest.

2. The blood of the Lamb washed away my sins.
I have no shadows, whiter than snow.
He set free my heart, defeat'd all my foes.
I lift up my head, shout "victory"!

3. The blood of the Lamb made me righteous.
The holy city, the trees of life,
All belong to me - I'm enthroned with Him.
In His light fore'er I abide.

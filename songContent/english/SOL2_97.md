# You Will Surely Give Me the Best

"My dear Lord, You'll give me the best!"
I said it not when my days 
were easy and light;
I said it not at the time when I gained
What I had ever dreamed of.
When I'm in loss, when I'm in lack,
When I suffer, when I am troubled,
I can't understand why; under pressure,
I am filled with worries.
Yet I must get over all feelings.
I trust Your eternal love 
Surely You will give me the best.
I know You will never miss a single detail 
All my hair You've numbered with care.

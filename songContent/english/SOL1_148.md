# He Walks with Me through Valleys to the Land of Abundance

My dear Lord holds my hand
To walk on this glorious marathon.
He is faithful in His commission.
Step by step, He leads me to His heart.

2. My dear Lord holds my hand
To walk on this warm and joyful path.
This path of love paved with pink petals -
O' how sweet, my heart is satisfied.

3. He holds me tight to passthrough
Floods and fires, slanders, long dark valleys.
Abundant feast He lays for me.
He leads me to the abundant land.

4. Long, dark valleys are over!
He's brought forth lasting revival.
He will stretch out His mighty right hand
To finish His most beautiful will!
(A glorious bride to welcome Him back!)
Hallelujah!

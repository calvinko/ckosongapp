# Sit in Heaven with the Lord

What amazing grace, Immense and boundless!
I am in Christ -
Died with You, live with You, 
sit in heaven with You.
Your love journey on earth was all for me.

2. In heav'n I sit with You! I am whiter than snow!
My sins no more!
I'm holy as the Lord, 
dwell in the Holy Place.
Forever Abba's smiles brighten my heart.

3. In heav'n I sit with You! What amazing grace -
Beyond measure!
The Father welcomes me 
as He welcomes the Lord.
Forever I live in this grace and love.

4. My perfect Christ for me appears before God.
You're my High Priest!
Perfectly You've brought me 
to the Father's bosom.
This highest Father's love belongs to me.

5. In heav'n I sit with You. This wondrous love and grace
Makes me amazed.
I'm closest to Abba. 
He delights in me.
I'm precious as the Lord in Abba's heart.

6. In heav'n I sit with You. This wondrous love and grace 
Who can fathom?
Abba has given me precious identity.
O' I am my Abba's dearest treasure!

7. In heav'n I sit with You - I'm closest to Abba,
Sit by His side.
He gives me great glory. 
I am enthroned with Him.
I will reign with Abba eternally.

8. In heav'n I sit with You. God has opened His heart -
Love overflows.
Now He can freely pour out His love on me.
O' He's dancing for me with great delight!

9. In heav'n I sit with You - enjoy Abba's embrace,
Warmest welcome!
I can see Abba's heart - beautiful, committed.
He desires me to be closest to Him.

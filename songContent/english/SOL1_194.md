# The Lord's Desire and Mine

Father, I desire that they
whom You have given me
Be with Me where I am,
be with Me where I am
So that they may see My glory
which You have given Me,
For You loved Me before the
foundation of the world.
I desire to be in them,
Be one spirit with them,
Most deeply united,
live with them day and night,
Enjoy the sweetest union,
Live in the river of love.

2. Father, I desire that
wherever the Lord is
I'll be with Him forever,
with Him forever
To see the Lord's glory
which You have given to Him,
For You loved me and the Lord
before the world was.
I treasure the Lord lives in me;
I'm in the depth of His heart.
Most deeply united, we are in one spirit,
Enjoy the sweetest union.

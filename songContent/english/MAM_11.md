# O' God, I Pray to You

O' God, I come to You.
You love to hear my voice.
I entrust everything to You.
You make all things work for me.
Sometimes Your answer comes late;
You have a better time.
Sometimes You answer me at once,
Cheer me up from time to time.
Every day I come to You, open my mouth wide.
I need You to give me blessings and grace.
Every day I come to You. In silence I wait,
Wait for You to come close to me.

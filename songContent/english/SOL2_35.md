# A Life of Praise

I should praise God every day.
Then I live the normal way.
Great is the Lord and highly to be praised.
His greatness can't be measured.

2. I should praise God every day,
Consider His love and grace.
My heart is strengthened and always fervent,
Till His glorious return.

3. I should praise God every day,
Look upon His glorious face,
Give praises of faith. Weakness becomes strength.
Shut the mouth of enemies.

4. I should praise God every day,
Glorify His name always.
Shout for joy, clap hands, sing praise and dance.
Rejoice before Him always.

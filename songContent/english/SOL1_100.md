# A Day of Glory and Sweetness

One day, there's no more suffering and enemies;
One day, there's no more tears and dark valley;
One day, there's no more darkness and clouded day,
No more bleak cold winter.
There's only cloudless morning,
Eternal beautiful spring.
One day, no more sorrow, heartbreak and death,
There's only sweetness eternally.

Everywhere's full of life and warmth,
Flooding with love, joy and song.
One day I shall see You face to face;
Forever I live in the depth of Your heart,
Drinking, enjoying Your endless love.
Enjoy with You love's eternity!
Glory upon glory to all eternity!

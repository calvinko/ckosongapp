# Daily You Meet Me with Your Lovingkindness

Daily You greet me with Your smile
And loving-kindness.
Father, Your heart and Your bosom
Are open to me.
You love to kiss me
And to embrace me.
Daily You yearn to see my face,
To hear my voice,
To walk hand in hand.
You are so joyful
To see my face,
To hear all the words,
Flowing from my heart!

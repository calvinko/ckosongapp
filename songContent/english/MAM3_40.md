# Our Family Photo

Let us take a photo shoot.
Many many of Your sons
Hold hands and stand side by side with smiles so bright!
Some sit in front; some stand behind.
What fantastic scene it is!
Each one enhances one another,
Making all shine even brighter!
Behold, many sons of God
Follow His only begotten,
Flying to the glorious home together.
Round and round, all surround
The Father's glorious throne.
La la la la la la, our happy reunion!
The beauty of heaven
Excels all but we can reach and obtain,
The true affection of the father and son,
A life of a happy family.
The fulfillment of God's will
Is a happy God's family,
A perfect family,
A happy perfect family!

# Endless Days

Now I am here with You,
You've always been with me.
Each day, each minute, each second,
You will keep for all eternity.
More than beautiful words.
In silence there are sounds.
It's the experience of You and me,
The bliss that You and I have found.
All the lovers of this world
Will come to an end one day,
But the union between You and me
Will continue through endless days.
We'll love each other through endless days.

One day I'll see Your face
And hear that familiar voice.
The meeting of lovers in the air,
A new beginning of our joy.
I want to be closer to You.
It's the cry in my heart
Until the day You come.
I'll love You more than from the start.
All the lovers of this world
Will come to an end one day,
But the union between You and me
Will continue through endless days.
We'll love each other through endless days.

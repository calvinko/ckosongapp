# The Long March of Love - The One-Time Marathon

It's so precious, You and I joined in love!
Fore'er, You have committed Yourself.
Fully You share Your life with my life,
What a joy to live with You through the day.
You are my life, You change me
From glory to glory;
From year to year our love grows deep more and more.
Live side by side, we fight and dance.
This glorious marathon, full of sweet memories,
I treasure this unique journey!
Burning love drives me to march with You.
Let me be true to You, strive to the end
Toward the goal, for life, I race.
How You treasure my love to You
Through all battles and pain,
I treasure that I can live for You
On earth, give my love to You.

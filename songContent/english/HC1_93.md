# Song 7:10-13

1. I’m my beloved’s, his desire’s for me.
Come, my beloved! Come, my beloved!
Let’s go out to the field; in the villages
Let us spend the night.
Early let us go
To the vineyard and see
Whether the vine has bud'd and bloomed,
The pomegranates have blossomed.

2. There I will give my love to you.
The mandrakes give off a fragrance.
At our gates, at our gates are all pleasant fruits,
All manner new and old.
My beloved, my beloved,
O' you whom my soul loves,
All these I have laid up for you,
I have laid up for you.
O' my beloved, all of these
I have laid up for you

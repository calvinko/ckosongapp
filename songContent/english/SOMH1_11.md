# Prepare to Meet the Lord

You need to prepare to meet your God,
And the day of the stage of Christ.
This world and its lust will soon pass away.
They will quickly pass away.
Only the Lord is always the same.
His love satisfies my heart.
May I always love Him with a pure heart,
Set my hope on the grace brought by His coming.

2. You need to prepare to meet your God.
Life is short and time is swift.
What you have prepared for eternity?
Can you meet Him without shame?
Thinking of the endless days to come,
I should treasure this life more.
May You teach me how to number my days.
Same heart with You, I'll strive for You till You come.

3. This light momentary affliction
Is indeed preparing for us
An eternal weight of glory,
To enter the Lord's joy.
I have seen the joy set before me.
By faith I'll overcome all.
I now boast of my glorious hope,
Despise the world and love Him with all my heart.

4. Help me to see the eternity —
The endless generations to come,
Joy upon joy, eternal glory
Forever increasing.
Help me to see the results of sins -
The lake of fire with suplfur;
There will be no more hope, no more hope,
But sufferings forever and ever

5. Don't you feel fearful when you think of
The heaven and the lake of fire:
The endless glory; the pain with no end?
What do you think? How do you feel?
O' Lord, help us to redeem our time,
And bring many back to You.
What do you think? How do you feel?
Let us prepare for eternity.
One day we will meet the Lord face to face.

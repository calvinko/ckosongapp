# The Lord's Love is the Same. He Saves Me to the End

1. When the enemies accuse me,
The Lord saves me to the end.
When the world tries to tempt me,
The Lord saves me to then end.
When I don't have any strength,
The Lord saves me to the end.
When I'm down and my heart fails,
My Lord stretches out His hand
To save me.

2. When I'm weak and I fall,
His love stays the same.
When I feel I am not good,
His love stays the same.
When I lose hope and despair,
His love stays the same.
When I feel God has left me,
His love always
Stays the same.

Chorus:
I don't want to rely on myself,
But trust in His unchanging love.
His love is always the same
Let me not be afraid of the enemies or failures,
But trust in Him. He'll save me to the end.

# His Desire Is For Me

1. Lord, Your ever precious purpose
Is for me to dwell in You.
Lord, You suffered and gave Your life;
That we may meet face to face.
Through disciplines, trials, enemies,
You urge me to come to You.
With bands of love, cords of kindness,
You have bound me close to You.

2. You give me the Holy Spirit
That I’m ever joined to You.
In close friendship we’re heart to heart,
Forever in company.
You do love me; I do love You;
Nothing can make us apart,
One day You’ll come to take me up,
To share Your endless glory.

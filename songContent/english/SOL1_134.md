# What You and I Treasure

On the bitter cross You thought of me;
You saw the joy and sweetness ahead,
And You despised the shame and the cross,
That I may rest on Your bosom so sweet!

2. When You sweetly think: You've died for me,
And on the cross, You drank the bitter cup;
Your heart is filled with o'erflowing joy,
For You treasure that You have died for me.

3. I so treasure that I can love You,
And that I can live and suffer for You.
It is my joy to echo Your love,
Weaving many sweet eternal memories.

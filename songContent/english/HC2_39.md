# The Awesome Deed of the Trinity

Lord, You loved me, and gave Yourself.
For me, You were crucified.
The Father crushed and chastised You,
And left You there to die
The Father, the Son, and the Spirit
Performed this awesome deed!
You all desire for me to join
In Your love fellowship!
*(To live in Your heart!)*

2. Lord, You loved me, and gave Yourself.
You were despised as a worm:
Reproached by men, and struck by God.
You were poured out for me.
Now Your Father is my Father.
His bosom I will stay!
We taste Father and son affection.
May You be satisfied!

3. Lord, I treasure Your blood sealed love.
You tasted death for me.
To show Your love, You suffered much
Your steadfast flaming love.
The wondrous cross, the bleeding heart
Capture and melt my heart.
Lord, You betrothed me by Your blood.
I'll embrace You fore'er.

4. Now and forever I'm Your darling,
You are my true Beloved.
We are deeply unit'd; we'll share
The sweet eternity!
The royal family in New Jerusalem
Overflows riches and love,
Enjoys endless love forever!

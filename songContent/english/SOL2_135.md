# Fearfully Made and Born Again

Wonderfully, fearfully, I was creat'd,
All things bright and beautiful, full of wonders.
My heart's amazed and I praise my dear Abba,
"How great You are! How great You are!"

2. Wonderfully, fearfully, I'm born again.
I am Your created one, yet born of You.
It's so joyful to be Your dearest child.
You love me most! You love me most!
You love me as You love the Lord.

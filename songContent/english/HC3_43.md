# It is For Whom It is Prepared by the Father

Abba, on which side of Your throne will I sit?
Abba, where will I live in the new city?
Which part of the universe will I rule?
What kind of people will I oversee?
Abba, I believe for the blessing of the universe,
And for my joy, You have the best plan.
By that time, I will see what You see.
I'll see the whole picture,
Abba, Your complete plan,
Your design and Your arrangement.
I will work with You with gladness and joy.
I'll praise You forever!
Abba, to You be the glory.

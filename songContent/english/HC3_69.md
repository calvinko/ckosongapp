# You are All Mine

You are the best - O' You belong to me!
You're outstanding among ten thousand!
All maidens love You. they love You,
But You are all mine!
You love me with all Your heart.
Day and night You desire me.
You long for this wedding feast of ours.
You are all mine.
"Lord, You are all mine" - 'Tis my sweetest portion
Now and forever.

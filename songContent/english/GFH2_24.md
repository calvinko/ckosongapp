# The Love Of The Blood

1. May I oft meditate:
What a place You put me in Your heart!
How You long to be united with me!
What a price You have paid for this!

2. May I oft meditate:
"I am willing," You said through Your blood.
Betrothed me with Your blood ever to You,
How You desire and long for me.

3. May I oft meditate:
How You long to abide in my heart,
Be one spirit with me, ever draw near.
You will to share all things with me.

4. Never forget Your grace!
May I think of Your love every day;
Shakes the earth, thrills my heart, Lord, Your great love!
May my heart ever love You only!

# Song 2:8-13

Hark! This is the voice of my beloved!
O' He comes leaping over the hills.
My beloved's like a young stag, like a gazelle.
There he stands behind our wall.
He's gazing through the windows.
He's looking through the lattice.
My beloved says to me,
"Arise, my beautiful one, my love;
Come along with me and behold,

Lo, the winter is past; the rain is gone.
Trials ended, flowers bloom on the earth.
The time of the singing of birds has come.
The turtle-dove's voice has been heard!
The fig tree has ripened its figs;
Vines in blossom give forth fragrance.
O' My darling beautiful one,
Arise! Arise! And come along, and come along!"

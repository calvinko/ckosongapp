# Unshakable Foundation

Never shaken, fore'er the same:
It's God who justifies me.
In Christ, I will never be condemned.
I am God's righteousness.

2. Never shaken, fore'er the same:
Abba loves me as the Lord;
The Lord loves me as Abba loves Him 
Eternal highest love.

3. Never shaken, fore'er the same:
In heav'n I sit with the Lord 
Enjoy Abba's welcome and delight.
I am Abba's dear child.

# Taste the Perfect Fruit of the Cross

My eyes of faith behold the Father's heart;
I fully trust in the pow'r of the blood!
Boldly by faith, come to the throne of grace,
See with my eyes the Father's sweet welcome;
Enjoy the bliss of sitting in heaven with Christ,
I fully taste the sweet perfect fruit of the cross.

2. May I behold the worth of Jesus' blood,
Gladly be drawn by Abba's sweet welcome;
My spirit's free. I dwell in His love.
My eyes of faith fore'er capture His heart.
Let me always look upon Jesus and His cross,
Compelled by love, 
moved by His grace, I'll march forward.

3. How warm and sweet is the fruit of the cross,
Sets free my soul to sing and dance with joy!
The Father's heart, His smiling face how warm!
The Lord's embrace, His tender words - how sweet!
In God's circle of love and fellowship I live.
Heaven on earth, I foretaste heavenly bliss.

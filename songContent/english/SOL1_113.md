# Daily Fireworks

I think of the vast universe, all stars,
Your greatness, power and infinite glory,
You've named heavenly stars
many as seashore.
Heavens show Your way
and speak of Your glory.
How great and holy!
Who can compare with You?
Glorious! Beautiful!
You're the endless fountain!
Daily You light fireworks,
cause all stars to dance,
Celebrate our union,
Your love dream's come true.

2. Abba, it is precious
I see Your glory.
How blessed and glorious
to be Your dear child.
You've prepared a playground
for all of Your sons,
Reign with You fore'er,
Your sovereignty'll increase.
How great and holy!
My Abba's glorious!
Glorious! Beautiful!
My Abba's joyous heart!
Daily You light fireworks,
cause all stars to dance,
Celebrate our joy and
the deep affection.

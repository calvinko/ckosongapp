# My Life Response

Every stage of my life 
let me give my love response
To my dear Lord in different times.
In my youth, I loved You; 
enjoyed heaven on earth.
My vision was clear and bright,
I strived for You in my twenties.
In my prime, I built the church 
through all the trials.
I treasure to strive for You!
From now on to the day we meet,
May I give You my love more and more.
I will go from peak to peak.
Thank you, my dear Lord, 
You take care of my life.
Your love and grace fill all my life journey.
You make my path shine 
until the perfect day,
Ever brighter!
Seeing Your face, I'll love You more,
More than all words can tell.

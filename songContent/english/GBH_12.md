# I'll Never Forget You

1. Can a woman forget her nursing child, 
forget her nursing child,
That she should have no compassion 
on the son of her womb?
Yes, these may forget, forget, 
yet I will not forget you.
Behold, I have engraved you 
upon the palms of My hands!

2. Yes, I've loved you with everlasting love, 
you'll be ever on My mind.
I will never leave you, nor forsake you; 
I will carry you through life.
How happy! I have God Himself 
as my Father eternally.
When I'm fatherless or motherless, 
Yahweh will take care of me.

3. He loves me as He loves His only Son, 
He loves me with the highest love!
His thoughts of love, more than sands or stars, 
O He even numbers my hairs.
How happy now He's my Abba, 
and forever I'm His darling child.
O Father and son walk hand in hand, 
journey on endless glorious path!

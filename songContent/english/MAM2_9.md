# Let the Flowers Speak

Let the flowers speak. Let the grass whisper.
Let mountains echo, let the sea sing songs of love.
From the beginning, from days of old to now,
Day to day utters speech;
Every creature tells a message throughout the earth.
There is no speech nor are there words.
With romance, with passion,
Tenderly, truthfully,
They proclaim God's infinite beauty;
Reveal God's love dream, His longing, His will.
They declare the desire
And love of God.

Flowers bloom, birds sing in the green pastures;
On the mountain top, in fleeting colours of the dusk;
In the night sky lit by the silv'ry moon;
In light breeze and drizzle;
In the snow-flutt'ring morning; By the seashore
The rolling waves dashing against the rocks;
By the cliff with gushing waterfalls
Tenderly You whisper
Many words of love and show me Your wonders.
Now I have heard them all, and I turn to You.
I run to You, I embrace Your
Dream of love.

# The Father's Longing

May I oft' meditate
You've thought of me since eternity.
Abba, You think of me; You long for me.
O' I have captured Your heart.

2. May I always realize
Your desire for me is long and deep.
Day and night I lean on Your bosom.
I enjoy this fatherly love.

3. May I enter Your love,
Talk with You face to face more and more.
This is sweet moment of father and son.
We're content in embrace of love.

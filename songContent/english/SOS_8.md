# Song 1:5-7 Long to be By Your Side

I am very dark but I am lovely,
O' daughters of Jerusalem.
I am like the tents of Kedar. I'm like
The curtains of Solomon.
Do not gaze at me because I am dark,
For the sun has tanned me.
My mother’s sons were angry with me;
They made me keeper of the vineyards,
But my own vineyard I have not kept
Tell me, you whom my soul loves,
Where do you feed your flock? Tell me, tell me, my love,
Where do you make it rest at noon? Why should I
Be like one who veils herself beside the flocks
Of your companions, O’ my love?

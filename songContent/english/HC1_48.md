# Fly Above the Storm

I walk on the waves and see the storm.
The moon's hidden and the stars do not shine,
The howling wind overwhelms the song in the night.
And the waves crash against my stride.
I'm about to fall.
But I sing in my heart.
The light shines brighter within me.
I'm peaceful and calm, steadfast and firm.
From my past experience,
I know You have planned for this very moment.
O' Lord, let me hold You tight.
Let You lay a rich feast for me.
I've gained more of Your blessings and You build me up.
We won't forget all this experience.
I fly into the storm with a smile,
Over the waves.

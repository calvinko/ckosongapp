# Born of God

1. More than fearfully created, 
I was born again of God.
Now my life is joined to His life; 
such love bond satisfies me.
Born of God! Wonderful! 
Born of God! Wonderful!
He's my true eternal Abba! 
I'm His darling child, His joy!

2. Such love's miracle amazes me, 
manifests Your love and pow'r.
O such deepest tie and love bond 
satisfies Your heart and mine.
Born of God! Wonderful! 
Born of God! Wonderful!
I'm the fellow heir with Jesus; 
I'll inherit all with Christ,
Reign with Him forevermore. 
Wonderful! Wonderful!

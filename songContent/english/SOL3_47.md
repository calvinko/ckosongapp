# No More Condemnation! No More Separation

1. I treasure most You died for me,
Redeemed me to be Yours fore'er.
O the love scar of Your pierced side, You kept -
Your love pledge ever engraved.
Between us, Lord, there's no more sin,
Only You covenant of love!
You'll forever abide in my heart -
The deepest union of love!

In heaven, I sit with You - most accepted by God!
You dwell in my heart to be my glorious hope!
No more condemnation! No more separation!
We're ever joined as one.

2. Cov'nant of love, eternal love,
Binds us as one forevermore.
O the love scar of Your pierced side, You kept -
Your love pledge ever engraved.
Between us, Lord there's no more sin,
Only Your covenant of love!
Love and life are ever between us -
The deepest union forever!

In heaven, I sit with You - most accepted by God!
You dwell in my heart to be my glorious hope!
No more condemnation! No more separation!
We're ever joined as one.

3. With joy, I'll live with You Lord, in the endless spring,
Enjoy eternity and Your boundless love.
The Trinity embraces all His precious loved ones
In New Jerusalem -
God's boundlessness we'll enjoy!

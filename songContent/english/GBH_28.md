# I Can Face All with Jesus

I can face the world with Jesus.
I can face today and tomorrow
With the risen Lord!
Through storms, through life's billows,
Through the night, long dark valley,
Across the sea, over the mountain,
He leads me to glory.
He'll walk with me through my life-long journey
Till I see His face!
He'll carry me through my life-long journey
Till I see His face!

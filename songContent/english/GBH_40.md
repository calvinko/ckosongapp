# One Day the Lord Will Come

1. One day the Lord will come,
take me to the bright clouds!
That day He'll wipe away my tears,
make me exult in dance!

*O Lord, I love You.
My heart longs for You.
Gladly waiting for that day
to see Your glorious face!*

2. One day the Lord will come,
my body to transform
To be like His glorious body;
how free and bright I'll be.

*O Lord, I love You.
My heart longs for You.
Gladly waiting for that day
to see Your glorious face!*

3. One day the Lord will come,
fill me with rapturous joy.
I'll behold His glorious loving face.
'Tis my exceeding joy.

*O Lord, I love You.
My heart longs for You.
Gladly waiting for that day
to see Your glorious face!*

4. One day the Lord will come,
take us to our new home.
The palace He's prepared for us
is filled with His love design.

*O Lord, I love You.
My heart longs for You.
Gladly waiting for that day
to see Your glorious face!*

5. One day the Lord will come.
Pain, tears all pass away.
I'll drink full from the spring of joy,
enthrall in His embrace.

*Dear Lord, I love You.
O may You come soon.
Dancing, waiting for that day,
I'll see Your loving face!*

6. One day the Lord will come,
how sweet will be His praise.
Eternal reward I'll receive,
inherit all with Him.

*Dear Lord, I love You.
O may You come soon.
Dancing, waiting for that day,
I'll see Your loving face!*

7. One day the Lord will come,
give me the King's reward.
Forever I'll reign with my Beloved.
His domain will ever expand.

*Dear Lord, I love You.
O may You come soon.
Dancing, waiting for that day,
I'll see Your loving face!*

8. One day the Lord will come.
O love's eternity!
We'll dwell in New Jerusalem
amidst New Heaven and Earth.

*Dear Lord, I love You.
O may You come soon.
Dancing, waiting for that day,
I'll see Your loving face!*

9. How blessed! We are the core
of God's most glorious plan.
Forever we'll joy in His love plan.
How sweet His endless dream!

*Dear Lord, I love You.
O may You come soon.
Dancing, waiting for that day,
I'll see Your loving face!*

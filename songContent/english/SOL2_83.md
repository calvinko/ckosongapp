# Alone with the Father

I love to be alone with You.
Freely I talk to You.
Every morning Your love is new.
May You renew my heart.

2. Alone with You in our secret place 
This is our sweet moment.
O' Father of almighty pow'r,
My heart is unveiled to You.

3. Father, nothing I hide from You,
Open my heart to You.
You're full of mercies and comfort.
I pour out my heart to You.

4. As Your dear child, peacefully I rest
In Your bosom of love.
Fatherly love fills all my heart,
My sorrow, troubles are gone.

5. Alone with You, I am most set free,
In laughter or in tears.
Gladly I'll be away from friends,
To be with You only.

6. The Groom will soon gloriously appear,
Take me to Father's home.
With endless joy, I'll see Your face
In heaven, our eternal home.

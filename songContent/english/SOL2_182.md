# March Forward to a Glorious Journey

I have a dream heavenly vision.
Others have doubts, 
envy and disbelief.
Through the long, dark valley
He lit up my path.
He set me free 
from the dark dungeon.
We prosper in the land of affliction.
Glory and joy wipe away all our tears.
His dream and mine, 
glorious commission!
We will never forget!

2. He is the first; He is the last.
He gives me faith; 
He'll take care till the end.
I overflow with hope,
Singing all the way,
Same mind with Him, 
anchor the race of faith.
'Tis His glorious 
commission of this age.
What an honour to closely follow Him!
*Through suffering enter glory.
Joyfully I'll finish His will.*

3. Long, dark valley is over.
He will stretch out His mighty arm for us!
We passed through floods and fires
To the fruitful land.
Valleys of tears turned into springs of joy.
We prosper in the land of affliction.
Glory and joy wipe away all our tears.

His dream and mine, 
glorious commission,
Will soon be accomplished.
Glory and joy ever-increasing
Until we meet one day in the cloud.
The vict'ry won, my Lord, You will come!
O' my Beloved, may You come quickly!

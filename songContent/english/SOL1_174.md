# Your Love, Dream and Glory

You are fairer than heaven,
Beyond compare on earth.
All the stars in heaven
And the flowers on earth
Speak of Your glory,
Your beauty, dream and love.
My heart treasures your journey of love.
It warms my heart.
Your desire, Your flames of love to me
Is the myst'ry of this whole universe.
O' You are the Lamb who once was slain!
Joyful young stag! You're worthy of my heart.
O' You are a glorious young stag!
My true Beloved! We enjoy our union.

# The Banner of the Lamb Flies Over Me

The love of the Lamb has melted my heart.
He loved me and gave Himself for me.
He shed His blood to betroth me.
This lifeyielding love shakes the world!

2. To gain my heart He became the Lamb.
He passed through all suff'rings for me.
On the cruel cross He overcame death.
His banner over me is love.

3. The wedding feast, marriage of the Lamb,
Is my sweet hope, my earnest desire.
It is His love dream, eternal yearning 
Satisfies our hearts forever.

4. The Lamb's glory shines in the city.
In His love and light we're heart to heart.
The Lamb's enthroned in His kingdom of love.
We will reign with Him forever.

5. The steadfast love of the Lamb and His bride
Is full of newness, romance and warmth.
Fore'er we live in the river of joy.
Our love will increase more and more.

6. The love of the Lamb has shaken my heart.
He loved me and gave Himself for me.
He shed His blood to betroth me.
This love has stunned the Father's heart.

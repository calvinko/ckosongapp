# All The Way He’s With Me

1. All the journey when I retrace,
thankful tears oft fill my eyes;
Once was lonely, vile and worthless,
now I shine forth as the light.
Ne’ er forget the Lord’s appearing,
and His love as honey sweet,
Drawing me from worldly follies,
all my heart belongs to Him.
Drawing me from worldly follies,
all my heart belongs to Him.

2. All my life in trials abounding,
suffer wounds so long and deep.
Yet His love spurs and comforts me;
by His words my wounds are healed.
With His presence I am sustained,
my soul wings to higher place!
Strength upon strength as I walk on,
smile across all storms and waves!
Strength upon strength as I walk on,
smile across all storms and waves!

3. O dear Lord, can I not love You?
May my life be love response!
Pour out all my heart before You,
share my daily life with You.
Think of You, call upon Your name,
closely follow all the way.
Talk and smile to You every hour,
deeper friendship grows each day.
Talk and smile to You every hour,
deeper friendship grows each day.

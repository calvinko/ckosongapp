# I have Stolen My Lord's Heart

I have stolen my Lord's heart,
Day and night He thinks of me;
His thoughts of love toward me
Outnumber the sands or stars.

2. I have stolen my Lord's heart,
He'll gaze on me forever;
He sets His love forev'r on me,
His love draws me close to Him.

3. I have stolen my Lord's heart,
He shouts for joy over me,
His smile is so bright and warm,
His love captures my heart.

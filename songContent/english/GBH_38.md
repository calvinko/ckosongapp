# Live for Him and His Purpose

O Lord Beloved, I long to see You!
Dearest Lord, O may You come soon!
Now on the Earth I live for You,
Your commission to fulfill,
Strive for Your purpose, Your love dream.

2. O Lord Beloved, I long to see You!
Dearest Lord, O may You come soon!
Now on the Earth I live for You,
To bring millions to You,
And build the church after Your heart.

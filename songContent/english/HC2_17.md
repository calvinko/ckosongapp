# Psalm 66

Shout for joy to God, all the earth,
Sing the glory of Your name,
Make Your praise glorious, for Your great power
Your enemies surrender to You.
All the earth will worship You,
And will sing praises to You.
You turned the sea into a dry land,
They walked through the river and rejoiced.
O' God for You have put us to the test.
You have refined us as silver is tried,
Brought us into the net.
We went through flood and firs,
Yet You brought us to the abundant land.
I'll come into Your house
With burnt offerings.
To You I will perform my vows
Which I spoke in trouble.
Blessed be God who has not
Turned away my prayers
Nor His mercy from me.
He's always with me.

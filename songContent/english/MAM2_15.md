# Redeem My Time! Draw Near to God

No phones, no video games,
No screen, no wasting time.
I don't want to get distracted.
I don't want to be addicted.
Though there may be struggles,
Challenges and failures,
I want to endure and overcome.
Fix my eyes on the Lord,
Draw close to Him.
Redeem my time; my faith is strengthened.
Let us fight this battle together!
Drawn by You, my heart is kindled!
Full of passion, I am satisfied.
I have strength to face every day!

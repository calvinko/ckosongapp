# His Gaze of Love

Can a woman forget her nursing child?
Even these may forget, I won't forget you.
Behold, I've inscribed you on My palms.
Your adorable countenance's always before Me.

2. The mountains shall depart, the hills be removed;
But My kindness shall not depart from you.
All things may change. My love is the same.
My covenant of love is forever sure.

3. My child, I will never leave you, never forsake you.
Till the end of the age, I'm always with you.
I love you as the Father loves Me.
Day and night I watch over you, fix My eyes on you.

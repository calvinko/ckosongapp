# YAH, the Infinite One’s Heart is Reserved for Me

You are Jehovah - I AM WHO I AM,
Infinite, most wonderful and glorious.
Centre everywhere, You're overflowing;
Circumference nowhere, You are boundless and great.
How great! And holy! Who can compare with You?
Eternal! Infinite! You're worthy of my heart.
It's so precious Your heart is reserved for me.
I was creat'd for You. I am Your desire.
Wonderful! Beautiful is Your love to me.
I'm enthralled in this love. You hold me to Your heart.
I'm Your satisfaction, Your desire, Your love.
May You capture my heart. I'm Yours forever.

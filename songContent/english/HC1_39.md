# A Blissful Life

1. What a joy it is to walk
On the way of godliness,
Abiding within boundaries.
It's delightful and free.

2. I don't feel that I'm constrained
'Cause I'm walking in the truth.
It's not about regulations,
But arrangements that are good.

3. At the right time, in the right place,
I can do what I need to do.
I can do what I want to do.

4. Every step easy and light,
You and I live this sweet life.
Living to You, living with You,
I feel easy and light.

5. I am willing, more than willing.
Your will is what I have chosen.
Your way is my every decision.

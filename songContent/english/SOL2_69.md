# If the Lord Comes Today

My Lord Jesus when will You return?
How much longer we still have to wait?
The night is far spent. The dawn is at hand.
We know You will soon return!

*Chorus:
May be by today,
I'll see my Lord face to face!
May be by next day!
Let's adorn ourselves to watch and wait.*

2. 'Tis our only want for many years,
The hope of the saints who fell asleep,
And the one same cry through all the ages,
"Come quickly, O' Lord Jesus!"

*Chorus:
May be by today,
I'll see my Lord face to face!
May be by next day!
Let's adorn ourselves to watch and wait.*

3. Soon our home above will be ready.
The Son of Man will come in glory!
Let us build God's household after His heart,
Bearing love and unity!

*Chorus:
May be by today,
I'll see my Lord face to face!
May be by next day!
Let's adorn ourselves to watch and wait.*

# Serving in God's Household

1. The church is God's household.
All of us are joined by love.
Hello, big brother! Hello, big sister!
We are so close!
Hello, young brother! Hello, young sister!
You're so lovely.

2. The church is God's household.
We serve God with one heart.
You preach the gospel. I shepherd the young sheep.
We work together.
You share the truth. I give comfort.
We bless many.

3. The church is God's household.
The Lord oversees, guides the church.
O' Let us offer ourselves to bless
This family.
Let us get involved to satisfy
the Lord's heart.

# Walk with Me

I treasure Your calling!
You never forget Your promise.
Walk with me throughout my journey.
You are my closest friend.
My past journey was full of Your grace.
You have carried me through
The long dark valley.
No matter in north wind
Or in the south wind,
You've always walked with me,
Never left me.
In my future journey
I want to walk with You.
Lord, You will keep my heart
Until the end.

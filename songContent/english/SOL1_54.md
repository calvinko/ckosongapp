# My Dear Abba

1. God's beloved Son died for me
the veil was torn fore'er.
Abba ran out to embrace me,
covered me with kisses.
A robe and a ring He gave me.
A feast He prepared for me.
He celebrated. He sang and danced
I've returned home.

2. My Abba is self-existing,
wonderful, glorious.
He is all-wise, all-knowing,
almighty, all-loving.
He is the perfect Creator.
His goodness man can't comprehend.
He's my Father, my dear Abba,
my true Abba.

3. The Lord's Father is my Father;
I'm truly born of Him.
His seed fore'er abides in me.
I can be most like Him.
The Lord and I are of one.
I am Abba's dearest child,
Joint heir with Christ,
inherit all forever.

4. I'm the dearest child of Abba.
His Spirit dwells in me.
I am born of the Spirit;
I know I'm Abba's true child.
In Abba's love, my heart is free,
I know He sings, dances for me.
Let me enjoy the Father's love
throughout my life.

5. Abba spared not His only Son,
for me He gave Him up.
How shall He not freely give me
all things along with the Lord?!
He loves me as He loves the Lord;
He welcomes me, delights in me.
He wants me to sit at His right, to be glorious.

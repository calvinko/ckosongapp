# Strive for the Lord

I strive even among daily chores,
In quietness live for You;
I strive even life’s busy and tight,
Lord, I still walk with You.
I strive despite the world’s corrupt course,
I’m sanctified to You.
I strive on! O’ Lord, for You I live.

2. I strive even in doubts and worries;
Let You comfort my heart.
I strive mid the storms of temptation,
Lord, keep my heart sincere.
I strive in the days of affliction,
My love to You I give.
I strive on! O’ Lord, for You I live.

3. O’ Lord, I know that You love me,
I have captured Your heart!
How precious Your favour, tender love,
That’s why I’m striving still!
Forget not You chose, commissioned me;
I’m glad to know Your will.
I strive on! O’ Lord, for You I live.

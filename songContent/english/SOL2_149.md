# Stunning Personal Love

Were I the only sinner,
He would still come for me.
He'd endure the cross for me,
Drink the bitter cup for me.
He truly loved me,
Gave Himself for me.
His boundless personal love
Thrills and melts my heart.

2. O' my Lord's personal love stuns the whole universe.
As a seal, I'm on His heart, as a seal upon His arm.
I am my Beloved's; He desires me.
His fervent and gentle love fore'er embraces me.*

3. O' my Lord's personal love is my myrrh, my treasure.
His eternal, fervent love forever belongs to me.
A world of two surpasses all things.
When I'm in love with my Lord,
With Him, I bless the world.

O' Lord, I love You
Draw me close to You.
Make me the closest to You,
Respond to Your love.

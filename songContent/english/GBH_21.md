# On the Cross

1. On the cross, Jesus conquered all our foes!
On the cross, Jesus took away my sins!
O He gave Himself for me!
His love no one can fathom.
By the cross, God's great love is shown to me!

2. On the cross "It is finished!"He proclaimed!
By His death, Jesus shatters walls of sin!
O such great salvation!
Now I taste glorious freedom.
By the cross, God and I embrace in joy!

3. From the cross, dazzling light and hope I see!
By the cross, from a broken life I rise!
O tender light shines from the cross,
Cheers me through life to march on.
By the Lord, my life blooms anew with
joy! O A glorious life I own!
He's my Beloved, my best friend,
And He lives in my heart forevermore!

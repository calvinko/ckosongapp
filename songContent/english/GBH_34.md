# God's Sweet Home

1. The desire of God and man 
is the warmest loving home,
Full of fragrance of love, 
songs of joy everywhere!
This home of warmth and grace, 
overflows with rivers of love.
This home is God and man's 
eternal joy and resting place!
Home, sweet, sweet home!
The hearts of God and man 
embrace in oneness in His home.

2. Through all eternity, 
in God's plan, the core is "home".
O the Father, the Son 
and the Spirit gave all for this.
The Lord came down to Earth, 
and was nailed upon the cross.
God's heart was pierced through; 
He gave Him self without reserve!
Home, sweet, sweet home!
It's my vision, my goal, 
eternal joy, and my sweet rest.

3. God's dearest love dream, 
the hidden myst'ry of ages past
Was disclosed before me, 
I've gained the treasure of my life!
So precious, now I know 
the deep myst'ry of the universe!
I treasure this goal of life, 
'tis my hope and 'tis my dream!
Home, sweet, sweet home!
The hearts of God and man 
overflow with joy in His home!

4. Through all eternity, 
and throughout the universe,
It's only in this home, 
God and man rest satisfied!
The abundance of God's 
house is beyond our wildest dream!
God reveals His glorious heart 
in His lovely family.
Home, sweet, sweet home!
God's fullness, richest grace 
overflows from His love-filled home.

5. One day God's city 
descends to the beautiful New Earth!
Every tear He'll wipe away; 
no more mourning, death or pain.
The dwelling place of God 
will forever be with man.
This city of pure gold 
shines forth beams like jasper stone.
Home, sweet, sweet home!
This masterpiece of God, 
ever is the model of His love.

6. The abode of God and man 
is the city of pure gold.
It's adorned with precious stones, 
lovely pearls as"welcome gates".
Its lamp is the Lamb. 
We will reign eternally.
The city shines and glows 
in the New Heaven and New Earth.
Home, sweet, sweet home!
This ocean of God's love 
ever ripples with echoes of love!
There's no end to His love dream; 
we will reign forevermore.

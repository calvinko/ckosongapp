# My Eyes of Faith See His Heart

My eyes of faith see the Lord's heart
Engraved with my glorious name,
See His precious thoughts and longing toward me.
His countenance and His bosom
Are my joy, my satisfaction, perfect rest.

2. My eyes of faith see the Lord's arm
Sealed with my lovely name,
See His mighty and gracious hand uphold me.
His arms of love tightly hold me.
I feel so warm His embrace heals my heart.

3. My eyes of faith see the Father's
Smiling face, love and welcome.
He's waiting to embrace me with sweet kisses.
My Abba's love satisfies me.
His affection and love fill the universe.

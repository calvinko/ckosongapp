# The Day That You Will Crown Me

When I think of the day You will crown me,
Which is the day You come to take me,
I can't imagine how I'll be
Overwhelmed with joy.
In the presence of all, 
You'll show Your love for me.
Your deep love for me 
thrills heavens and earth.
All tears and sorrows will pass away.
You know well all my labor.
The wounds on me will change to be
Prints and marks of love.
O' my love for You,
No words can tell but You know well 
My heart feels sweet.
My dear Lord, for Your love,
I'll cast my crowns before You on that day.

# The Perfect Salvation

1. My sins more than I can number,
His blood washed me white as snow,
And He has purged my conscience.
Now I am free before God.
Precious blood redeemed me.
What can I pay Him back?
Let me enjoy Your great love,
Your perfect salvation.

2. I was away from the Father.
By His blood, He brought me back.
The veil was torn for me fore'er.
I can see the Father's face.
I dwell in the Holy Place,
My home's God's tabernacle.
God's perfect love casts out my fear.
And I cry, "Lord, may You come!"

3. My Lord's ascended to heaven
For me, appears before God.
In heaven I sit with Him,
Enjoy the Father's delight.
Closest to the Father,
Closest to the Father,
I am His dear child forever.
It's so sweet to cry, "Abba!"

4. The bitter way You walked for me.
You said, "Finished!" on the cross.
You laid down Your life for me.
Now I can embrace You.
Lord, let me see Your heart,
Move me to love You more.
I offer my life for You,
Forever I will love You.

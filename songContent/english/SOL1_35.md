# For Love, Lord, You Came

1. For love, You came from heaven to the earth.
You were born in a manger.
You became the son of a carpenter.
But for love, Lord, You came.

2. For love, You endured various temptations.
You had no place to lay Your head.
When You were weary, You thought of my needs.
And for love, Lord, You came.

3. For love, You're willing to bear all the shame.
You were spat on the face.
You bore all the stripes, wore the crown of thorns.
But for love, Lord, You came.

4. For love, You were willing to be nailed.
You bore my sins on the cross.
For me, You were forsaken by God.
But for love, Lord, You came.

5. For love, You've overcome all pains and trials.
Lord, I want to follow You.
May I love the world with You all my life.
For Your love I'll go forward.

# Abba Welcomes Us to the Heavenly Home

Abba, since ages ago, You have designed
And have built a city for us.
You have many beautiful thoughts and plans
to bring Your family together.
For the unique design for everyone,
For the arrangements for the whole,
You have the longest consideration,
The perfect planning, the complete blueprint.
You've fortast'd the joy of the very moment
When Your plan is done. You wait with gladness
To see how excited we are
When we enter the eternal home.
You'll see us flying around, here and there.
We'll touch and look around with amazement.
You'll see our eyes sparkling and shining
With exceeding joy!
We'll be in awe. We'll praise You of Your wonders.
We'll be happy. We'll sing and dance, praise more and more.
We'll all celebrate. Forever we'll celebrate.
Forever we'll celebrate.

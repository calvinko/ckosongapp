# The Father's Affection

Such affection
is my treasure of the universe.
Unsearchable!
It's the greatest miracle of God.
I'm God's creation, yet I'm born of Him.
His life and my life are closely united.
The boundless God is my Abba,
I am His dearest child.

2. Such affection
is the treasure of Abba and mine.
Fatherly love is what
He and I treasure most.
For His great love He needs me most.
He enjoys embracing me and kissing me.
In Your bosom, closely we walk
Upward journey of love.
Now and ever Abba and I
Walk on the path of love.

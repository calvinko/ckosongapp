# You are Here, Always Here

In my life journey,
You are here, always here.
I have no fear of
The long, dark valley.
I am not afraid of
Rugged roads, cold stormy night.

2. In my every day,
You are here, always here.
Maybe I'm occupied,
Maybe I'm heavy laden.
May be I am troubled,
Maybe I'm sleepless and restless.

3. At this moment
You are here, always here.
I'm alone by myself,
No one can understand.
I have Your company,
I have You, the One who knows me.

4. You are here, always here.
You've promised to be with me.
Your gentle countenance,
Your heart warming words,
You come close and embrace me.
I have no more fears.
You come close and embrace me.
I'm able to face all.

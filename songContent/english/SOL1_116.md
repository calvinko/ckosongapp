# The Flame of Jehovah

It's so precious! You are my Beloved!
Your love is so passionate, romantic!
Set me as a seal upon Your heart,
As a seal upon Your arm, O' Lord!
For love is strong as death!
For love is strong as death!
Jealousy is cruel as Sheol,
as cruel as Sheol,
The flashes there-of are flashes of fire.
A very flame of Jehovah!
A very flame of Jehovah!
Many waters cannot quench love,
Neither can floods drown it.
If a man would give all the substance
Of his house for love, it would utterly
Be contemned, be contemned.
Now You and I united forever!
Your dream of love comes true.
O' let me drink fully of Your love;
En-thralled in embrace and praise,
And set my love on You!

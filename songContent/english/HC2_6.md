# Your Love is Higher than the heaven

Deeper than the sea, high'r than the sky,
Before time began, with no end,
Is Your tender love. The stars bear witness.
The sand tells Your boundless love.

2. Deeper than the sea, high'r than the sky -
Your love covers all my guilts removes all my shadows.
I have no doubts and fears
I can rest in Your bosom.

3. Deeper than the sea, high'r than the sky -
Your love uplifts my failing heart.
You light up my life. With passion I will shine.
I will march forward bravely.

4. Deeper than the sea high'r than the sky -
Your love has conquered my heart.
It's my longing, desire, my joy, delight and rest,
My song and my sweet though.

5. Deeper than the sea, high'r than the sky -
Your love's banner flies over me.
You are my love, my life, my dream and my vision.
You are the center of my life.

6. Deeper than the sea, high'r than the sky -
Your love's better than all my dreams.
You are my true Beloved. Your love touches my heart.
I sing songs of love for You.

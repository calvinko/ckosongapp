# Glorious Freedom! I am Free from Sin!

I didn't want to be trapped,
But temptations closed me in.
I didn't want to fail and fall,
But I was very weak.
without physical chains,
I was bound by sins.
O' Please come to help me!
Please come to save me!
God always loves me;
He knows all my struggles.
A Saviour He sent to me,
Paid the ransom for me.
Hallelujah! I am out of bondage!
O' My Lord loves me! He came to save me!
No more load of sin! Now I am set free!
I'm out of the darkness into the marvelous light!

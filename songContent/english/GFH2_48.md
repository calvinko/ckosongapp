# Glorious 🌟

1. Glorious, glorious -
The hour I first believed.
I have been joined to the Lord,
In one spirit.

2. Wonderful! wonderful!
Now I am in Christ.
It’s no longer I who live,
But a new man.

Chorus:
The precious Lord lives in me.
Co-heir with Him of all things,
I partake His glory,
Father’s love and the Spirit.

4. Love that’s true, love that’s true,
What a love it is!
I want You to be my Lord,
Live in love with You eternally.

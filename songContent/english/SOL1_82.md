# The Bleeding Heart

In this world, none can compare;
Forever, none can exchange.
The Lord's my Beloved; I am His darling.
This love song
shall be sung fore'er.
Before time, He thought of me.
Full of hope, He created me
To be most like Him, be closest to Him.
Be in love with Him and share His all.

2. Who knows how high heaven is?
He loves me high'r than the sky!
From heaven to earth,
From His birth to death,
He loved me, gave Himself for me.
To seek me, He suffered much.
To save me, He passed through death.
The Lord has opened His bleeding heart to me,
His infinite love towards me.

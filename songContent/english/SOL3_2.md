# The Wedding Feast of the Lamb

At the Lamb's wedding feast,
Your manifold glorious light
Shines forth, surrounding me.
My heart is drawn by You!
The joy of that moment of love
Will last throughout eternity.
Such stunning love expression
Is revealed throughout all time!
The joyous wedding feast may pass
But love endures and grows.
And in a flash I see
The depths of Your heart.
I am amazed! Forever I'm amazed!
I'll keep discov'ring Your love fore'er.
We walk this path of love hand in hand,
Enthralled in this love river evermore!

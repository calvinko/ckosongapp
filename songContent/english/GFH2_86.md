# Echoes Of Love

1. My darling among the maidens
Is like a lily among the thorns.
My darling, beautiful one,
May you arise and come along.
Let me hear your voice,
Let me see your face.
For your voice is sweet, your voice is sweet,
And lovely is your face.
My sister, My bride,
A glance of your eyes steals My heart.
My sister, My bride,
A strand of your necklace steals My heart.
My darling is charming as Tirzah,
Lovely as Jerusalem,
Awesome as an army with banners.
You are all together lovely!

2. My Beloved among the young men,
‘s like an apple tree in the forest.
His banner over me is love,
His love has captured my heart.
My Beloved is dazzling and ruddy,
Outstanding among ten thousand.
I am my beloved’s, My beloved is mine,
And His desire is for me.
Set me as a seal,
Set me as a seal upon Your heart.
Set me as a seal,
Set me as a seal upon Your arm.
Until the cool of the day
When the shadows flee away,
Make haste, my Beloved, be like a gazelle,
On the mountains of Spices.

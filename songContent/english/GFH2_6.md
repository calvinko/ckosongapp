# The Love Song Of Heaven And Earth

Gazes upon the earth, the hea’en above,
Yet the earth has never cared.
But today, dear Lord, it’s so different now,
Through heaven and earth 
Your love is shown.

Looking up, run my tears, 
‘cause You’ve once left there,
And now again You are there.
Why should the heaven 
bow to kiss the earth?
Why should it shelter the earth?

If You were the clouds up high,
I could only be a small grass down on earth.
Yet the clouds turn to rain, showering down,
To nourish my whole life.

Never dared I to dream of the things above,
But today I am seated in heaven with You.
Tis too good to be true, 
yet this message from You,
Is the best that’s ever come true.

# I Will Love You, Not be Shy

May my love be fervent to You.
May I pour out my love to You.
I will lift up my hands.
I will sing and I will dance.
I'll joyfully proclaim: He is risen!
No more hiding, no more fear,
I don't mind how people feel.
Leap with joy, I feel so free.
Over and over I'll say it again:
I love You! I love You!
I will love You with my all, not be shy.
And with all my strength,
I'll tell everyone
You're wonderful. You're living with me.
I taste Your love; You're pleased with me 
What a happy life of love with my dear Lord!

# Strive for the Lord till the End

O' let us rejoice in God,
Walk the everlasting way,
Bring forth a lasting revival,
Accomplish God's glorious will.
Daily practice godliness,
Live to Him from morn to night.
Glory and joy ever increasing,
Offer aroma of love.

2. Never stray from the right way,
Pressing on towards the goal.
Ne'er deny the heavenly vision.
Finish the last beautiful race.
Let us all arise,
Strive for Him till the end.
Be faithful to the Lord unto death,
Till we meet Him in the clouds.
"I will strive for the Lord till the end!"

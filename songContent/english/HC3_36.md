# I Only Want to Do Your Will

I only want to do Your will.
I’m no longer bound by my own desires.
Though my past dreams never strayed from Your will,
Today I desire to give even more –
all that is within me.
I know You care for my thoughts and my feelings.
You want me to gain the greatest happiness.
This is Your romance, the outflow of Your love,
I embrace them all.
Yet today, I understand more,
And I promise to commit to You even more.
Your path, Your will,
Your plan, Your arrangement –
They are all good! All are perfect!

# I want You to love Me Only

1. I want You to love me only.
This is the longing of my heart.
This is the desire of love.
This is the need in my spirit.
May You kiss me with kisses,
Look at me and talk to me,
Kiss me, embrace me, come close to me.
May You lay down the universe,
And leave Your glorious throne.
O' Lord, please come to my place.
Let us enjoy the time of You and me.

2. "I want you to love Me only."
This is the calling of Your heart.
This is the longing of love.
Now I know I affect You the most.
Your love has captured my heart.
I have no other desires.
My heart and my all belong to You.
O' Lord, my gaze of love
Is set on You fore'er.
Let me go to Your bosom.
We will enjoy the love of You and me.

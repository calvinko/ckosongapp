# Full Acceptance, Love Forever

1. Full acceptance, love forever!
Ocean of love, O how vast!
Deeply I taste, fully I drink
God’s great love with no ending!

2. Full acceptance, love forever!
All my doubts and fears are gone.
In Your love there’s sweetness only.
With You I can smile and leap.

3. Full acceptance, love forever!
I can rejoice all my life.
South wind, north wind, spring or winter,
We embrace, love each other.

4. Full acceptance, love forever!
How beautiful is Your heart!
Who can sep’rate me from Your love?
We’re united, heart to heart!

5. Full acceptance, love forever!
My heart is drawn after You.
Nothing can be compared to You,
Can I not desire You?

6. Full acceptance, love forever!
My soul is healed and compelled.
I lift my head, my heart’s set free!
With You gladly onward go.

7. Full acceptance, love forever!
Your yoke is easy and light.
You walk with me, hold my hand tight.
We leap over depths and heights.

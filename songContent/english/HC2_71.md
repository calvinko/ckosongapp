# Abba, I am Your Little One

Abba, I'm Your dearest little one.
I'm lovely before You.
I am always pure and innocent.
I hold Your hand tight, Abba.
In everything I rely on You.
I look unto You.
I am boastful and I have no fears
By my Father - the giant!
We've gone through many. We have deep experience.
I know You more. I'm mindful of
My Abba's work. I give You ideas.
I tell You my thoughts, my plans.

Abba, I'm still Your little one.
I won't carry Your load.
I have perfect rest. I trust You fully.
Abba, I want Your big hand to hold my little hand.
We're always hand in hand.
I rely on You.
I fully trust You.

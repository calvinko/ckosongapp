# Let Us Go to Find Them

I've planned to go to a foreign land.
I've packed my bag myself.
I can't bring along the comfort from my home.
I'll have it when I return.
I have missed the fam'ly time. I'm alone imagining
How they would chat at the table.

I've said goodbye to my loved ones.
I go with their blessings.
I can't look back. I can't slow down. I turn
My eyes to those whom I don't know.
I consider their pain: their labour in the dust,
Being despised in the pile of waste.

It's not from my abundance, but out of my poverty.
I am not impulsive or showing off my strength
Ever day I look at God's heart, His compassion.
I see His heart and my heart is moved.
Let me love whom He loves. I'll go
And find the lost. I'll bring them home.
Let His children smile; let God rejoice.

# The Love of the Son of God

The love of the Son of God -
Is from eternity past.
I'm in His dream of love.
I'm His secret of love!
'Tis the love of God!
He's longed for this union.
In one spirit,
joined heart to heart as one,
In love with Him!
He and I walk on this path of love
With pink petals, in the river of love.
He is God! His love of true Beloved-
Beyond compare! Embrace me to His heart.
(I rest in His bosom.)

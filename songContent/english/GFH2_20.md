# Bitterness On The Cross

1. Behold! The sun gave not its light.
The earth was darkened and shook;
He left His Father’s face and died,
For me lowly and vile.

2. I view the cross from every side,
His blood, His wounds I meet:
His head, His face, His hands, His back,
His side, His trunk, His feet.

3. For me to understand His love,
He let His side be pierced;
Flowed blood and water proving this:
His heart‘s wounded for me!

4. Hear, on the cross the Lord’s first words:
"Father! Forgive them! Forgive!"
He died to pardon countless sins;
He died that we might live.

5. To the corners of the earth
I’ll tell The wondrous love of the cross.
Begone, the world’s riches and pride! I
lose them to gain Christ!

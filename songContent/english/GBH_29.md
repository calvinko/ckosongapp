# Living in My Heart

1. "To live in my heart" is Your sweet love dream,
Your love desire, Your longing of love.
"To live in my heart" manifests Your heart.
Your love to me is God's burning flame!
Love desires to be nearest forever.
Hearts offer hearts! Hearts live in hearts!

2. "To live in my heart" is Your ultimate choice.
You set Your love forever on me.
Tis Your love choice, Your eternal joy.
My heart is Your sweet dwelling place.
Tis Your fairest paradise, heaven!
Your fairest heart is my heaven, my home!

# Fully Trust and Obey

1. O' God, You created all things,
And they exist by Your will.
All things have their purpose.
All things are beautiful.
They're well designed by God.

2. O' God, You created all things,
Everything's beautiful in its time.
You've made all things in order.
Nothing happens by chance.
You make all work for Your will.

3. O' God, You've seen us from afar,
And You know our every thought.
Your love is personal;
You guide us with care.
You make all things work for our good.

4. O' God, strengthen our faith in You.
We don't argue; we have no doubts.
May we give thanks and obey;
We say "Yes" to Your plan,
And trust all You arrange for us.

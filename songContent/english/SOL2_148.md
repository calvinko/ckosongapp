# You Betrothed Me with Your Blood

Lord, You loved me, gave Yourself on the cross.
With Your precious blood I'm betrothed to You.
Death-daring love has melted my heart.
O' Lord, Your heart and mine are united.
Heart to heart we embrace in love fore'er.

2. Facing the cross, You saw the joy ahead:
You'll gain my heart; Your love dream will come true;
You'll meet me in the clouds and bring me home;
Enthralled in the wedding feast of the Lamb,
We'll drink in the endless river of love.

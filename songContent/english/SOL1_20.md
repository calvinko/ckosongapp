# One Day the Lord Will Come

1. One day the Lord will come,
To take me to the clouds;
That day He'll wipe away my tears,
I'll dance and sing aloud.

*Chorus:
O' Lord, I love You. 
My heart longs for You.
Gladly waiting for that day,
I'll see Your glorious face.*

2. One day the Lord will come,
To transform my body.
To be like His form of glory,
How wonderful and free!

*Chorus:
O' Lord, I love You. 
My heart longs for You.
Gladly waiting for that day,
I'll see Your glorious face.*

3. One day the Lord will come,
I'll marvel and rejoice.
To see my Lord's glorious face,
Shall be my spring of joy.

*Chorus:
O' Lord, I love You. 
My heart longs for You.
Gladly waiting for that day,
I'll see Your glorious face.*

4. One day the Lord will come,
Take me to Abba's home.
I'll see His wondrous love design,
For me the new abode.

*Chorus:
O' Lord, I love You. 
My heart longs for You.
Gladly waiting for that day,
I'll see Your glorious face.*

5. One day the Lord will come,
Gone all sorrows and tears!
I'll drink from the river of joy,
Enthralled in His love.

*Chorus:
O' Lord, I love You. 
My heart longs for You.
Gladly waiting for that day,
I'll see Your glorious face.*

6. One day the Lord will come,
Crowns, rewards to present.
We'll reign fore'er, His government
Will increase with no end.

*Chorus:
O' Lord, I love You. 
My heart longs for You.
Gladly waiting for that day,
I'll see Your glorious face.*

7. One day the Lord will come,
He is my hope so blest,
There'll be new heaven and new earth,
In which dwells righteousness.

*Chorus:
O' Lord, I love You. 
My heart longs for You.
Gladly waiting for that day,
I'll see Your glorious face.*

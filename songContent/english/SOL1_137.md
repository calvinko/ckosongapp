# His Love's Purpose

He died for us! He died for us,
That we can be forgiven,
That we may be set free from sin,
That we may enter heaven.

2. He died for us! He died for us,
To remove all barriers of sin;
Fore'er the veil was torn in two;
I dwell in my Abba's bosom.

3. He died for me! He died for me,
To cleanse me whiter than the snow,
So that I can unite with Him,
And that He may dwell in my heart.

4. He died for us! He died for us,
That whether we wake or sleep,
We should live together with Him,
We should live together with Him.

# Abba Shepherds Me Till Eternity

The Father in heaven is my true Abba,
I shall not want. I shall not want.
He makes me lean on His bosom.
He makes me rest in His love, His affection.
He comforts my heart. He restores my soul.
He leads me in the everlasting way.
For His name's sake, for His glorious will, He
Leads me in the finishing way.
Though I have walked through trails, floods and fires,
I fear no evil for You are with me.
Abba, Your big hand holds my little hand.
Your big hand always holds tight my little hand.
Your tender love and care warm my heart.
You prepare a feast before my enemies.
You make me rejoice in all afflictions.
You give me countless blessings.
I have endless gifts each day.
You give me the Spirit to nurture me,
To enjoy Your love, to be glorious,
To taste the Lord's love. My cup runs over.
Rivers of living water flow from my heart.
Every day of my life, Your mercy and love,
Your affection will follow me.
I will dwell in Your sweet home.
I'll reign with You forever.

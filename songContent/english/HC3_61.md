# Sing and Worship

I will sing to God my whole life.
I'll sing praises to Him while I live.
I think of His goodness daily.
I remember all His grace.
I will always number His mighty deeds,
His great work and wonders.
I'll consider His promises
And I'll respond to Him.

2. In the morning, I will praise Him.
I will give thanks and I will worship.
When I'm busy, I remember Him.
I will never forget,
I set the Lord always before me.
I know I am not alone.
O' let me always sing to Him.
Let me pray to Him.

3. From young to old, I praise Him.
I sing praises to Him everywhere.
When all things are easy and smooth,
I sing and give thanks to Him.
In all the storms and in the dark nights
I'll still sing and praise aloud.
I fully trust God is the best
And He rules over all.

4. I love Him. I'll sing songs to Him
Among the nations.
I will praise Him, exalt Him high!
I'll sing and worship Him
'Til the day I surround His throne.
Then I'll life up my praises to Him.

# The Tender Love and Footsteps of the Lamb

O my dear Lord, 
Your love to me is so precious.
You're infinite; 
who can tell Your glory?
Yet, Lord, You came 
with joy, leaping upon mountains;
Became a Man, 
You're born in a manger.
You chose to grow 
in Nazareth, a lowly town.
You're carpenter, 
daily facing wood and nails.
You've long decid'd 
to die for me on the cross.
Lord, I treasure 
Your beautiful journey on earth.
O Lord, besides You 
to whom would I belong?

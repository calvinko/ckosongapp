# On Mountains Of Bether

1. Lo, the winter is past; the rain is gone
Trials ended, flowers bloom on the earth.
The time of the singing of birds has come.
The turtledove’s voice has been heard!
The fig tree has ripened its figs;
Vines in blossom give forth fragrance.
O My darling, beautiful one,
Arise! arise! and come along,
And come along!

2. On Mountains of Bether I wait for You
Sojourn on earth, I long for Your return.
I lay aside all things to see Your face.
Apart from You, I’ve no desire.
Steadfast I’ll be, love You only.
Wait for You on Mountain “Cloven”.
Lord, I am Yours, and You are mine.
O Lord Jesus, I long for You! 
I long for You! I long for You!

*(Bether means cleaving or separation.)*

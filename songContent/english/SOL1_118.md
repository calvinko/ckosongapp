# Your Love Dream-To Live in My Heart

O' Lord, You are more real than all things.
You're infinite, ever o'erflowing.
You and I are deeply united.
One spirit, heart to heart.
For Your endless love,
O' Lord, You need me most.
Your dream of love - to live in my heart,
Live with me day and night.

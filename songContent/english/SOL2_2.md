# Love Journey of the Lord from Infant to Adult

He became flesh, dwelt among men.
He's full of grace and full of truth.
His glory shone in this dark world.
He is so good; He is all fair.

2. My gracious Lord sojourned on earth.
He had no place to lay His head.
He is the light shines in darkness,
Righteous among the unrighteous.

3. Man of sorrows, for all His life,
He was reviled, acquaint'd with grief.
My Lord humbled Himself and walked
On the path of self-denial.

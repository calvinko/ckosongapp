# The Love That Stuns My Heart

Heaven and earth tell me of Your love.
Without a word they declare Your deep love,
Reveal Your love, glory, beauty
So softly, in harmony.
You gladly came from heav'n to earth.
To come close to me, You became a Man.
You sought me, saved me from all miseries,
Put me on Your shoulder, set me on Your heart.
Your love is flashes of fire, perfect from the beginning.
Your love journey moves my heart. How beautiful - Your footsteps!
Your unreserved love for me was proven on the cross,
With Your pierced side, Your many wounds.

2. Love unto death shakes heavens and earth.
It stuns my heart; I'm conquered by Your love.
Banner of love flies over me.
I am Yours. You have gained my heart.
The song of songs is Your love song,
Telling me of Your deepest love for me.
My Lord, You saved me by Your precious blood,
Betrothed me to be Your darling forever.
I'm Your creation of love. I exist because of You.
Since eternity past, You've been thinking about me.
Your love story has been told from creation till today.
Your proof of love is everywhere.

O' Lord, I want to know more Your forever loving heart,
The fullness of creation, Your love journey on earth,
Profound knowledge of the cross, Your desire for me today,
Your eternal sweet dream of love.
O' Lord, may I live in the depth of Your loving heart,
Be Your dearest, perfect darling!

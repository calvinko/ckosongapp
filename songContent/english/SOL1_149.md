# Living in God's Circle of Love

The eternal dance of Abba, the Lord,
And the Spirit sweet, full of love.
They invite me and long for me to join
Their eternal dance of love.
I'm so blessed to take part in this,
To be embraced by Them all.
Daily You light fireworks,
cause all stars to dance,
Celebrate our deepest love.
(Join in our eternal dance.)

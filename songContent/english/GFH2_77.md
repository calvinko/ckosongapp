# Jesus Is My Portion

1. Jesus gives me living water,
He’s my dew and my joy.
He is the bread of life that fills my hunger;
Jesus is my rock, my fortress,
my refuge and my shield.
He is my inner strength, my ready Helper.
He’s my life, transforms me
from glory to glory.
He’s my glorious boastful hope of this last age.

*Chorus:
Jesus is the Bright Morning Star,
the henna in the yard;
I am His, He is mine, I love Him ever.*

2. Jesus is my Mediator,
who pleads and intercedes.
He’s my High Priest who can sympathize and bless;
Jesus is my King who subdues
my heart’s adversaries.
He is the King of kings. His foes will perish.
All will be put under Him;
All knees’ll bow to Him.
The universe will be filled with His vict’ry.

# Gaze of Love, Ever Active Thoughts

Abba, You're more real than all. You surpass all things.
You're sitting on the throne and surrounding me.
Abba You're closest to me. You dwell in my heart.
I treasure day and night You attend to me.
From eternity past You needed me most.
You earnestly desire me to be Your dearest child,
Joyfully share with me this deepest affection!
Your ever active love and thoughts gain my heart.

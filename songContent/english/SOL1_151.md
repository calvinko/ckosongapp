# The Utmost of My Abba

From eternity past
when there's no beginning
To eternity which has no end,
You're thinking about me,
You've given me the best:
You gave me Your only beloved Son.
You even forsook Him on the cross.
You once suffered the greatest pain.
Your heart was pierced through
with many arrows -
I enter the holy place.

You want me to enter the depth of Your heart;
In one spirit I am joined with You -
I am Your dearest one,
You love me as the Lord.
I enjoy Your highest Father's love.
Now I taste Your deepest affection.
I'm born of You, dearest to You.
I am most glorious, the most blessed one.
You want me to be like the Lord.

Abba, You're overwhelmed with joy for me
Because You've gained
what You have long desired.
Out of Your boundless love,
You really need me most.
You truly need me the most.
Your love is wholehearted, committed.
Because You are infinite,
Your love to me is the most personal.
I give thanks to You, my Abba.
I truly affect You the most!

# Forever a Man Emmanuel

1. Long long ago, You knew Your path of suffering,
And You'd be raised on the bitterest cross.
But glorious Lord, You never had a second thought;
For love You longed to be a Man for me.
My dearest Lord, so precious with joy You have come!
Forev'r a Man, so deep You comfort my heart,
Shatter my fears and my feeling of distance.
You yearn to be closest to me and live in me;
And now You live with joy forever in my heart!

2. Forev'r a Man, You make me know my true worth,
And I'm the seal, the dearest dove on Your heart!
Born as a Child, You make Heaven and Earth wonder,
Myriads of angels behold, adore and praise!
My dearest Lord, so precious with joy You have come!
"Forev'r a Man" sealed Your love's deep commitment.
Your fervent love comforts my soul, warms my heart.
May I forev'r enjoy Your presence and love.
O Emmanuel forev'r You're worthy of my heart!

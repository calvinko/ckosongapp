# The Heart of the Loving Father

Not even once do You reject me
When I return to You.
As long as I'm willing to return,
Flee from my sin; turn to the light.
You will run to me, embrace me,
And kiss me with kisses.
Quickly You take out the best for me to enjoy.
You hold me, treasure me;
Carry me and dance with joy.
This is the heart of the heav'nly Father,
The bosom of God,
The loving kindness of God
It's beyond all man's dreams!

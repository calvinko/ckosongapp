# The Victorious Love

The greatest suffering - You despised.
The strongest pain - You overcame.
Being chastised by the Father - You endured.
The lonliest time - You went through.
And then You cried, "It is finished!"
The veil was rent. Your love dream has come true!
You've come to my heart. You embrace me.
Banner of love flutters over me.
I am Yours! I am Yours!
You have gained me now and forever.

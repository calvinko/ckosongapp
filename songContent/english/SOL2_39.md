# One Sacrifice for Sin Forever

Jesus Christ as the High Priest
Had offered one sacrifice
For sins forever and He sat down
At the right hand of God, at His right.
From that time He's waiting, He's waiting
Until His enemies are made His footstool.

2. For by one offering,
For by one offering,
He has perfected forever, forever
All those who are being sanctified.
The Spirit also testifies to us,
"I'll remember no more their sins and lawless deeds."

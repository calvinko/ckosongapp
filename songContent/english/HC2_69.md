# Abba and Me

Abba and me, I feel so sweet
When I call You again and again.
The past experiences tell me how good You are.
Don't need to search anymore.
In this moment I know I am before You
And that You are before me.
Now I don't need to recall past memories.
I deeply believe You are my Abba.
Now my Abba, I come near to You.

# His Bloodsealed Love

O' Lord, I treasure You shed Your blood.
This love melts my heart, makes me amazed.
It warms my heart, draws me close to You.
I live in Your bosom of love.

2. O' Lord, I treasure You shed Your blood.
You've kept the nail prints - the wounds of love.
Your love's ever fresh, forever the same.
'Tis my eternal rest and joy.

3. O' Lord, I treasure You shed Your blood.
Your love overcame all pain and death.
We are united; I belong to You.
As a seal I am on Your heart.

4. O' Lord, I treasure You shed Your blood.
Your true love for me is proven.
This is my rest, the rock of my faith.
I freely embrace Your love.

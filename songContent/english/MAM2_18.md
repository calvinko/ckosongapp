# The Calling to My Faithful Youth

My dear child,
Let no one despise your youth,
For I have called you
Personally.
I treasure your youth.
I raise you up
In this generation,
For I've chosen you
To take up the task.
Rise up, take up, strive on.
Although you are young,
You are not weak.
Be courageous,
Go where I've sent you.
I'm with you and for you.
Now pick up the baton
And run with endurance.
Keep the faith and finish the race.

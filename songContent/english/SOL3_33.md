# You Fervently Desire to Have Your Supper With Us

1. You desire to eat the Passover with
Your disciples that night.
Now You eagerly desire to
Break the bread with us.
You've revealed Your heart to us
And Your manifold love!
O the Spirit leads us to
Worship and remember You.

2. Blood You shed, and "Finished!" You declared.
This new cov'nant You've sealed.
Far exceeding time and space is Your
Glorious vict'ry of love!
O the Marriage of the Lamb
Is our longing of love.
We will toast and celebrate
In the kingdom of God!

3. Let us celebrate and let's enjoy
Your great vict'ry of love!
Till eternity, I'll treasure most
You're my true Beloved.
Brothers, sisters, raise a toast
With this blessed cup,
Sweetly taste the covenant,
Love Him all our lives!

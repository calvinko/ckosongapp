# Stay Before the Lord

Stay before Him, in the light of His face.
In His presence enjoy the good portion.
Draw near to Him - It is your daily need.
Let nothing take away your good part.

Chorus:
Stay before Him. See His glory.
The Holy Spirit will mould you.
Draw near to Him; He'll draw near to you.
I long to be with Him always.

2. Stay before Him, in the light of His face.
Receive His words, let His love fill your heart.
You're drawn by Him, away from all your cares.
Draw near to Him, enjoy a world of two.

3. Stay before Him, in the light of His face.
Pour out your heart and sing love songs to Him.
He's your best friend. Tell Him your sorrow and joy.
Live in sweet love with Him and share your all.

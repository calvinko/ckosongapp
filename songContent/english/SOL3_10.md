# I Did Not Appear By Chance

I did not appear by chance. I exist for You!
I am reserved for You.
Since time of old, I am Your love dream,
Your first and only love, and Your eternal love!
How precious You're my all knowing
Beloved of manifold love!
And I am Your eternal love,
Your most loved, well-suited bride!
Your matchless, fairest love, eternal love
Is forever mine!
You, the glorious, boundless Lord, belong to me!
You've committed Yourself
Forever to Your darling love!

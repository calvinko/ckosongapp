# The Road of Faith

The road of faith gets brighter as I walk on.
The road of faith gets more joyous and glorious.
The road of faith reveals the Lord's preciousness.
The road of faith draws me closer to the Lord.

*Chorus:

By faith I overcome the world.
By faith I never thirst.
Living water flows from my heart.*

# No Hardship is Greater than the Lord

What are you, O' great mountain?
You shall become a plain!
All the enemies and
Giants will become our food.
There are storms and waves,
But we'll rise above them all.
Even in a furnace,
The Lord will walk with us.
Is there any challenge
Greater than the Lord?
There's no situation
He cannot control.
Nor are. there enemies
He can't make use of.
Is there any weakness
He cannot strengthen?
There are storms and waves,
But we'll rise above them all.
Even in a furnace,
The Lord will walk with us.

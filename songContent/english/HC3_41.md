# In Quietness, I'm Before You

I don't rush; I'm not anxious.
In quietness, I'm before You.
It feels like an eternity
Though it's just a moment.
I lay aside all;
With all my heart I stay in Your presence.
I will stay, I will stay.
I will stay and wait.
Let the radiance of heaven
Surround me and fill me.
I am enthralled in Your delight
And I am drawn by You beauty

# The Father Has Given Me a Comforter

The Lord asked the Father for us,
And He gave us a Helper.
The Spirit of truth comes and
Lives in our hearts forever.
We enjoy the Trinity living in us.
We walk this journey together.

2. The Lord asked the Father for us,
And He gave us a Helper.
He reminds us of the Lord's words,
Brings us closer to the Lord.
Many gifts He gives us; strengthens us.
We work together for the Lord.

3. The Lord asked the Father for us.
He gave us a Comforter.
Gently He calls in our hearts;
He frees us from the snare.
He teaches and guides us to walk the right path.
We bear the fruits of righteousness.

4. Thank the Lord for seeking the best for us.
Thanks Abba for the Comforter.
Thank the Holy Spirit for living and
Staying with us forever.

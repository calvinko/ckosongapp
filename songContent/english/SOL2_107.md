# The Lord's Love Dream; His Gaze of Love

My dear Lord, You're above all, more real than all things.
In heav'n You live for me and You surround me;
You even live in my heart, You're closest to me.
I treasure day and night You're gazing on me.
I'm amazed from long ago I've been Your love dream.
In the universe, You want me to be Your dearest bride!
With You, I live a life of sweetness and beauty,
Your love thoughts towards me are ever active.

# The Unique, Self-existing, Infinite and Incomparable Lord

You're boundless, self-existing.
Your glory I'm amazed.
You're the only holy One,
who can compare with You?
Only You're always with me,
love me with all Your heart.
You always attend to me.
Your love thoughts more than stars.

2. Only You have desired me
since eternity past.
Your thoughts of love towards me-
ever active and fresh.
Your love is infinitely
personal towards me.
You make all work together;
Nothing can distract You.

3. Lord, Your love never changes,
it's forever steadfast.
I forever treasure Your desire towards me.
Your joy and affection overflow like rivers.
Your fervent, beautiful love
Makes me desire, adore.
(Draws my heart towards You.)

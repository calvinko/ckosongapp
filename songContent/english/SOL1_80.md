# Wholehearted Love

It's so precious! I am Your perfect one;
I'm Your treasure, Your dearest only one.
You love me with Your whole heart and mind;
You have set Your love on me for-ever!
In Your admiring eyes,
I'm fair, without a flaw.
I have captured Your heart;
You love to think of me,
You leap for joy and sing ov'r me.
I am Your living spring,
A garden fountain,
Gladden Your heart, give You great joy.
O north wind, o south wind, blows on my heart,
Spreads its fragrance of love to delight You.
In love with You, like a sweet dream!
May my whole life and eternity
Live with You in deep love,
Living out the 'Love Song' with You,
Enthralled in the joy of our love!

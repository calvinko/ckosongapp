# My Father is Infinitely Great

When I see all the stars,
I think of Your glory.
You're the endless fountain,
Overflowing with love.
May Your glory ever capture my heart.
I desire nothing besides You.
Let me behold Your beauty
And I will be drawn away.

You are the boundless One,
Greater than the universe.
All things in this world
Have become minor to me.
Your vast bosom of love is my rest.
Let me always stay in Your embrace.
O' how precious I am Your dearest child,
O' how sweet!

Father, my heart loves You!
I long to know You more,
Fully drink of Your love,
Daily rejoice before You.

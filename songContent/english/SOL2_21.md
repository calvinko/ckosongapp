# What Manner of Love

Behold, behold what manner of love
Abba has bestowed on us,
That we should be called children of God!
And we are children of God!

2. Therefore the world does not know us,
Because it did not know Him.
Therefore the world does not know us,
Because it did not know Him.

3. Beloved, now we are children of God,
What we shall be has not shown.
But we know that when He is revealed,
We shall be like Him, like Him.

4. For we shall see Him as He is,
And everyone who has
This hope in Him purifies himself
Just as the Lord is pure.

# Abba, You are Committed to My Life

My Abba, not only did You give me a playground
That is full of fun and wonder,
But You play with me. We’re so happy.
We are happy together.
My Abba, not only did You plan for my life -
It’s such a unique story,
But You’re committed. You walk with me every step.
You’re always guiding me.
You give me light, a helping hand.
We walk through every valley and climb every mountain.
You won’t go away or leave me
After providing for all my needs.
You treasure to be my eternal Abba,
To live with me now and forever.
You long for the fam’ly joy, and we can be heart to heart.
Your commitment has made
This deepest affection come true.

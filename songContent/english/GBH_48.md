# The Vision of New Jerusalem

1. New Jerusalem! This most glorious vision
Captures my heart, 'tis my dream, drive and joy.
Sweetest union, He's the Lover of my soul;
His love fully satisfies my heart.
I'm God's dearest, born of Him. 
He's my dearest Abba;
His highest love satisfies my heart.

2. New Jerusalem is God's tabernacle,
And our fairest eternal sweet home.
It has jasper walls, 
adorned with precious stones;
The gates are pearls, the street is gold.
There the river of life flows, 
pure and clear as crystal;
On the riversides are the trees of life.

3. New Jerusalem is God's tabernacle,
His eternal sweetest resting place,
And our fairest, warmest heavenly home.
God, and man dwell in love, satisfied!
Live in oneness with our God 
and with one another;
We share the sweetest eternity!

4. New Jerusalem is the Bride of the Lamb,
His love's masterpiece 
shines forth evermore.
The deep union of love 
between Christ and His church
Is most moving! All will call us blessed!
On the throne we'll reign with Christ, 
glory ever increasing.
O most colourful eternity!

5. Abba yearns for me 
to be His most glorious child,
To inherit all with His beloved Son.
I'm His darling child, 
His dream, glory and joy;
His infinite love's my sweet rest.
Father and son's mutual love 
satisfies His heart and mine.
Forever we will wonder at His love plan!

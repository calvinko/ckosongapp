# Anytime, Anywhere

The years have past. As I look back,
This child has grown, able to carry his very own,
Or so he thinks. He's still so small,
Needs to be carried by the Father of all.
Abba Father, You've always been here.
Though at times, I wasn't aware.
You treasure my smiles; You collect all my tears.
Never have You missed a single day,
Anytime, anywhere.
Hold me in Your arms. Draw me ever near.
Press me to Your heart. I need Your tender care.
You treasure my smiles;
You collect all my tears.
Never do I want to miss any days,
Anytime, anywhere.

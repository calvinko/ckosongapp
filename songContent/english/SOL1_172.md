# I'm so Precious in Your Heart, O' Lord

I'm so precious in Your heart, O' Lord.
You do not just call me Your friend.
You want me to be Your darling.
Forever and ever
You desire me.
For me You came from heav'n.
You longed to be a Man.
My glorious Lord,
You passed through death for me.
You love me. You're willing
To die for me.
You were chastised and forsaken by God.
You bore all shame for me and gave Your life.
On the cross, You opened Your heart.
Freely I live
In Your deepest love.

# Born to be Dearest

I treasure Your beautiful heart, Abba.
I am more than Your best creation;
I'm born of You! Dearest, closest to You.
I enjoy with the Lord
Deepest love, highest affection of Yours.

2. Now I can inherit all with the Lord.
He is not ashamed to call me brethren,
Because I was born of You, born of You.
I am dearest to You.
My Abba, Your work is so wonderful.
(My Abba, Your work satisfies my heart.)

3. My Abba, I treasure Your wondrous work.
Your seed forever abides in me.
So I can bear pleasant fruit for You -
Infinitely glorious!
Shining ever brighter till perfect day.

4. I treasure Your beautiful heart, Abba -
Eternal love thoughts, deepest affection,
Full attention, love from the beginning.
Your thoughts exceeding all stars,
Gaze of love and Your delight towards me.

5. Every day joyfully I go to You,
Run to Your open arms, bosom of love.
Sweetly I taste this deepest affection,
Most beautiful, highest love.
Leaping with joy, I embrace You.

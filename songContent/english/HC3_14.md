# Every Day I Pray to God

1. Everyday I pray to God.
I entrust my day to Him.
In the morning I direct my pr'yers to You.
May You provide what I need,
Give me sufficient grace.

Chorus:
The Father knows our needs. He loves us.
He will listen, listen, listen to our pr'yers.
The Lord asked us to pray in His name.
He will fill us; He will greatly fill us.

2. Every day I pray to God.
I ask for Your protection.
May You save me from all evil and dangers,
Keep my going out, coming in,
To prosper in all things.

3. Every day I pray to God.
May You send mighty angels
To encamp around me and may You help me
Overcome all temptations,
Save me from accusations.

4. Every day I pray to God.
May You give me wisdom,
Help me to be diligent and faithful,
Give us the same heart and mind
To bring forth revival.

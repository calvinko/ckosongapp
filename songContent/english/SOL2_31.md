# He is the God Who Gives Me Hope

I put my faith in the glorious God.
He's able to raise the dead the Lord of life.
I put my faith in the glorious God.
He can make all from nothing the Creator!

2. He's full of life, He's full of might.
He gives life to my spirit when I am down.
He has the pow'r of resurrection.
He gives strength to my spirit when I am weak.

3. He is the God who gives me hope.
He's my song, He is my strength. He makes me smile.
He is the God who gives me joy.
He has turned my mourning into dancing.

4. In the wilderness, He lays a feast.
He can make rivers flow in the desert.
I have hope in all circumstances.
Ha! Ha! Ha! Almighty God is my Abba!

5. O' my brethren, lift up your head.
Your Abba, almighty God, loves you the most.
O' my brethren, march on forward!
Your Abba, almighty God, holds your hand.
(Your Abba, almighty God, carries you.)

# Abba's Personal Love for Me

My Abba's personal love
For me is high and deep.
He would gladly send His Son
If it were only for me.

*(Chorus)
Your love thoughts for me 
who can comprehend?
Now You are my dear Abba! 
Deep affection we share.*

2. If it were only for me,
He would prepare the Lamb 
By the blood of His Son,
He washed me whiter than snow.

*(Chorus)
Your love thoughts for me 
who can comprehend?
Now You are my dear Abba! 
Deep affection we share.*

 3. Abba, Your personal love
For me is high and deep.
You forsook Your beloved Son
To gain me as Your dear child.
I'm in Your bosom of love.

*(Chorus)
Your love thoughts for me 
who can comprehend?
Now You are my dear Abba! 
Deep affection we share.*

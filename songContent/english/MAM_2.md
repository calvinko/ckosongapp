# By Him, I'll Do My Best

I know sin is not good. I don't want to sin.
I have received Jesus
And become a new creation.
Old things have passed away. All things have become new.
I will walk with the Lord. By Him I'll do my best.

2. It is best to love God, to be close to Him,
And I love what God loves.
I want to follow His word.
Now is better. Life is wonderful with Him.
I'm a new creation. I enjoy heav'nly blessings.

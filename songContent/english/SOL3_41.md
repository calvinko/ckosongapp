# Your Perfect Rainbow-Like Love

1. Most Precious, Lord, Your love of the cross,
And Your scar of love on Your side,
Reveal stunningly that I'm on Your heart -
I'm Your bone of bones, dearest to Your heart!
Your love's fairer than the rainbow;
You're the perfect ladder, rainbow bridge.
You have perfectly bridged heaven and earth,
Now You and I are closely tied as one!
O the rainbow arches over the land -
Your love covers me, surrounds me all my life.

2. The perfect seven hues of the bow -
Lord, You are the First and the Last;
From red to purple You're responsible,
Until I sit on the throne with You!
I will reign with You forever!
We'll enjoy our rainbow-like love path.
Such an endless spring, fair eternity!
I'll enjoy Your dream fore'er realized on me!
Lord, You and Your love are ever mine!

3. The rainbow is the sign of Your pledge -
No flood will destroy the Earth again.
The rainbow declares Your affections deep,
And more bountiful, vibrant is Your love!
'Round the throne today is a rainbow!
O it welcomes me to the throne of grace!
You're my precious Lord, Beloved of manifold love!
O Your love is fair! It can't fully be expressed!
Yet You, Your whole self, and Your love
Are ever mine!

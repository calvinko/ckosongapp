# Lord Jesus, I Love You

1. Lord Jesus, I love You! 
Lord, You died for me.
You made me white as snow, 
Your heart's fairest jewel.
You brought me from darkness
Into Your marvelous light.
You have changed my life.
I'm forever Yours!
You're forever mine!

2. Lord Jesus, I love You! 
Lord, You died for me.
You're my life. 
You're my song, sweetest portion.
You're my guide, my good shepherd,
My strong rock, my rest.
You're my hope, my morning star,
My Beloved, my all!
You're forever mine!

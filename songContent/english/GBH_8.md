# Who Affects the Father Most

I am amazed I affect You most!
You've thought of me from eternity past.
For me, You sent Your beloved Son from Heaven,
And He gave Himself for me on the cross.
Abba, Your beautiful heart
Was pierced through with arrows!
O glorious! The Lord cried out,
"It is finished!"
The veil was rent; You ran to me,
Embraced me to Your heart, covered me with kisses,
Rested on my neck; You're overjoyed!
Your love dream's gloriously fulfilled!
La La La La La La La La La La La La La La La La La
You prepared a rich feast; You sang and danced!
You shouted for joy over me. Your heart was filled;
Embracing me, You're ravished in love!
I am amazed: infinite You are,
Out of love, You need me most!
Yearning for me to be close to You,
Hand in hand You and I live in love.

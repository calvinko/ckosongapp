# He Came From Heaven To Earth

1. God’s beloved Son came from heaven to earth,
His name is Jesus,
Dwelt among men with glory,
Full of grace, full of truth.
From heaven to earth He came to bring me
Back to the Father’s fold.

2. God’s beloved Son came from heaven to earth,
Was in a manger born.
He bore all sorrows for me.
He walked to the cross for me.
God’s beloved Son came from heaven to earth;
He gave Himself for me.

3. God’s beloved Son came from heaven to earth,
To fight battles for me.
Destroyed the works of Satan,
He took away all my sins.
From heaven to earth He came to bring me
Out of death into life.

4. God’s beloved Son came from heaven to earth,
Forever He is man.
He is so kind and friendly.
What love and warmth He gives me!
God’s beloved Son came from heaven to earth,
His great love melts my heart.

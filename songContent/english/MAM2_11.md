# Lord, May You Come

O' my Lord, my Beloved, may You return.
May You come and receive me.
In the clouds, see Your face. In Your arms, we celebrate!
O' come, let us celebrate!
O' brothers and sisters, let us rejoice,
Let's dance and celebrate!
Let us join hand in hand. Let us sing new songs
To welcome this new stage!
La La La La La La La La La La La
La La La La La La La

# The Glorious Lord of Life

Who would have envisioned 
the bleak and barren trees
Can blossom with such beautiful flowers,
Cov'ring branches with stunning colours.
O' the resurrection pow'r!
O' the infinite power of life!

Flowers blooming with smiles 
celebrate You're risen.
You raised me up together with You.
You have become my glorious life.
As You live, so I will also live.
In You, my Lord, there's no dead end.
But only man's unbelief.
Once I was dead, but You raised me up.
Fore'er I'm Your glorious new creation.

I am Your dearest darling, Your beautiful lily.
God's seed abides in my heart forever.
I partake in His divine nature.
Much more, Lord, You abide in my heart.
You make my life shine with glory,
Singing with sweet melodies.
You bring my heart into beautiful spring.
Lord, in Your love I am forever young.

In this cold and dark world 
there may be pain and grief.
Yet, by Your love, I see beyond dark clouds.
My heart's gladdened, 
nourished and sweetened.
Tender love wipes away all my tears.
Lord, in Your love, my path is bright.
I leap with joy like rising sun.
Live in Your love, we walk hand in hand
On this glorious and brilliant path of faith.

When I am left without hope, Lord, 
You will give me hope.
O' my dear Lord, with You I'll march forward.
Then I will fly to Your bosom.
You'll meet me with Your embrace,
Your embrace of love, Your smiling face.

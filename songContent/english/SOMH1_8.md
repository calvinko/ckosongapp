# Love Till the End

1. When your hair has turned grey,
When you're no longer strong,
I will never forsake you;
I will not depart from you.
I live for you, always by your side.
Till the end, I will love you.
My love shall follow you.

2. When you're tired and without strength,
I sympathize with you.
When you lie on your sickbed,
I sustain you, restore you.
Each step of the way shows my tender love.
Till the end I'll be with you;
sufficient is My grace.
My dear child, as your days,
so shall your strength be.

3. When you walk through dark valleys,
I'm with you, hold you tight.
I've triumphed Satan and death;
Fear not, My darling.
I died for you, bought you to be mine.
When you're awake or asleep,
I'm with you always.
To be with Me far better than all.
I am your eternal joy;
Your Beloved and best Friend.

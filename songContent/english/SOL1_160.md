# The Deepest Affection

It's so precious
You are my dear Abba.
I am Your dearest child, dearest child.
What we treasure most
is this deep affection,
We enjoy this affection forever.
Sweetly You and I embrace
In this highest love.
'Tis Your sweet dream of love
through all Eternity-
Brings forth the most wonderful plan.
For You, I was creat'd,
Born again. I'm for You.
Your love, Your heart reserved for me.
How glorious!
Your love dream has now come true!
I am in Your bosom, Your beloved one,
And I'm joint heir with Christ.
With my dear Lord, I inherit all,
Sit with You on the throne!
You love me as You love the Lord!
It's my eternal sweet rest!

# As the Heavens Are High Above the Earth (You Desire Mercy, Not Sacrifice)

As the heavens are high above the earth,
As the heavens are high above the earth,
So great is His mercy, His lovingkindness
For those who fear Him!
As far as the east is from the west,
As far as the east is from the west,
So far has He removed
Our transgressions from us.
As a father shows compassion to His children,
Yah-weh shows compassion to those who fear Him.
For He knows our frame, our form;
He remembers that we are but dust.
He desires mercy, not sacrifice.
His tender mercies will never fail.
Yes they are new, new every morning!
Your mercies, Lord, endure fore'er!
Beloved Lord, You love's so tender.
Your eternity and mine
are bound as one forevermore!
And You and I are joined as one!
Lord, Your mercy, compassion and Your love
Will never end!
The depths of Your heart is my rest!

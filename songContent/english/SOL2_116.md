# The Fruit of the Cross

The Lamb of God bled and died;
The veil was rent fore'er!
The wall of sins was shattered.
God took away all my sins.
I am perfect, whiter than snow.
A living way He has opened
To the Father,
I'm part of God's circle of love!

2. I've entered God's circle of love:
They're always by my side
Sweet fellowship, love interflow,
Love of the Trinity God.
I enjoy most this fellowship:
The Father, the Son, the Spirit
I cherish most God's precious name.
He's my portion! (He's fore'er mine!)

# The Longing of Love

My darling, my beautiful one,
Arise and come along with me.
The winter's past, the rain's over and gone,
Flowers appear, the time of singing has come.
The voice of turtle dove is heard.
The fig tree puts forth her green figs.
And the vines with the tender grapes.
My love, my fair one, arise and come away.

2. My Beloved's mine and I am His,
He feeds His flock among the lilies.
Turn, my Beloved, until the day breaks
And the shadows flee away, flee away.
Turn, my Beloved, be like a gazelle,
Like a young stag on the mountains of Bether.
Turn, my Beloved, be like a gazelle,
Like a young stag on the mountains of Bether.

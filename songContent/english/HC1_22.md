# O' Lord, May You Come

I don't want any fightings, any death,
I don't want any hunger, disasters.
Lord, may You come back quickly
And bring peace to the earth.

2. I don't want any sorrows, any pains,
I don't want to cry and say goodbye.
Lord, may You come back quickly
And bring love to the earth.

3. Come O' Lord, come O' Lord,
Come to take us to meet You.
Come O' Lord, come O' Lord,
Bring to the earth the eternal bliss.

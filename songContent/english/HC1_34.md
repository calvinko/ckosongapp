# We Come to Remember the Lord

1. The Lord said, "Do this in remembrance of Me."
On that night, He commanded us
That we would not forget, we would not forget
The Lord was born for us,
He died for us,
He suffered for us, He gave His all.
We don't forget that He pours out all His love for us.

2. The Lord said, "Do this in remembrance of Me."
On that night, earnestly the Lord
Desired us to always remember
His salvation grace, His salvation grace.
We remember His heart is always for us.
We remember one day we'll meet the Lord in the clouds!

# My Heavenly Home

1. My heav’nly home, full of warmth and restful peace,
Pearls as the gates, wall of jasper, street of gold,
Precious stones laid, and the Lamb as lamp shining.
There’s no longer night; River of life flows forever.
See His face, taste His love, My heart overflows with joy.
With all the saints I’ll gladly sing and reign together!

2. ‘The First’, ‘the Last’ - my Lord is ever-living.
‘Alpha’, ‘Omega’ - He will bring me to glory;
The good Shepherd’ll guide me to the River of Life.
In rest eternal, we shall taste the sweet fruit of life.
In the bright morning light, know His love in depth and height,
Hear echoes of love ringing in the City of Life.

Come, O Lord! Come, O Lord!
Take the church into the clouds.
Dark is the night,
May Your light shine forth upon all land.

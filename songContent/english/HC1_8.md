# God Wrote Me a Long Love Letter

O' God, You wrote me this long love letter
To tell me Your love so very deep.
O' God, You wrote me this long love letter
To show me Your grace so very rich.

2. O' God, You wrote me this long love letter
To give me Your light in my heart.
O' God, You wrote me this long love letter
To brighten all my life journey.

3. O' God, I come, I come to You
To enjoy Your precious word.
Draw me nearer, nearer to You.
O' God, I love You!

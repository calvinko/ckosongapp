# I am Your Sweet Love Dream

From everlasting to everlasting,
I'm Your deepest desire, sweet dream of love.
For me You became a man forever.
You gladly chose to be the Son of Man.
Lord, for me You were hung on the tree.
O' Your love for me stuns the universe.
You longed to closely unite with me.
Hand in hand we walk on this path of love.
Together we will share this love eternity.
I am Your first love, eternal love.
I've captured Your heart; You love me fore'er
Please quickly return, O' my Beloved.
Full of romance we'll meet in the clouds.
Fly to the place You have prepared for me.
Fly to the place You have prepared for us.
New Jerusalem! New Jerusalem!

# This is the Day That the Lord Has Made

This is the day that the Lord has made.
We will rejoice, be glad in it.
This is the day that the Lord has made.
We will rejoice, be glad in it.

2. His mercy and lovingkindness
Are new and fresh every morning.
Love and mercy will follow me today,
And my Beloved will be with me.

3. He holds authority for me.
He causes all things work for me.
No good thing will He withhold from me.
I have a good inheritance.

4. Sufficient for the day - its own trouble.
Do not worry for tomorrow.
Enjoy every day, every stage, every step.
Enjoy, admire your whole journey.

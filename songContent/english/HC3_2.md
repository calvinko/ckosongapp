# With Love We Shall Overcome

1. We shall overcome;
We shall overcome;
We shall overcome all things stopping us.
O' by the Lord, we do believe
We shall overcome all things.

2. We shall always march forward;
We shall always march forward;
We shall fly across the highest mountains.
For the love of the Lord compels us all.
We shall always march forward.

3. We shall love one another;
We shall love one another;
We shall love in unity forever.
For the love of the Lord compels us all.
We shall love one another.

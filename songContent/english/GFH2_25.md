# Deep Deep Love Of The Lord

Lord Jesus, Your love‘s so deep;
I can never measure all.
For my sake, You suffered,
Gave Yourself and bore my sins.
Rugged road, You hold my hand;
In troubles, You relieve me.
I should rest inside Your love,
Let Your love fill me deeply.
I know You did everything for me.
I should love You more than anything.
In north wind or south wind,
I’ll still sing and praise You.
Only You can fill my heart.
May the whole world taste Your grace.

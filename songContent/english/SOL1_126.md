# My Intimate Friend

What a Friend I have in You,
Faithful and true.
If men are faithless,
Your love is always sure.
Whenever I need You,
You give Your hand
to carry me through.
Even men do wrongs to You,
The wounds You still can endure.
Your love makes me lift my head.
Your grace makes me walk ahead.
Having such a Friend like You,
All my life I'm content.
You accompany me to the end,
Never leave me, my intimate Friend.

# Come to Reign, Lord 👑

1. And I behold new heaven, new earth
And I behold new heaven, new earth
Ah...... Ah......
I behold new Jerusalem,
Out of heav’n coming down.
Things of sorrows have passed away,
Have all passed away.
No more death on earth thus shall be found,
No more mourning on earth shall remain.
Christ our Lord will forever reign,
New heaven, new earth He brings!
Ah...... Jesus Christ,
You’re the City’s glorious light;
You are the King of kings.

2. O King of kings, O King of kings,
You are worthy to reign.
For great is Your salvation pow’r!
Your loving-kindness is vast.
E’en a sinner You change to a prince;
In the desert, You make rivers.
What a glory - Love of the cross
Set all the captives free!
Ah...... Jesus Christ,
Heaven and earth moved by Your grace,
Give forth their songs of praise.

3. O our dear King, O our dear Lord,
You are the King of peace.
As the mountain is Your justice,
Your love is like the deep sea.
All the nations will be blessed by You.
All peoples will yield their hearts to You.
To us You are ever-loving.
In wonder angels sing!
Ah...... Jesus Christ,
*May You come soon, May You come soon,
We long to see You reign.*

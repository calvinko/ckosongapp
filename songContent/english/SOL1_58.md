# I have Captured My Father's Heart

1. I've captured my Father's heart,
Day and night He thinks of me;
His thoughts of love toward me
Outnumber the sands or stars.

2. I've captured my Father's heart,
He'll gaze on me forever.
His eternal love and care
Draw me close to His bosom.

3. Abba, Father's heart is mine,
He leaps for joy over me;
His smile is so bright and warm,
In His bosom I rejoice.

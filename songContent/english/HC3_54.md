# My Heart is Consumed

Since long ago I've decided to live for You,
But today I heard You saying, "My heart is consumed."
My heart's heavy within me.
O' Lord, what can I do for You?
At this moment, I want to feel what You feel—
Let Your zeal consume my heart.
What are You looking at, Lord? Who is on Your mind?
Who is pressing on Your mind?
Now what do You want to say?
O' Lord, I am here. Please say it one more time.
I want to hear Your voice, to know Your heart.

There are needs everywhere.
There is work everywhere.
I can give myself for You anywhere,
And work for You anytime.
But Lord, what is consuming You?
I'm here, and I wait on You.
I'm here for You. I'll stay with You.

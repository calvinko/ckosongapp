# THe Most Beautiful Return

1. May I return
To the place in Your side
To fill the space reserved
For me in Your heart.
As Your bone of bones,
In the blessing of love I dwell!
By day, by night,
I sweetly live with You.
Without You, Lord, I drift astray.
I will return to Your love!
I'll be anchored firm in Your love.
Deeply I drink of You!

2. In Your presence and love I dwell.
You've reserved Your heart for me.
You have kept the place
In Your side for me.
I'm Your most loved one.
I'm the bone of Your bones.
I'm Your only love,
Your ultimate darling love,
The love secret deep in Your heart!
Without You, Lord I wither dry.
I will return to Your love!
In Your love I rest, anchored firm,
My heart overflows!
'Tis the fairest love return
To the depths of Your heart!

# I Never Dreamed of

I never dreamed that I would become
The true child of Abba Father!
I once sinned and strayed away from God,
But now I'm closest to the Father.
I cry out "Abba, Father" everyday
I enjoy His love and care everyday.

2. I never dreamed that Abba would
Design for me a glorious life.
He uplifts me and He molds me.
He makes me as perfect as He is.
I come close to Abba everyday.
I enjoy His guidance everyday.

3. I never dreamed that Abba would
Give me a joyful eternity.
I will reign with the Lord one day.
I will inherit all with the Lord.
I will worship Abba everyday.
I will lead all to praise Him everyday.

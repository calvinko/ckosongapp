# What I have Gained is so Beautiful

What I have gained is so beautiful,
More than I can ever dream of,
More than I can obtain in this world;
I cannot exchange it with anything.
Your affection has shown.
You have gladly bestowed
So that I may know -

What I have gained is so beautiful,
More than all the things You have made.
The sky is clear and blue.
The clouds tell your message,
Much more is Your personal love.
You partake in my life,
Light up all my days.

What I have gained is so beautiful.
I am no longer a slave to sin,
But my heavenly Father's dear child,
And you walk with me in this life journey
Through suff'ring to glory.
Understand Your heart, Your desire for me,
Enjoy the abode with You and the Father,
All these make me dance with cheer.

What I have gained is so beautiful.
One day I'll live in the heav'nly place;
All tears, sorrows will pass away.
Every day I can see You face to face,
Live in eternal sweetness and grace.
O' Lord, having You, all are so beautiful!

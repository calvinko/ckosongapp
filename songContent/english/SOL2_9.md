# My Eyes of Faith Behold

My eyes of faith behold
The Lamb once slain for me 
Covered with wounds!
You suffered much for me;
You long to see my face.
I should love You always,
Cherish Your face!

2. My eyes of faith behold
The risen, good Shepherd 
With mighty pow'r!
You're with me all my days
Through trials, flood and flames.
I will be strong and brave, rejoice always.

3. My eyes of faith behold
My Beloved in glory 
With love and grace!
You set me on Your heart,
Think of me day and night.
In Your bosom I'll stay, rest with delight.

4. My eyes of faith behold
My Bridegroom coming soon 
Full of glory!
You'll take me by Yourself,
To be with You always.
I'll watch and wait for Your glorious coming!

# Precious Blood!!!

1. Precious blood perfected me!
I'm the precious jewel of God!
Father gave me to His Son
As a seal set on His heart.
I'm all fair and beautiful!
They delight in me the most!
God's great glory is revealed!

2. Precious blood perfected me!
I've been made a precious stone!
There's no flaw, no spot in me -
I'm compatible with God!
I'm the Father's dearest child!
I'm the Lord's dear darling love!

3. Precious blood perfected me!
I've been made a precious stone!
Accusations overcome,
A victor'ous one am I!
I can lift my head up high,
Overcome the foes, the world!

4. Precious blood perfected me!
I approach the throne of grace!
I behold His glory bright -
I'm transformed with face unveiled!
Glor'ous vict'ry of the cross -
Now a conqueror am I!
His love dream let us fulfill!

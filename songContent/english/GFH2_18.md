# The Cross Of Golgotha

1. O the cross of Golgotha
Tells the love of my Saviour, how deep!
Down from heaven full of wealth and glory,
You did come, shed Your blood for me.
Foxes have holes, even birds of the air have nests, 
Yet You had no place to lay Your head.

2. O the cross of Golgotha
Tells the love of my Saviour, how rich!
Suffered all the shame and scoffs so sorely,
Gave Yourself, bore the stripes for me.
The crown of thorns, the wounded hands and feet and side -
I am set free by Your sacrifice!

3. Think and think - O Lord, Your love undying!
Ne’er its depth can I realize!
Gazing upon the cross - Your arms are open wide,
May I come and consecrate my life.

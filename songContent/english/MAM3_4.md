# The Lovely Family of God

1. The church is the family of God.
We all have a part in it.
God is our true Abba.
The Lord is our dear Brother.
We were once strangers,
Now we're one family. How sweet!

2. The church is the family of God.
We love one another.
We meet in the Lord's name.
He is greatly with us.
He supplies all our needs.
He has promised to bless us.

Chorus:
This is the warmest home.
This is the sweetest home.
This is the most joyful home.
This is the best home.

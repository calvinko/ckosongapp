# I'm Brought Forth Most Deeply From You

1. I'm brought forth dearly and deeply from You,
From the depths of your heart's desire.
Since eternity I've been Your love dream!
I am always in Your love thoughts.
Since eternity I've been on Your heart!
You have longed to gain me as Your own!

2. I appeared from Your deep love longing.
I appeared from Your beautiful heart.
I appeared from Your flaming desire!
I appeared from Your dream of love,
From Your boundless and fairest thoughts!

3. I appeared from Your love, pow’r and wisdom.
I appeared from Your wounded side.
I’m the flesh of Your flesh, bone of Your bones.
I return to the place in Your side.
I am Your well-matched darling love.

4. We most deeply belong to each other
In our union of manifold love.
O we share such a vibrant life union!
I am always Your joy ahead.
’Tis a love path with no end!

5. I'm most deeply brought forth from Your heart.
I belong most deeply to You!
Without me there’s a missing piece in Your heart.
You embrace me into Your heart.
You love me, need me boundlessly.
I am Your greatest joy forever!
'Tis an endless romantic path
Of sweet and eternal love!

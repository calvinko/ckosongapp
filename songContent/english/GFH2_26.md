# Your Love So Touching

1. Lord, Your love‘s so true! 
For me You suffered all sneers and sores.
For me You bore the cross! 
For me You abide in my heart. 
For me You ever live! 
I know for sure this love is true, 
Not just brief mercy You give.

2. Lord, Your love‘s so deep, 
Gives me abundant life of glory, 
Like a charming melody. 
Thru’ days, thru’ months, thru’ all the years, 
Brings forth enchanting hymns. 
When I reach home, may I repeat 
ev’ry song of love at Your feet.

# I was Created for the Lord

With Your infinite love,
Wisdom, and pow'r,
You made me to be
Your best creation:
Compatible with You,
In harmony with You.
Each other we delight,
I'm in love with You.

2. My Lord, for Yourself, 
You creat'd me
Compatible with You, 
I'm Your closest one.
One spirit I'm with You, 
most deeply united,
Heart to heart we belong, 
I'm Yours, You are mine.

3. My Lord, for Yourself, 
You creat'd me
To enjoy Your great love, 
satisfy Your heart.
Day and night I'm with You, 
in love with You fore'er,
Hold sovereignty with You till eternity.

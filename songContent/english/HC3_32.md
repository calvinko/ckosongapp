# I Mind My Father's Business

Father, I grow in Your love and grace.
Today I understand Your will better.
Abba Father, I've grown.
I am mindful of Your business.
I am mindful of Your business.
What You've entrust'd me, faithfully, I'll finish.
I still have my journey in this life -
Every step I will walk with You.
Let the One who bore me rejoice.
Let me be Your glory.
O' Let me be Your crown.

2. Father, I grow in Your teaching and way.
I don't just seek for my own pleasure and feeling,
I don't ask for smooth days;
Things don't have to go my way.
I can fly above storms and waves.
Beyond the dark clouds I can see the blue sky.
I walk firmly on the rugged roads.
I don't need answers for everything.
I won't delay the work of my hand.
I will mind Your business
Until the last days.
I will mind Your business
Until the last days!

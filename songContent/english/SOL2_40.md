# The Great Love of the Father

1. Abba, Father is rich in mercy.
Because of His great love for me,
When I was dead in my transgressions,
He made me live with Christ my Lord,
And raised me up together with Him,
Seat'd me in heaven with Him;
And raised me up together with Him,
Seat'd me in heaven with Him.

2. Abba, Father's boundless love for me
Forever is my rest and peace.
He loves the Lord, the same He loves me,
Without reserve, wholeheartedly.
Abba Father, open my eyes,
And let me see Your true heart.
I'll be carefree; I'll live joyfully,
Rest in Your bosom of love.

# My Lover in Glory

In Glory my Lover, my best Friend, 
holds all authority!
He's the beloved Son of God, 
my glorious Saviour!
For me, He came from Heaven to Earth, 
as a Man forever.
He loved me so much that He gave Himself 
on the cruel cross for me.
But He has risen from the dead 
and ascended to Heaven!
O one day, from Glory He will come back
To bring me to His most wonderful home.
Now in Heaven, He lives for me; 
from dawn to dusk He's thinking of me.
O I belong to Him; His desire is toward me.
His fairest heart, His strong arms, all are mine!
How I love Him! He's my treasure, He's my portion,
Incomparable!!!

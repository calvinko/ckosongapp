# The Lord is My Life

1. Lord I enjoy every day You're my life.
In all circumstances I enjoy Your supply,
And my heart is quiet, peaceful, strengthened, steadfast.
I'm not troubled, nor anxious.

2. I've never thought that it is so easy,
Free and light hearted to live a life of victory.
I don't need to frown, struggle, nor feel distressed.
Under Your wings, I shout for joy. I rejoice in
All the days You've made.
There was a time when I felt
I was rushing day after day.
Now I can walk in a wide place,
Rejoice in Him, happy and free -
Walk with the Lord in a wide place,
Rejoice in Him, happy and free.

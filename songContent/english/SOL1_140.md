# It's the Lord, the Author and Finisher, Who Commissioned Us

It's so precious the glorious Lord
Commissioned us in this age!
Your grace is sufficient for me,
You'll grant me the Holy Spirit!
You'll be with us! You'll co-work with us,
Followed with Your miracles.
Revive Your work, accomplish Your will.
There will be a glorious bride waiting for You!

2. You in my life, my hope overflows,
I fear no valleys, floods or fires.
You are my Beloved and Shepherd,
You've fully committed Yourself;
You are the First, You are the Last,
You'll take care until the end.
O' Lord, I love You, deeply love Your church.
Faithfully I'll follow You till the end.
(May Your bride love You
and welcome Your coming!)

# The Love in the Beginning

O' the Life in the beginning,
Is so boundless, glorious!
O' the love in the beginning,
Colorful, like a rainbow!
O' Creator, You're self-exist,
An everflowing fountain!
From eternity, Your heart is sweet
With a secret of love.
You long that I, Your created one,
May unite with You as one
In love's deepest union.
With Your infinite love,
Wisdom and power,
You create me for Yourself
To be Your most compatible, dearest lover,
To fully enjoy Your glorious life,
Drink fully of Your passionate,
Romantic, rainbow-like love!

Hallelujah! Hallelujah!
Your fantastic dream of stunning love
From eternity has now come true!
O' the upward journey
Of this love life with You
Is so charming, scenic, like a dream!
O' how sweet to walk with You in deep love!
This carpet of love, paved with pink petals,
And the river of love that You drink with me,
Will have no end, increasing in glory,
Bliss and sweetness forever!

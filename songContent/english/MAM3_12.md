# Heaven is a Good Place

1. Heaven is a good place.
My dear Lord has prepared for me
A beautiful city, a beautiful home.
La la la la la la la la,
God and I will live forever together.

2. Heaven is a good place.
Each one is shining in this royal family.
Having sweet fellowship, we praise one another.
La la la la la la la la,
You and I will live in love together.

3. Heaven is a good place.
Every day is rich and abundant:
Endless discoveries, new activities.
La la la la la la la la,
God and we will have lots of fun together.
No eye has seen, no ear has heard,
No mind has conceived,
The most beautiful heaven,
The home of you and me!

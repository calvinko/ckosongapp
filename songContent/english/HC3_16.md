# Fight the Battle of Faith Every Day

1. O' God, though I cannot see You,
I believe You are here.
You are the almighty Creator.
You are in charge of all things.
You will reward all those Who seek You.

2. O' God, though I cannot see You,
I believe in Your words.
Every day I think about Your promises.
I remember You're with me.
I call unto You, and
You will answer me.

3. There are lies from the enemies,
And the trials of the world
Cover me, but I turn ot You.
I know You won't leave me.
You stretch out You hand and
Save me to the end.

4. Every day I come to You
And I trust in You.
Every day I talk to You
And enjoy You.
I do not look at the troubles,
I fix my eyes on You.
My faith rises and
My glory rejoices.

# Living in the Father's Delight

You sent Your Son to be a Man
To die for me on the cross,
So I can live in Your bosom.
Now I know You delight in me.

2. The veil was torn, and You dashed out,
Embraced me to Your heart.
You kissed me, rejoiced for me.
Now I know You delight in me.

3. Abba Father, You have inscribed
My name fore'er on Your palm.
I am Your beloved son.
Now I know You delight in me.

4. You meet me with Your mercy.
You help me with Your count'nace.
Your thoughts for me exceed the stars.
Now I know You delight in me.

5. You gave me to Your beloved Son
As Your most precious gift.
I am the Lord's dearest darling.
Now I know You delight in me.

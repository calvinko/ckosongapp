# Mountain of Myrrh, Hill of Frankincense

To the Mountain of Myrrh,
I will taste death for you;
To the Hill of Frankincense,
I'll pray for you, bless you.
Until the day breaks,
The shadows flee away,
I will return and take you
To our sweet home above.

2. Lord Jesus, I love You,
On earth I live for You.
On this path of suffering,
I will never turn back.
Oh my Beloved,
May "the day" soon come!
When the shadows flee away,
You will receive me home!

3. Hurry my Beloved,
Leaping like a gazelle,
On the mountains of spices.
You will come soon, come soon.
My bright morning Star,
The night is far spent.
May You return from the blue,
Take me into glory.

# This is My Father’s World

1. This is My Father’s world!
I can be free of worries.
Dancing, singing as a bird of cheer,
praising His watching over me.
This is My Father’s world,
I can sing Ha! Ha! Ha! Ha!
My name has been set upon His heart,
I am the apple of His eye.

2. My Lord has conquered Satan,
He has overcome the world.
He is risen, taken to heaven,
As head over all things for me.
Ha! Ha! Ha! Ha! Ha! Ha!
He has won the victory!
Ha! Ha! Ha! Ha! Ha! Ha! Ha! Ha!
My Brother holds authority!

# The Love Song of the Father's Heart

1. My dear child, do you know
How joyous I'm for you?!
Your countenance and your every move
Shines like a precious stone,
Dazzling, lovely.

*Chorus:
I love to embrace you,
'Tis My pleasure and joy,
For you I dance and sing
The love song of My heart.*

2. My dear child, do you know
How content I'm with you?
Now and fore'er nothing in this world
Can ever replace you in My heart.

*Chorus:
I love to embrace you,
'Tis My pleasure and joy,
For you I dance and sing
The love song of My heart.*

3. The days ahead are joyous,
Come, fully enjoy My all.
You can freely lean on My bosom.
I'll give you eternal, greatest blessings.

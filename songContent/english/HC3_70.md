# You Long to be My Abba

Eternal God, You long to be my Father.
Before all came into being,
When there was not yet any parent or family,
Your passionate Father's heart was beating fast.
You always want us to be Your dearest children,
You long for Father and son time.
You long for us to run to You, to call You,
To hold Your hand and dance with You,
To enjoy Your all.
You long for us to understand Your great plan.
For this, You created all.
Though men did not understand
And men failed and were weak,
You still went forward with Your plan.
You poured out Your all,
And You suffered all pains.
When the Lord cried out, "It is finished!"
You tore the veil and You dashed out
To embrace Your children.
You proclaimed God's dream has come true!
Hallelujah!

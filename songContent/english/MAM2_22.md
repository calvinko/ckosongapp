# The Trinity and I

1. Before all creations,
God has been in control.
Before time, before space,
God has been working.
His love, His power,
His goodness are always there.
O' He is the
Wonderful Creator.

2. Before all, there is love
Amid the Trinity.
They make plans, They have fellowship,
They have the best design.
He made men, He made all,
He made God's family appear. 
O' This is God's
Beautiful love plan.

3. O' Father, I give thanks
I am Your dearest child.
Lord, thank You, You have brought me
Back to the Father.
The Spirit has come.
Now we can embrace with God.
O' We have the
Deepest union with God.

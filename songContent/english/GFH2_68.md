# In God’s Household 

In God’s household lived many saints. 
How excellent they are!
United in love and honesty,
Never split. (never split)
For the love of Christ has melted me,
That I know what love should be,
Lost in brothers’ and sisters’
Loving streams. (loving streams)
Let’s join hands to walk the journey,
Building up ourselves in love;
Stand up in unity, satisfy Him.

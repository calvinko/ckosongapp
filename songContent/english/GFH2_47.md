# Union With The Lord

1. O Father on Your right there’s the Son of Man!
He’s my Brother, my Beloved, my best Friend.
I even can become fellow-heir with Christ!
Who can comprehend my riches and joy?

2. Union with Him is my greatest blessing;
Freed from the sphere and yoke of slavery!
Before God, I am a new creation in Christ,
In His glory and light ever abide.

3. Union with Him is my greatest blessing;
I now belong to my Beloved alone.
All thru’ this life, I enjoy His company;
In love live with Him through eternity.

# The Manifold Love of the True Beloved

Before all creation,
You are my true Beloved.
The rainbow-like love
Has been hidden in Your heart.
Lord, You set Your colourful,
Romantic love on me.
Without me, You are in want.
Lord, I am Your bone of bones, flesh of flesh.
Your love stirs up my love.
Your affection kindles mine.
Filling with echoes of love,
As the deep calls unto deep!

2. Fearfully, wonderfully,
I was created.
Lord, Your love will transform me,
Uplift me unceasingly.
You made me in Your image
To be Your dearest darling,
Heart to heart, in harmony.
O' Lord, I'm Your greatest delight, contentment.
My glory will forever increase
Ravish Your heart.
Our sweetness will ever grow. We live in
The endless river of love!

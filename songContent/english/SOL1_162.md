# Rest and Rejoice in Your Bosom

I'm the dearest one in Your bosom.
You love me as You love the Lord.
My beautiful smile gladdens Your heart.
Every moment You treasure.
My Abba, You enjoy loving me.
Your hand holds my little hand.
My heart's content in this affection and love,
Rest 'nd rejoice in Your bosom.

2. Day and night You long to see my face.
You love to hear my sweet voice.
Abba, my joy is also Your joy.
You want me to enjoy all.
You meet me with Your love and Your smile.
Your gentleness makes me great.
You desire to give me infinite blessings,
Bring me into glory.

3. Abba, I treasure this deep affection
And You are my dear Abba.
Calling You "Abba"
makes my heart feel sweet.
Your bosom is my rest.
This affection precious, warm and sweet.
You make all stars dance with You.
I rejoice in Your circle of love,
And eternal dance of love.
(Dance of the Royal family!)

Daily You light fireworks,
cause all stars to dance,
Celebrate our union!
(Join in our eternal dance!)

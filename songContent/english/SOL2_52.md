# Bitter Love Journey

Slapping, scourging, 
purple robe, crowns of thorns!
The Lord bore the anguish alone.
On Calvary, He was nailed on the cross,
Suffered willingly for us all.
The Saviour poured out Himself,
His life, His love for me;
He was earnest to gain my heart,
Even died for me on the cross.

2. Huge tides of love 
shattered the wall of sin,
A guilt purging fountain opened.
"It is finished!" 
He declared on the cross,
Perfect salvation accomplished!
When I consider His heart,
I'm moved by this great love.
For His salvation grace and love,
Fore'er I'll love Him with my all.

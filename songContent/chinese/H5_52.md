# Glorious New Beginning in Nepal, South Asia

1 This is a great day in Nepal! (29-4-95)
He gives us a new glorious start.
I see the dawn breaking in Nepal;
O! let us rejoice, dance and sing!
Give ourselves to Him, and our hearts unite,
And build the dear church of His love,
And give the best gift to our Lord.

2 The night when we met in Patan, (5-4-95)
The moon was smiling in the sky;
I know the Lord is joyful and smiling,
For His will in Nepal will come to pass.
I thought of Patan from time to time,
Before I came from Hongkong to Nepal －
So we should have faith in our Lord.

3 He was glad when we went to Nepal; (4-4-95)
He is more zealous than we are.
He knew everything, He prepared the way;
He has been waiting for so long.
O! in His vineyard, let us give our love;
Fruits new and old may He enjoy.
O! let's rejoice with our Lover.

4 O! this is the hand of the Lord;
He is controlling everything.
We want to follow our Lord fully;
No matter what happens, we'll follow.
He will sustain us ; He will guide us;
He'll revive His work in Nepal;
He will be with us to the end.

# Carry Us Home 狂喜回天家

詞：余光昭　曲：edited from “spiritual”

One day from heaven He’ll come,
He’ll come to carry us home.
To the New Jerusalem,
He’ll come to carry us home!
In the clouds, His radiant chariot,
I’ll see His glorious face!
He’ll take us to His warm fairest home,
He’ll come to carry us home!
O, Lord Jesus, may You come soon come soon.
Come to carry us home.

一天從高天祂再來，
祂來接我們回家。
狂喜到新耶路撒冷，
祂來接我們回家！
那一天在輝煌美麗雲彩，
我們要見祂榮面！
祂領我們到祂至美溫暖家鄉，
祂來接我們回家！
主耶穌啊！我們願祢快來，
提接我們回天家。

英文：16-6-2009　睡中醒時　UBCP，溫哥華
中文：17-10-2010　作者自譯英文詩（Carry Us Home）　溫哥華

# Lord Jesus, I Love You 主耶穌我愛祢

詞：余光昭　曲：Dvorak（略改）
Lord Jesus, I loveYou!
Lord, You died for me!
You make me white as snow,
Your heart’s fair-est jewel.
You brought me from darkness
into Your marvelous light;
You have changed my life!
I’m forever Yours!
You’re forever mine!

主耶穌我愛祢！
祢曾為我死！
祢使我白如雪，
在祢心閃耀。（與祢永聯合！）
祢領我出黑暗
入奇妙光明，
祢改變我生命！
我永屬於祢！
祢永屬於我！

約2009-2010年　溫哥華

# 'Faith on' Darling

詞、曲：余光昭

1 Every victory and obedience
Is so precious to Him.
Every victory and obedience
He so appreciates.

2 Faith on, darling; faith on, darling;
I am gazing on you.
Faith on, darling; faith on, darling;
Your victory I long for.

3 Faith on, darling; faith on, darling;
I hold you with My hand.
Faith on, darling; faith on, darling;
I am looking on now.

4 Faith on, darling; faith on, darling;
You are spotless and pure.
Faith on, darling; faith on, darling;
You are so beautiful.

3-1-1995 about 4-5 a.m. Lamma Island

# The Infinite One Loves Me; I am His Love Dream

詞：余光昭  曲：Dvorak（略改）

Infinite One, You love me.
I am Your love dream!
Infinite One, I love You.
I love You most!
You and I, though so distant,
You have bridged the gap.
You raise me to be Your
Most compatible beloved.
Compatible beloved.

20-2-2018九龍水塘壩尾
